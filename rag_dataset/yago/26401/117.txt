Original U.S. One-Sheet Poster (27x41) Rolled Never Folded Fine Plus Condition

Universal PIctures Original U.S. One-sheet poster for the Alan Alda directed film SWEET LIBERTY (1986). The film also starred Michelle Pfeiffer and Michael Caine. The film was largely an Alan Alda project, and is somewhat drawn from his own early experience in the film business. Look for an appearance by Lillian Gish! This poster was used in a theater, but was never folded and remains in fine plus condition.
