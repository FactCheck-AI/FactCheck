About

Classic Hollywood film actress who appeared in movies such as Duck Soup, So This Is Africa, and The Bridge of San Luis Rey. She passed away from a heart attack at the age of 78.

Before Fame

She made her big screen debut in the 1928 silent film White Shadows in the South Seas.

Trivia

She was cast in the 1932 Broadway production of Adam Had Two Sons.

Family Life

She was married to fellow actor Jon Hall.
