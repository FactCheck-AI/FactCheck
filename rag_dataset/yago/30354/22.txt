Carlos Thompson was an actor who graced the silver screen many times throughout his Hollywood career. Thompson's career beginnings included film roles in the drama "Flame and the Flesh" (1954) with Lana Turner and "La Mujer de las Camelias" (1954). Later, Thompson acted in the Robert Taylor adventure "Valley of the Kings" (1954). Thompson was most recently credited in the Daniel Day-Lewis box office smash dramatic adaptation "Lincoln" (2012). Thompson was married to Lili Palmer. Thompson passed away in October 1990 at the age of 74.
