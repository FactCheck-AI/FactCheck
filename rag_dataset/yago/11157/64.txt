Ginger Elaine White and Carl Justin Dickinson, both of Denver, Colo., are engaged to be married. The wedding is planned for October 7, 2006, on the Mayan Riviera in Mexico at the Moon Palace Resort.

Miss White is the daughter of Jimmy and Judy White of Wilmington, N.C. Mr. Dickinson is the son of Natalie Carter of Godfrey, Illinois, and Jim and Vicki Dickinson of Kansas City, Mo.
