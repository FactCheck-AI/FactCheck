Jennifer Dickinson of Greenville, S.C., and Andrew Pruitt of Clemson, S.C., were married Saturday at Foothills Equestrian Nature Center in Tryon, N.C.

The bride is the daughter of Judy Dickinson of Lakeland and Thomas Dickinson of York, S.C. The bridegroom is the son of Robert and Gail Pruitt of Landrum, S.C.

Mrs. Pruitt graduated from Clemson University and is employed by Fabric Editions, Ltd. in Greenville. Pruitt graduated from Tri-County Technical College and is employed by Resurgent Capital Services in Greenville.

After a wedding trip to the Mayan Riviera, the couple will reside in Greenville.
