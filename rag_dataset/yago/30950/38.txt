Catch-22, Joseph Heller. First Edition, Second Printing. Published in 1961 by Simon and Schuster, New York. The first edition, first printing had a run of 1,000. The second printing had a run of 2,500. Original dust jacket, short tears at edges and lower spine. Signed and inscribed by Heller to Olive Plunkett. In original dust jacket.

Inscribed:

To Olive Plunkett –

With Warmest regards from Jackie Meben, and with the sincere hope that this book will give you at least a little pleasure.

Joseph Heller

December 15, 1961
