File photo - Prince Joachim of Denmark and Her Royal Highness Marie Cavallier wedding in Mogeltonder's church, Southern Jutland, Denmark on May 24, 2008. This is a re-wedding for Prince Joachim (younger son of Queen Margrethe II and Prince Henri de Montpezat), 39, who married Alexandra Manley on 18 November 1995 and divorced on 8 April 2005. Princess Marie Cavallier, 32, is a French citizen and belongs to the Roman Catholic Church. Prince Joachim of Denmark is in stable condition after undergoing brain surgery on Friday evening. The 51-year-old royal was admitted to Toulouse University Hospital in France, where he was immediately taken into surgery for a blood clot in his brain, the Royal House announced in a statement.
