Ali Wentworth (b. 1965) is an American actress and comedian, who is married to Good Morning America co-host George Stephanopoulos.

Wentworth, along with Stephanopoulos, co-hosted the 2013 Sesame Workshop Benefit Gala.

Among her credits are being a cast member on In Living Color from 1992-1994, and appearances on Seinfeld, Law & Order: Special Victims Units and Felicity.
