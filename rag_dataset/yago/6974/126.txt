Actress

Married to: Francis Greco

On: 03/10/2001

For her March 2001 wedding to investment banker Francis Greco in Toronto, Lauren Holly went all-out glam in a sheer, beaded, bell-sleeved dress by Escada with fur-trimmed coat. The ?Dumb and Dumber? star, who was previously married to Jim Carrey and actor Danny Quinn, carried a bouquet of lilac-accented roses and accessorized with a 37-carat diamond Harry Winston cross necklace.
