Abhishek Bachchan and Aishwarya Rai's divorce rumours rife once again during Anant Ambani and Radhika Merchant Wedding: Bollywood diva Aishwarya Rai and her husband Abhishek Bachchan have been in the headlines for a long time now.

Now, once again the duo is a part of gossip around the corner and the rumours related to their divorce have started to rife once again.

During Anant Ambani and Radhika Merchant's wedding both Aishwarya Rai and her husband Abhishek Bachchan made their appearances. The only thing that led to the speculation of their divorce was that they both were seen as separate.

Yes, Abhishek Bachchan was seen with his family, including Sir Amitabh Bachchan and his wife Jaya Bachchan while Aishwarya Rai was seen entering with her daughter Aaradhya Bachchan.

This gave spark to the rumors that recently settled down. A few months ago similar speculation was making rounds on social media. However, the couple had put an end to the speculation, back then, with a post on their 17th Anniversary.
