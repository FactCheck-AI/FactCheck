He is survived by his wife of 36 years Julie P. Franklin, daughters Priscilla Ann Franklin-Payan and Elizabeth Marie Franklin, grandchildren who were his world, Deryck and Noah Payan and Maddox James Espinoza. Followed by his other children Monica Franklin Payan, Corina Franklin-Shakleford, Jessica Franklin- Svan, John D Franklin and James A Franklin, included are 5 grandchildren and 2 great grandchildren.

He is also survived by his siblings Kay Franklin Llanes, Patricia Miranda, and William Franklin with extended Family.
