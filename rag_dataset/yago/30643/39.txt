AMMAN — In the presence of Their Majesties King Abdullah and Queen Rania, HRH Princess Iman Bint Abdullah II married Mr Jameel Alexander Thermiotis on Sunday.

Their Royal Highnesses Prince El Hassan Bin Talal, Crown Prince Al Hussein Bin Abdullah II, Prince Hashem Bin Abdullah II, Prince Feisal Bin Al Hussein, Prince Ali Bin Al Hussein, Prince Hashim Bin Al Hussein, Prince Talal Bin Muhammad, Prince Ghazi Bin Muhammad, and Prince Rashid Bin El Hassan attended the wedding, held at Beit Al Urdun, Their Majesties’ private residence, according to a Royal Court statement.

Their Royal Highnesses Princess Muna Al Hussein, Princess Basma Bint Talal, Princess Salma Bint Abdullah II, Princess Alia Bint Al Hussein, Princess Aisha Bint Al Hussein, Princess Zein Bint Al Hussein, Princess Raiyah Bint Al Hussein, Princess Sarvath El Hassan, Princess Rahma Bint El Hassan, Princess Sumaya Bint El Hassan, Princess Zeina Al Feisal, Princess Rym Ali, Princess Ghida Talal, Princess Miriam Ghazi, Princess Zeina Rashid, Princess Taghrid Muhammad, and Her Highness Princess Firyal were also in attendance.

Royal family members, as well as members of the Thermiotis family and a number of invited guests attended the wedding.

The Royal Hashemite Court congratulated Their Majesties King Abdullah and Queen Rania on this occasion, and extended its warmest wishes of happiness and prosperity to HRH Princess Iman and Mr Thermiotis.
