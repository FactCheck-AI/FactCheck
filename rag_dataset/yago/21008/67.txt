Kevin & Perry Go Large follows two hormonally challenged lads on their quest to lose their virginity and become the world's top DJ mixmasters. It's the summer holidays and Kevin (Harry Enfield) and Perry (Kathy Burke) know there is only one place where the DJ reigns supreme and girls are gagging for it - Ibiza! Armed with a suitcase full of condoms and some wicked gear the boys head for the promised land of sun, sea and big shags. On arrival the boys spy the girls of their dreams - Candice and Gemma - as well as their idol - superstar DJ genius Eye Ball Paul (Rhys Ifans). Will this be the best summer ever? Will the boys get their new track "Big Girl" played in the clubs? Will they finally get to do it?
