Ibrahim Yar'adua, the son of late President Umaru Musa Yar'adua, has married a second wife.

He married Amira, the daughter of former Comptroller General of Nigeria Immigration Service (NIS) Muhammad Babandede.

The wedding fatiha was held on Friday, October 20, 2023.

Ibrahim married his first wife, Saratu Sodangi, a lawyer, in 2018.
