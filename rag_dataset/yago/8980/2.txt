NEW MADRID -- Ray Charlie Barnett Sr., 40, died Feb. 7, 2006, at his home near Henderson Mound in New Madrid County.

Born May 11, 1965, in Aberdeen, Miss., he was the son of Joyce Sue Sissom of Henderson, Tenn., and the late Charlie Jasper Barnett.

On Aug. 1, 1987, in Sikeston, he married Mary Rebecca Barnett, who survives of the home.

Other survivors include: two sons, Christian Micheal Barnett, who is serving in the military in Iraq, and Charlie Ray Barnett Jr. of the home; two daughters, Lena Marie Barnett and Paul Sue Barnett of the home; one sister, Lois Lena Stacks of Silerton, Tenn.; two brothers, William Edward Barnett of Blodgett and Marvin Roy Brown Jr. of Henderson; and two grandchildren, Jayden Alexander Gravil-Barnett of Sikeston and Taylor Jo Rebecca Barnett of McGee.

Two sisters preceded him in death.

Visitation is from 5 to 8 p.m. Sunday at Richards Funeral Home Inc. in New Madrid, where services will be conducted at 11 a.m. Monday.

No burial will be held.
