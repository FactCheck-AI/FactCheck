Synopsis:

Erotic drama based on the autobiography of Italian stage actress Piera Degli Esposti. The story deals with the incestuous relationship between the open-minded mother and daughter, Piera, who share men they have sexual relations with. Piera’s parents eventually end up in mental institutions, but remain sexual "eccentrics".
