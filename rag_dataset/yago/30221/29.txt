Maria de las Mercedes of Orleans (24 June 1860 - 26 June 1878) was Queen of Spain as the first wife of King Alfonso XII. She was born in Madrid, the daughter of Antoine of Orleans, Duke of Montpensier, and of Infanta Luisa Fernanda of Spain.
