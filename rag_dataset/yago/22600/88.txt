The Wedding Party of Princess Margaret of Connaught and Crown Prince Gustav VI Adolf of Sweden, 15 June 1905

Standing, left to right: Princess Victoria Eugenie of Battenberg, Princess Beatrice of Edinburgh, the groom Crown Prince Gustav VI Adolf, and sister of the bride Princess Patricia of Connaught. In front: the flower girl Princess Mary, who later became Viscountess Lascelles and the bride Princess Margaret of Connaught.

Gustaf VI Adolf was King of Sweden from 1950 until his death in 1973.

Princess Margaret was the elder daughter of Prince Arthur, Duke of Connaught, and Princess Louise Margaret of Prussia.

The couple had five children but Margaret died suddenly in 1920. The official cause of death was infection following an operation. At the time she was expecting their sixth child.
