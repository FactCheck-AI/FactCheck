Grand Duchess Anastasia Mikhailovna of Russia (28 July 1860 – 11 March 1922) was by birth member of the House of Romanov and a Grand Duchess of Russia and Grand Duchess of Mecklenburg-Schwerin by marriage.

Marriage[edit]

On 24 January 1879, she married Friedrich Franz, Hereditary Grand Duke of Mecklenburg-Schwerin

Issue[edit]

Princess Alexandrine of Mecklenburg-Schwerin (24 December 1879 – 28 December 1952) married King Christian X of Denmark and had issue.

Frederick Francis IV, Grand Duke of Mecklenburg-Schwerin (9 April 1882 – 17 November 1945) married Princess Alexandra of Hanover and had issue.

Princess Cecilie of Mecklenburg-Schwerin (20 September 1886 – 6 May 1954) married Wilhelm, German Crown Prince and had issue.

Titles and styles[edit]

28 July 1860 – 24 January 1879 Her Imperial Highness Grand Duchess Anastasia Mikhailovna of Russia.

24 January 1879 – 15 April 1883 Her Imperial and Royal Highness The Hereditary Grand Duchess of Mecklenburg-Schwerin.

15 April 1883 – 10 April 1897 Her Imperial and Royal Highness The Grand Duchess of Mecklenburg-Schwerin.

10 April 1897 – 11 March 1922 Her Imperial and Royal Highness The Dowager Grand Duchess of Mecklenburg-Schwerin.
