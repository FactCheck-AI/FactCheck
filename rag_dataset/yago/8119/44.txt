Carolina Maria Annunziata Bonaparte Murat Macdonald, 1782-1839. Caroline Bonaparte, Imperial French princess, younger sister of Emperor Napoleon I of France. In 1800, Caroline married Joachim Murat, Marshal of the Empire, Prince Murat and later King of Naples. Steel engraving by H.B. Hall after a portrait by Jules Champagne from Frank B. Goodrich’s The Court of Napoleon or Society under the First Empire, J. B. Lippincott, Philadelphia, 1875.
