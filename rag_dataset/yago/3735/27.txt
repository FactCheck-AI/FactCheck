Born: 1927

Computer Science Contributions:

John McCarthy is most known as the founder of AI, however he also founded the Lisp programming language, and co-founded timesharing. Timesharing allows multiple users to use one large computer at one time. He also started the Stanford AI Laboratory which is a major name in AI development. His impactful life ended on October 24, 2011.
