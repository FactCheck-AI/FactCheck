The Pony Express is a 1925 American silent Western film, The film was directed by James Cruze and starred his wife, Betty Compson, along with Ricardo Cortez, Wallace Beery, and George Bancroft.

Cast

Betty Compson as Molly Jones

Ricardo Cortez as Jack Weston

Ernest Torrence as Ascension Jones

Wallace Beery as Rhode Island Red

George Bancroft as Jack Slade

Frank Lackteen as Charlie Bent

Johnny Fox as Billy Cody (credited as John Fox Jr.)

William H. Turner as William Russell (credited as William Turner)

Al Hart as Senator Glen

Charles Gerson as Sam Clemens

Rose Tapley as Aunt

Duke Kahanamoku as Indian Chief
