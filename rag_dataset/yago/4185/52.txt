Jan. 1, 1970 - Paris, France - One of the most important figures of cinema as an art ABEL GANCE revolutionized film technology with a masterful mix of real-time editing and rapid cutting, a technique he invented. The Father of Surrealism, Gance earned international recognition in 1919 for his three hour epic J'Accuse, a powerful anti-war film which included location filming of battles.
