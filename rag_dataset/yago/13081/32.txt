Actresses Claudia Scarpatetti (L) and Claudia Hiersche pictured during the shooting of German TV soap 'Verbotene Liebe' (Forbidden Love) in Engelskirchen, Germany, 24 June 2006. Scarpatetti as Carla von Lahnstein and Hiersche as Susanne Brandner have the first lesbian civil marriage in German TV to be witnessed in a double-feature episode on 15 September on ARD.
