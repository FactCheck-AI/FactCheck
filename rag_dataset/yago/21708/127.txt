Biography of Victor Amadeus III King Sardinia 1726-1796

In 1726 Victor Amadeus III King Sardinia was born to Charles Emmanuel III King Sardinia (age 24) and Polyxena Hesse Rotenburg Queen Consort Sardinia (age 19).

Before 1723 [his father] Charles Emmanuel III King Sardinia (age 21) and Anne Christine Wittelsbach (age 18) were married. He a great grandson of King Charles I of England, Scotland and Ireland 1600-1649. He the son of Victor Amadeus King Sardinia (age 56) and Anne Marie Bourbon Queen Consort Sardinia (age 53).

On 23 Jul 1724 [his father] Charles Emmanuel III King Sardinia (age 23) and [his mother] Polyxena Hesse Rotenburg Queen Consort Sardinia (age 17) were married. He a great grandson of King Charles I of England, Scotland and Ireland 1600-1649. She by marriage Queen Consort Sardinia. He the son of Victor Amadeus King Sardinia (age 58) and Anne Marie Bourbon Queen Consort Sardinia (age 54).

On 31 Oct 1732 [his grandfather] Victor Amadeus King Sardinia (age 66) died. His son [his father] Charles Emmanuel III King Sardinia (age 31) succeeded Duke Savoy, III King Sardinia. On 31 Oct 1732 His grandson Victor Amadeus III King Sardinia (age 6) succeeded III King Sardinia.

On 13 Jan 1735 [his mother] Polyxena Hesse Rotenburg Queen Consort Sardinia (age 28) died.

On 05 Mar 1737 [his father] Charles Emmanuel III King Sardinia (age 35) and [his step-mother] Elisabeth Therese Lorraine Queen Consort Sardinia (age 25) were married. They were half first cousins. He a great grandson of King Charles I of England, Scotland and Ireland 1600-1649. She a great x 3 granddaughter of King James I of England and Ireland and VI of Scotland 1566-1625. She by marriage Queen Consort Sardinia. She the daughter of Leopold Duke of Lorraine 1679-1729 and Élisabeth Charlotte Bourbon Duchess Lorraine (age 60). He the son of Victor Amadeus King Sardinia and Anne Marie Bourbon Queen Consort Sardinia.

On 25 Oct 1739 [his future brother-in-law] Infante Philip of Spain (age 19) and Louise Elisabeth of France (age 12) were married. They were first cousin once removed. She a great x 3 granddaughter of King Charles I of England, Scotland and Ireland 1600-1649. She the daughter of Louis XV King France (age 29) and Marie Leszczyńska Queen Consort France (age 36). He the son of Philippe V King Spain (age 55) and Elisabeth Farnese Queen Consort Spain (age 47).

On 31 May 1750 Victor Amadeus III King Sardinia (age 24) and Infanta Maria Antonia Spain (age 20) were married. They were third cousins. He a great x 2 grandson of King Charles I of England, Scotland and Ireland 1600-1649. She the daughter of Philippe V King Spain and Elisabeth Farnese Queen Consort Spain (age 57). He the son of Charles Emmanuel III King Sardinia (age 49) and Polyxena Hesse Rotenburg Queen Consort Sardinia.

On 02 Sep 1753 [his daughter] Maria Joséphine of Savoy was born to Victor Amadeus III King Sardinia (age 27) and [his wife] Infanta Maria Antonia Spain (age 23).

On 31 Jan 1756 [his daughter] Maria Teresa of Savoy was born to Victor Amadeus III King Sardinia (age 30) and [his wife] Infanta Maria Antonia Spain (age 26).

On 17 Dec 1757 [his daughter] Maria Anna Savoy Duchess Chablais was born to Victor Amadeus III King Sardinia (age 31) and [his wife] Infanta Maria Antonia Spain (age 28).

On 18 Jul 1765 [his brother-in-law] Infante Philip of Spain (age 45) died.

In 1771 [his son-in-law] King Charles X of France 1757-1836 (age 13) and [his daughter] Maria Teresa of Savoy (age 14) were married. They were second cousin once removed. He a great x 4 grandson of King Charles I of England, Scotland and Ireland 1600-1649. She a great x 3 granddaughter of King Charles I of England, Scotland and Ireland 1600-1649. She the daughter of Victor Amadeus III King Sardinia 1726-1796 (age 45) and Infanta Maria Antonia Spain (age 41).

On 20 Feb 1773 [his father] Charles Emmanuel III King Sardinia (age 71) died at Turin.

On 07 Aug 1785 [his brother-in-law] Infante Louis of Spain (age 58) died.

On 19 Sep 1785 [his wife] Infanta Maria Antonia Spain (age 55) died.

In 1796 Victor Amadeus III King Sardinia (age 70) died.
