New Delhi: Bigg Boss 8 contestant Dimpy Ganguly, former wife of Rahul Mahajan, has announced that she is engaged. (Also Read: Rahul Mahajan and Dimpy Ganguly Divorced, Now 'Just Friends')

Dimpy, 30, shared the happy news with her fans in a Twitter post on Tuesday. The actress and her fiance, Dubai-based businessman Rohit Roy, have reportedly been seeing each other since a while now.

Rahul met Dimpy Ganguly on reality show Rahul Dulhania Le Jayega and the couple married in 2010. Some reality TV appearances later, they separated with Dimpy accusing Rahul of domestic abuse.
