Dimpay mahajian (bengali: july 25, 1985; born soumasri gangulay) is a model and actress. by her marriage on reality television (for the program rahul dulhaniya le jayega), she and her husband rahul mahajan created history by being the first couple to marry on indian reality television for a national audience (the previous version of the program did not culminate in a marriage).
