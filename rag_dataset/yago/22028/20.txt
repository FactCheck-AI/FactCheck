Prince Gustav of Vasa, Count Itterburg 9 November 1799 – 4 August/5 August 1877) born Crown Prince of Sweden, was the son of King Gustav IV Adolf of Sweden and Queen Frederica. His Austrian princely title (from 1829) was actually spelled Wasa.

Marriage[edit]

On 9 November 1830, he married in his first cousin Princess Louise Amelie of Baden.

Issue[edit]

Prince Louis of Vasa (3 March 1832 - 7 March 1832) died in infabncy.

Princess Carola of Vasa (5 August 1833 – 15 December 1907) married Crown Prince Albert of Saxony but had no surviving issue.
