Oil on canvas “Portrait of Marie Amélie of Saxony”, queen of Naples and Sicily, capped at the Fontange, canvas rentoilée, wooden frame carved and gilded original.

Maria Amalia Christina Franziska his sister Marie-Josèphe married the dauphin of France Louis in 1747, another, Marie-Anne, was elector of Bavaria. In 1738, she married Charles of Spain (1716-1788), king of Naples and Sicily, younger son of King Philip V of Spain. Marie-Amélie exerts a great influence on her husband.

18th century

Italy
