Ronald Reagan, Jane Wyman (subjects)

N.p. N.p., 1945. Vintage candid photograph of actors Ronald Reagan and Jane Wyman, 1945. With the stamp of United Press International to the verso.

After starring together in "Brother Rat" (1938) and its sequel "Brother Rat and a Baby" (1940), Reagan and Wyman were married in 1940. The couple divorced in 1949, a decision purportedly attributable to Wyman's difficulty with marriage to a Democrat, which Reagan still was at the time.

10 x 8 inches. Near Fine.
