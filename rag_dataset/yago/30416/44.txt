Biography

Lord Guilford Dudley was born circa 1536 in Ely Place, London, Middlesex, England. His parents were John Dudley, 1st Duke of Northumberland, 1st Earl of Warwick and Jane Guildford, Duchess of Northumberland.

Guildford married Lady Jane Grey, Queen of England (disputed) on May 21, 1553 in Durham House,London,England.

He died on February 12, 1554 in Tower Hill, Tower of London, London, Tower Liberty, England from Decapitation and was buried on February 12, 1554 in Chapel of Saint Peter-ad-Vincula, Tower of London, London, Tower Liberty, England.
