Summary :2003 French feature film, directed by Jacques Doillon. Focuses on the relationship between a rich white man, Fred (Pascal Greggory) and a poor Arab girl, nineteen-year-old Raja (Najat Benssallem). Raja is an orphan who is scarred by life and Fred is an emotionally-bankrupt Frenchman who is transfixed by Raja's fragility and beauty. In French and Arabic, with English subtitles. Offair, SBS, 110min. DVD
