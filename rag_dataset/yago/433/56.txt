Russell Crowe and his wife Danielle Spencer have reportedly separated after nine years of marriage, according to Us Weekly.

The couple met twenty-two years ago and married back in 2003 on his farm in Australia. They have two children together: Charles, 8, and Tennyson, 6.

According to various reports, the split was amicable, and Danielle is currently living in Australia with their children, while Russell is busy filming Noah in New York.
