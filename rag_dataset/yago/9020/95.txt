Anna Faris and Dan Fogler have joined Kids in America, the Topher Grace vehicle coming from Universal Pictures and Imagine Entertainment. The film is a comedy that will be directed by Michael Dowse and produced by Gordon Kaywin and Grace himself.

According to The Hollywood Reporter, the former That '70s Show star plays a recent college student who gets involved at a "raucous Labor Day party in 1988." Faris (Scary Movie) plays his twin sister and Fogler (School for Scoundrels) will appear as his best friend. Teresa Palmer (The Grudge 2) will play the object of Grace's affections.

That '70s Show vets Jeff and Jackie Filgo scripted the film, based on a story by helmer Dowse. Production begins next week on the project.
