Birth Name: Peter Sean Sollett

Place of Birth: Bensonhurst, Brooklyn, New York City, New York, U.S.

Date of Birth: January 9, 1976

Ethnicity: Ashkenazi Jewish

Peter Sollett is an American film director and screenwriter. He has directed the films Raising Victor Vargas, Nick & Norah’s Infinite Playlist, Freeheld, and Metal Lords.

Peter is the son of Helene and Rob Sollett. He is married to Spanish screenwriter, director, and producer Eva Vives, with whom he has a son. Peter and Eva co-wrote Raising Victor Vargas.

Peter’s maternal grandfather was named Steven Ian “Steve” Kandler.
