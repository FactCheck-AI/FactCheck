Carte de visite photographs from an album including the Austrian Royal family and relatives together with various members of the Austrian Imperial court.

c 1862 - 1865.

Herzog Karl-Theodor in Bayern (1839-1909)

(Karl-Theodor Wittelsbach in Bayern)

Ophthalmologist.

Son of Maximilian Joseph, Duke in Bavaria and and Princess Ludovika of Bavaria.

In 1865 he married Princess Sophie of Saxony after who's death he married in 1874 Infanta Maria Josepha of Portugal.

Cdv by unknown photographer.
