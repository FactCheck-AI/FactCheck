Eric Abelsson, Herzog von Schleswig

M, #105491, d. 1272

Last Edited=17 Jun 2003

Consanguinity Index=0.0%

Eric Abelsson, Herzog von Schleswig was the son of Abel Valdemarsson, King of Denmark and Matilda von Schauenberg. He married Margaret von Rügen, daughter of Jaromar Herzog von Rügen.1 He died in 1272.1

He gained the title of Herzog von Schleswig.

Margaret von Pommern

F, #105495, b. 1230, d. 1282

Last Edited=2 Jul 2022

Margaret von Pommern was born in 1230. She was the daughter of Sambar von Kassuben, Duke of Kassuben.1 She married Christopher I Valdemarsson, King of Denmark, son of Valdemar II 'the Victorious' Valdemarsson, King of Denmark and Berengaria de Portugal, in 1248. She died in 1282.1

She was also known as Swinislawa.

Erik V 'Klipping' Christofsson, King of Denmark was born in 1249. He was the son of Christopher I Valdemarsson, King of Denmark and Margaret von Pommern.1 He married Agnes von Brandenburg, daughter of Johann I Markgraf von Brandenburg and Sophia Valdemarsdottir, on 11 November 1273. He died on 22 November 1286 murdered.3

He succeeded as the King Erik V of Denmark in 1259.3

Mathilde Christofsdottir, Princess of Denmark

F, #105499, b. 1250, d. 1300

Last Edited=10 May 2003

Mathilde Christofsdottir, Princess of Denmark was born in 1250.1 She was the daughter of Christopher I Valdemarsson, King of Denmark and Margaret von Pommern. She married Albrecht III Markgraf von Brandenburg, son of Otto III Markgraf von Brandenburg and Beatrice of Bohemia, between 1269 and 1272.1 She died in 1300.1

She gained the title of Princess Mathilde of Denmark.
