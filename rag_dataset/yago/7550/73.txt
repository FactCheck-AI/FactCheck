Laura Ingalls Wilder’s mother, Caroline Lake Quiner was born on December 12, 1839, in Brookfield, Wisconsin

She was born in the family’s log cabin and is the earliest known settler born in the town of Brookfield

She married Charles Ingalls on February 1, 1860, who later died in 1902

She died April 20, 1924 in De Smet, South Dakota at the age of 84, on Easter Sunday

Her parents were Henry Quiner and Charlotte Quiner

Her mother later remarried Frederick Holbrook in 1849

Her children were Mary Ingalls(1865-1928), Laura Ingalls Wilder(1867-1957), Caroline Ingalls(1870-1946), Charles Ingalls(1875-1876), and Grace Ingalls(1877-1941)
