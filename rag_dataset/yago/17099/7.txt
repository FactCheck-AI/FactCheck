The poster presents movies played on the 50th anniversary of the KMP, The Hungarian Party of Communists. It displays the following movies: The Red and the White directed by iklós Jancsó, Sweet Anna directed by Zoltán Fábri, Boys in the Street directed by Péter Szász, From Noon till Dawn directed by Tamás Rényi, Silence and Cry directed by Miklós Jancsó, Guns and Doves directed by Márton Keleti, A Few Steps to the Frontier directed by Márton Keleti.
