Canadian born artist, animator and filmmaker. Munroe has been credited on such television shows as Nickelodeon's "Hey Arnold!", to writing and directing the "TMNT" (2007) film, to directing, the critically and commercially panned live action adaptation of Dylan Dog: Dead of Night.

Munroe was the lead artist, writer and director of the CGI cinematics on Midway's Freaky Flyers; a cartoony air vehicular racing game.
