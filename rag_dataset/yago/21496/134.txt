This film was originally selected as an AFI Movie Club pick on March 30, 2021.

Released in June 1978, HEAVEN CAN WAIT grossed over $12 million in its first 12 days. This film was co-written, co-directed and produced by AFI Life Achievement Award recipient Warren Beatty.
