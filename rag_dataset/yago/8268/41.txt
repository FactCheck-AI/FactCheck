Hotel Rodney is a boutique hotel located in the heart of historic downtown Lewes Delaware. Built in 1926 on the corner of Second and Market Streets, Hotel Rodney is a central part of the town’s history and charm. Shops, restaurants, spas and antiques are right outside our doors. Headed to the sun? The Delaware Bay beaches are just down the road, Rehoboth Beach is a ten minutes' drive and Cape May is a ferry ride away. Lewes is also home to some of the best outlet shopping on the East Coast, and it’s tax-free. Come for a romantic getaway or a family vacation. And if you are planning an event, we can host your wedding, business meeting, reception or party. Our hotel and beautiful lobby are smoke-free. Come visit Lewes, experience the beauty of Delaware and the charm of Hotel Rodney.
