1999 | Unit Production Manager -- As unit production manager, Gina enjoyed working on this teenage coming out story. He Bop!!, a light-hearted drama earned many awards during it's festival run. He Bop!, was both written and directed by AFI's 1999 newcomer, Brian Dannelly. This remarkable drama set in the fun days of the 1980's "disco era"! Brian has since written and directed his hit feature film entitled Saved! Principal cast includes: Steve Herd, Lenore Thomas, Ben Alexander and Mink Stole.
