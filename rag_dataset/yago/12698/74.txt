Vista Energy Argentina SAU (formerly known as Vista Oil & Gas SAU, and before that "PELSA") is an Argentine company engaged in the exploration and production of hydrocarbons and the marketing of oil, natural gas and natural gas liquids (" NGL”), with offices in Buenos Aires and Neuquén and a field office with technical staff located in the Entre Lomas concession.

It currently owns (i) 100% of the operated exploitation rights of the Entre Lomas Neuquén, Entre Lomas Río Negro, Bajada del Palo Oeste and Bajada del Palo Este concessions.
