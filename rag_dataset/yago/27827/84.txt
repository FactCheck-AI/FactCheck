Princess Ida Caroline of Saxe-Meiningen (25 June 1794 – 4 April 1852) was a German princess, a member of the House of Wettin, and by marriage Princess of Saxe-Weimar-Eisenach by marriage. Adelaide (who became Queen consort of the United Kingdom and of Hanover as wife of King William IV of the United Kingdom

Marriage[edit]

On 30 May 1816 she married Prince Bernhard of Saxe-Weimar-Eisenach.

Issue[edit]

Princess Louise Wilhelmine Adelaide of Saxe-Weimar-Eisenach (31 March 1817 – 11 July 1832) died unmarried and without issue.

Prince Wilhelm Karl of Saxe-Weimar-Eisenach (25 June 1819 – 22 May 1839) died unmarried and without issue.

Princess Amalie Auguste Cecilia of Saxe-Weimar-Eisenach (30 May 1822 – 16 June 1822) died in infancy.

Prince Edward of Saxe-Weimar-Eisenach (11 October 1823 – 16 November 1902) married Lady Augusta Gordon-Lennox but had no issue.

Prince Hermann Bernhard Georg (4 August 1825 – 31 August 1901) married Princess Auguste of Württemberg and had issue.

Prince Friedrich Gustav of Saxe-Weimar-Eisenach (28 June 1827 – 6 January 1892) married Pierina Marocchia but had no issue.

Princess Anna Amalie Maria of Saxe-Weimar-Eisenach (9 September 1828 {{snd} 14 July 1864).

Princess Amalie of Saxe-Weimar-Eisenach (20 May 1830 – 1 May 1872) married Prince Henry of the Netherlands. No issue.
