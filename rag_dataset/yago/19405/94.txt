Blessing Douglas, former wife to the Olusoji Jacobs, has revealed that her three years marriage to the don of Nigerian Veteran thespians, Olu Jacobs and Joke Silva, is over.

She disclosed this in a now deleted post which was earlier posted on her Instagram page, answering a fan who asked, “Are you still married? my bro who is in the UK is madly in love with you.”

She responded, “No. He will have to register for interview o.”

The mother of one further confirmed this again when another follower asked, “Are you still married to your husband?”

She replied, “No. Oya make una rest.”

Recall that the former couple tied the knot in 2018, and welcomed their first and only child, Atarah Jacobs.
