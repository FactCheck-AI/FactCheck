Prince Luitpold o Bavarie, Prince Regent of Bavaria (Luitpold Karl Joseph Wilhelm Ludwig; 12 Mairch 1821 – 12 December 1912), was de facto ruler o Bavarie from 1886 till 1912, due to the incapacity of his nephews, King Ludwig II for three days and King Otto for 26 years.

Issue[edit]

Ludwig III of Bavaria (7 January 1845 – 18 October 1921) married Archduchess Maria Theresa of Austria-Este and had issue.

Prince Leopold of Bavaria (9 February 1846 – 28 September 1930) married Archduchess Gisela of Austria and had issue.

Princess Therese of Bavaria (12 November 1850 – 19 December 1925) never married.

Prince Arnulf of Bavaria (6 July 1852 – 12 November 1907) married Princess Therese of Liechtenstein and had issue.

Titles an styles[edit]

12 March 1821 - 10 June 1886 His Royal Highness Prince Luitpold of Bavaria.

10 June 1886 – 12 December 1912 His Royal Highness The Prince Regent of Bavaria.
