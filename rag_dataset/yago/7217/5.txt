Leopold I (; 16 December 1790 – 10 December 1865) was the first King of the Belgians, reigning from 21 July 1831 until his death in 1865.

Issue with Louise of Orléans[edit]

Louis Philippe, Crown Prince of Belgium (24 July 1833 – 16 May 1834) died young.

King Leopold II (9 April 1835 – 17 December 1909) married Archduchess Marie Henriette of Austria and had issue.

Prince Philippe, Count of Flanders (24 March 1837 – 17 November 1905) married Princess Marie of Hohenzollern-Sigmaringen and had issue.

Princess Charlotte o Belgium (7 June 1840 – 19 January 1927) married Archduke Maximilian of Austria but had no issue.
