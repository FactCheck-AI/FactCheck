The funeral of Prince Mikasa of Japan has taken place today at the Toshimagaoka Imperial Cemetery in Tokyo. Crown Prince Naruhito and his wife Crown Princess Masako, Prince Akishino and his wife Princess Kiko and their daughters Princess Mako and Princess Kako, Princess Yuriko, wife of the late Prince Mikasa attended the Shinto funeral service for Prince Mikasa at the Toshimagaoka Cemetery on November 4, 2016 in Tokyo. Princess Mikasa died aged 100.
