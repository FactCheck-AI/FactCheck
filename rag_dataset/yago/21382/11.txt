Liza Minnelli”s ex-husband, David Gest, was found dead Tuesday at a London hotel.

Police reportedly told TMZ that 62-year-old Gest was found at the Four Seasons Hotel at the Canary Wharf.

“Police were called at 10:17 on Tuesday to the Four Seasons Hotel, to reports of an unexplained death of a man in his 60s. London ambulance service were called to the scene,” police reportedly said.

At this point, the cause seems to be “non-suspicious,” authorities told the website, An autopsy has been scheduled.

Gest was married to the Oscar-winner from 2002 to 2007. The film and television producer accused Minnelli of abusing him during their marriage. He appeared on UK”s “Celebrity Big Brother” earlier this year, but had to leave early for medical reasons.
