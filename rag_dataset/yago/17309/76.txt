Nancy Juvonen is an American film producer. She and Drew Barrymore own the production company Flower Films.

Among the films she has produced are Never Been Kissed (1999), Charlie's Angels (2000), Donnie Darko (2001), 50 First Dates (2004), Fever Pitch (2005), He's Just Not That into You (2009), and How to Be Single (2016).

She is married to talk-show host Jimmy Fallon.
