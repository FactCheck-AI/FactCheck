Margaret of Anjou, Queen Consort of Henry VI, (1792). Margaret of Anjou (1429-1482) married Henry VI of England in 1445. The mental instability of Henry meant that Margaret effectively led the Lancastrian cause in the Wars of the Roses. She founded Queens College, Cambridge.
