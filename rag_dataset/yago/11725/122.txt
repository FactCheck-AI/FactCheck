The Last Unicorn and the fairytales of the natural world!

Beagle’s The Last Unicorn, directed by Jules Bass and Arthur Rankin, Jr. We discuss the film’s major themes, including the underlying meaning of its emotional narrative, the nature of good and evil and bravery, and much more!
