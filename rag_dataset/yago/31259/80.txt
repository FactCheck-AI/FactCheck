Francis I (III) of Lorraine (1708–1765) Holy Roman Emperor from 1745 and Grand Duke of Tuscany from 1737 Duke of Lorraine from 1729 to 1737 (Titular thereafter). Married Maria Theresa of Austria (1717–1780) Archduchess of Austria, Oueen of Hungary, Croatia, Bohemia, Grand duchess of Transylvania, Duchess of Mantua, Duchess of Milan, Queen of Galicia and Lodomeria, Duchess of the Austrian Netherlands, and Duchess of Parma. By marriage, Duchess consort of Lorraine, Grand Duchess consort of Tuscany and Empress consort of Holy Roman Empire.

Francis I (1768 – 1835) Emperor of Austria

Francis II & I (1768 – 1835) the last Holy Roman Emperor from 1792 until 1806 and the first Emperor of Austria as Francis I from 1804. King of Hungary and Bohemia, Archduke of Austria from 1792, King of Lombardy-Venetia from 1815, Duke of Milan from 1792 to 1796, Duke of Brabant, Limburg and Luxembourg, Count of Flanders, Hainaut and Namur from 1792 to 1793. Founded The Austrian Imperial Order of Leopold in 1808. Founded The Austrian Imperial Order of the Iron Crown in 1815. Married firstly Elisabeth of Württemberg (1767–1790) daughter of Frederick II Eugene, Duke of Württemberg, secondly Maria Theresa of Naples and Sicily (1772–1807) daughter of Ferdinand IV & III King of Naples and Sicily, 7th Grand Mistress of the Order of the Starry Cross 1792-1807, thirdly Maria Ludovika of Austria-Este (1787–1816) daughter of Archduke Ferdinand of Austria-Este, 8th Grand Mistress of the Order of the Starry Cross 1807-1816 and fourthly Caroline Augusta of Bavaria (1792–1873) daughter of Maximilian I Joseph, King of Bavaria, 9th Grand Mistress of the Order of the Starry Cross 1816-1835
