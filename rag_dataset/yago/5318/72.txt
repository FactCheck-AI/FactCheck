Loni Kaye Anderson (born August 5, 1945 in Saint Paul, Minnesota) is an American actress best known for appearing on the sitcom WKRP in Cincinnati.

She portrayed Jayne Mansfield in the 1980 TV biopic The Jayne Mansfield Story opposite Arnold Schwarzenegger as Mickey Hargitay.

In 1988, Loni Anderson married her third husband Burt Reynolds. They divorced in 1994. In 1997, she released her autobiography, My Life in High Heels.

Notes[]

Loni Anderson is referenced in Episode 821 when young Mike holds a pair of oranges against his chest to mimic breasts.

Loni Anderson co-starred in the short-lived TV comedy Easy Street with Jack Elam in 1987.
