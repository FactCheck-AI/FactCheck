Friedrich Wilhelm IV. (Hohenzollern) von Preußen is a member of the House of Hohenzollern.

Friedrich Wilhelm IV., King of Prussia, was born on October 15, 1795. He was the son of Friedrich Wilhelm III. von Preußen and Luise Auguste Wilhelmine Amalie von Mecklenburg-Strelitz. He died on January 2, 1861 and was buried in the Church of Peace (Friedenskirche) in Sanssouci, Potsdam, Brandenburg.

See Wikipedia for further information.

On November 29, 1823, Friedrich Wilhelm married Elisabeth Ludovika von Bayern, daughter of Maximilian I., King of Bavaria, and Karoline von Baden. They had no children.
