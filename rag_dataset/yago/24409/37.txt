Queen Sirikit was born on August 12th, 1932, in Bangkok, Thailand. She is loved and respected by many Thai people. She is the wife of King Bhumibol Adulyadej and the mother of four children. Queen Sirikit has done a lot to help Thailand.

On her birthday, which is also National Mother's Day, people in Thailand celebrate their mothers. Children give flowers and gifts to their mothers. Schools and communities have special events to honor mothers.

Queen Sirikit is known for her work with the Thai silk industry. She has helped to make Thai silk famous around the world. She started projects to improve education and health in Thailand and also cares about nature, working to protect forests and wildlife.
