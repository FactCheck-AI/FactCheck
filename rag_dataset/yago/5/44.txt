Physical description

Carte-de-visite depicting a three-quarter length portrait of Princess Dagmar of Denmark (1847-1928), who became Maria Feodorovna, Empress of Russia as a spouse of Tsar Alexander III. The carte has been trimmed down around the edges.

Marks and inscriptions

GHEMAR FRERES / PHOTOGRAPHES DU ROI / 27, Rue de L'Ecuyer, 27 (Printed on verso as part of logo.)

Princess Dagmar / of Denmark (Handwritten on verso in pencil.)
