Lady Louise Mountbatten and Crown Prince Gustaf Adolf of Sweden were married on November 3, 1923, in the Chapel Royal in St. James's Palace.

The bride was the second of four children of the late Prince Louis of Battenberg, later the 1st Marquess of Milford Haven and his wife, Princess Victoria of Hesse and by Rhine, a granddaughter of Queen Victoria. Four bridesmaids, Princesses Margarita, Theodora, Cecile, and Sophie of Greece attended her. Another niece and nephew, Lady Tatiana Mountbatten and the Earl of Medina carried her train.
