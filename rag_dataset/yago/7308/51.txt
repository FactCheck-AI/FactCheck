Andrew Lane is an American film producer and director. Together with Wayne Crawford he co-produced the 1983 comedy Valley Girl starring Nicolas Cage in his first co-starring role.[1] The two were responsible for producing several other films including Night of the Comet, Servants of Twilight, Mortal Passions, and Jake Speed.
