LEOMINSTER ---Mary A. (Gordon) Neal, 68 years old, of Leominster, died Monday, September 27, 2021 at Bear Mountain at Andover.

Mary was born in Syracuse, NY to Harold Gordon and Ruth Ann (Daly) Gordon. She was a long-time resident of Shirley, MA where she raised her family with Peter Neal. She is predeceased by her parents, and her brother William Gordon. She is survived by her sisters; Jane Everett and Ellen Gordon as well as her sons Kerry Neal of Ashby, and Casey Neal of Ashburnham, and her grandchildren Carina Conley, Joshua Neal, Benjamin Neal and Gavin Neal.

Mary loved to travel and spend time with her family and friends. She was known for her generosity by always wanting to lend a helping hand to anyone in need. Her heart was so full of love for everyone who crossed her path in life.

Calling hours for Mary will be held Friday, October 1st from 5-7 pm at Richardson Funeral Home, 106 West Street Leominster MA. In lieu of flowers please make donations to the American Heart Association.
