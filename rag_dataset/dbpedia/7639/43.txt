Laurence Leamer is the author of sixteen books including five New York Times bestsellers. He has worked in a factory in France and a coal mine in West Virginia. He was a Peace Corps Volunteer in Nepal. An expert on the Kennedys, Leamer's play Rose about the Kennedy matriarch was an off-Broadway hit. The play was also performed to acclaim in Chicago where it will return next year. There will be a production in Boca later this year. The author is currently writing a book about Donald Trump in Palm Beach.
