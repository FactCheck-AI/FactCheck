Caterpillar to Butterfly Life Cycle Book introduces young readers to the butterfly life cycle with breathtaking, full-page, colored photographs of real butterflies and simple, engaging text. It introduces young readers to the beauty and diversity of these amazing insects. The narrative is simple yet engaging enough to keep children of ages 4 to 6 spellbound. This book is easy-to-read, informative, well-illustrated, and fun.

Recommended For: Grades Pre K to 1

Paperback, 32 pages
