Child actor - TV series and commercial actor. Born in Istanbul, 2015. Her height is 108 cm and her weight is 20 kg. Zeyno Asya Orçin, the daughter of musician İbrahim Orçin and The Teacher Sibel Kaptan Orçin, is a Leyla Şirin Agency actress. The little star, who loves to paint, also attends ballet and archery courses.

Zeyno Asya Orçin, who has never been in a TV series before, was among the cast of the series Misafir for the first time in 2021 and played a girl named Balım in the series. In the same year, she also played in a commercial movie.
