Asya is an alias used by an Unknown Female Singer, but it is possible that could be Melody Castellari.

Only one Eurobeat song was made under the alias, "A Love At First Sight", other songs that have been released under the other Saifam sublabels 21st Century Records and Urban Zoo, mostly consisting of Italo-Dance/Trance/Euro House covers of popular non-Eurobeat songs.
