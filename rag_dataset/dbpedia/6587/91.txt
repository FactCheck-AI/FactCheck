#4544, Walter Turner TOYNBEE

Born 1852 in Lincoln, Lincolnshire Died 1930 in Hyde Park, London

Father Robert TOYNBEE (1824 - 1898) Mother Harriet Louisa TURNER (1826 - 1873)

Inherited Riseholme, a fine house just north of London. Like his brother he was also a good and keen cricketer. He retired and moved to Canterbury in 1902 where he enjoyed watching cricket and playing golf at Royal St George.
