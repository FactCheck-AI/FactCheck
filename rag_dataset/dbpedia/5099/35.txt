Ellie Darcey-Alden (born 4 September 1999[1]) portrayed Francesca Latimer in the 2012 Doctor Who Christmas television story The Snowmen.

Personal life[]

She is the elder sister of Joseph Darcey-Alden, who portrayed Francesca's brother, Digby Latimer, alongside her.

Career[]

Before Doctor Who, she also appeared in another famous franchise: the Harry Potter series, where she played a younger version of Harry's mother in Harry Potter and the Deathly Hallows: Part 2.
