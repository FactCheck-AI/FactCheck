Congratulations to the 2,069 Fall 2016 semester Dean's List awardees from the University of Maryland College of Computer, Mathematical, and Natural Sciences.

To be on the Dean's List, a student must complete 12 or more credits with a semester GPA of 3.5 or higher. This recognition is also noted on the student's academic record.
