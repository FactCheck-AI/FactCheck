Actor Pavail Gulati has started shooting for the second schedule of Shahid Kapoor headliner actioner Deva. Pavail was recently spotted at a Mumbai studio, filming for the second schedule. Apart from him and Shahid, the film also stars Pooja Hegde.

Deva has been directed by acclaimed Malayalam filmmaker Rosshan Andrrews. He is known for films like Kayamkulam Kochunni (2018) and Salute (2022).

Billed to be an action-thriller, the film is about a police officer who, while probing a case, uncovers a web of deceit and betrayal during the process. The film is backed by Zee Studios and Roy Kapur Films. It is scheduled to be released in theatres on October 11, 2024.
