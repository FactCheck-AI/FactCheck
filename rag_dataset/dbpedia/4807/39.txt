BLIDH FOR RYDAHL

The New York Rangers acquired forward Anton Blidh from the Colorado Avalanche for forward Gustav Rydahl.

What does it give the New York Rangers

Blidh is a depth piece for New York, but someone with some NHL experience. He will probably remain in the AHL for now.

What does it give the Colorado Avalanche

Rydahl is a big center who is finishing up his first AHL campaign. He is probably staying in the minors for the foreseeable future.
