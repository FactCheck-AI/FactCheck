SUN PRAIRIE ——–Bernard F. Moy Sr., age 92, passed away on Friday, April 19, 2024 at the Sun Prairie Health Care Center. He was born on July 20, 1931 in Bristol Township and was the son of Leo and Florence (Schmitt) Moy. He married Vera Muckler on October 10, 1959 at St. Joseph’s Catholic Church in East Bristol. He served in the U.S. Army. Bernard also farmed his whole life on the family farm.

He is survived by his 2 daughters, Kathy (John) Rock, and Debbie Moy; a son Bernard (Sandy) Moy Jr.; and a grandson Quintin Moy; a sister Margaret Blaschka;

He was preceded in death by his parents; wife Vera in 1998; his son Michael in 2019; a sister Catherine (Lenus) Ebert and a brother-in-law, Lefty Blaschka.

A Mass of Christian Burial will be held at 11:00 a.m. on Wednesday, April 24, 2024, at Sacred Hearts of Jesus and Mary Catholic Church, 229 Columbus St., Sun Prairie. A visitation will be held from 9:30 a.m. until the time of Mass at the church. Burial will be held in Sun Prairie Memory Gardens.
