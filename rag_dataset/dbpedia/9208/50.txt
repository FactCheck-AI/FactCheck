Nepal’s capital, Kathmandu, is the country’s most populous city. Nepal is India’s neighbouring country. The country is divided into seven states or provinces. It has an area of 1,47,181 square kilometres.

Kathmandu, Nepal’s capital and largest metropolis, is the country’s largest city. Kathmandu is also known as “Temple City.” Since 1985, SAARC has had its headquarters in Kathmandu.

Many museums may be found in Kathmandu. There are numerous museums and art galleries in the city.

Tourism is a significant contributor to the economy. Artists’ handicrafts and art, as well as gifts, contribute to the country’s economy.
