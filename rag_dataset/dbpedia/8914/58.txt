About

Hip-hop recording artist and former battle rapper who is known for his affiliation with the Brooklyn based artist collective Brown Bag AllStars. In 2012, he was listed as one of Allhiphop's Top 50 Underground Hip-Hop Artists.

Before Fame

In 2010, he released a free album entitled Soul Like Khan.

Trivia

In early 2011, he competed in an Ultimate Rap League battle.

Family Life

He is originally from Los Angeles, CA.
