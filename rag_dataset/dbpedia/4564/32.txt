U.S. Marines assigned to Marine Medium Tiltrotor Squadron (VMM) 162 depart to participate in a Weapons and Tactics Instructor Course at Marine Corps Air Station New River, North Carolina, Sept. 19, 2022. VMM-162 prepared personnel and aircraft in support of a WTI course at Marine Corps Air Station Yuma, Arizona. VMM-162 is a subordinate unit of 2nd Marine Aircraft Wing, the aviation combat element of II Marine Expeditionary Force. (U.S. Marine Corps video by Staff Sgt. Theodore Bergan)
