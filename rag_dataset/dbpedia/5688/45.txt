Milagres Church (Mangalore)

The Milagres Church (Portuguese: Igreja Nossa Senhora do Milagres, English: Church of Our Lady of Miracles) is a historic Roman Catholic Church situated in the Hampankatta locality of Mangalore. The church was built in 1680 by Bishop Thomas de Castro, a Theatine from Divar, Goa. The original structure was constructed at the site of the present-day cemetery. It is one of the oldest churches in Dakshina Kannada.

Mount Rosary Church

Mount Rosary Church (Portuguese: De Nossa Senhora do Melagres de Calliampoor) is a Roman Catholic church situated in Kallianpur in the Udupi district. The church came into existence in 1837 at the time of the Goan schism when some Catholic families withdrew from their Parish Milagres Church and acknowledged the jurisdiction of the Vicar Apostolic of Verapoly. They built a church only a few yards away from the Milagres Church. Since then it has undergone alterations and additions.

Milagres Church (Kallianpur)

The Milagres Church (Portuguese: Igreja Nossa Senhora do Milagres, English: Church of Our Lady of Miracles) is a Roman Catholic Cathedral situated at Kallianpur in Udupi district, Karnataka.

Roman Catholic Diocese of Udupi

The Roman Catholic Diocese of Udupi is a diocese located in the city of Udupi in Karnataka. It was announced by Pope Benedict XVI on 16 July 2012. It comprises the three civil districts of Udupi, Karkala, and Kundapura and suffragan of the Archdiocese of Bangalore. The The diocese contains 46 parishes, and the parish church of Church of Our Lady of Miracles, also known as Milagres Church, at Kallianpur in Udupi district, is the Cathedral church of the dioecese.
