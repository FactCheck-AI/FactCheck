LOONEY TUNES B-Team

From Ralph Wolf and Sam Sheepdog to Bugsy and Mugsy, the B-team of the LOONEY TUNES squad prove to be just as funny as their more famous colleagues, as evidenced by this collection featuring lesser-known characters.

TORTOISE BEATS HARE (1941); BUGS BUNNY GETS THE BOID (1942); FELINE FRAME-UP (1954); FROM A TO Z-Z-Z-Z (1954); BUGSY AND MUGSY (1957); THREE LITTLE BOPS (1957); STEAL WOOL (1957); TREE FOR TWO (1952); FEED THE KITTY (1952). Runtime approx. 63 min. NOT RATED; contains cartoon violence.
