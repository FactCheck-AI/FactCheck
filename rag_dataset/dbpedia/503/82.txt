El Triunfo (Chiapas)

Maps, pictures and statistics of El Triunfo, a beautiful large village of 2,418 inhabitants of the State of Chiapas

The town of El Triunfo (Chiapas) belongs to the municipality of Escuintla. There are 2,418 inhabitants and it is 363 meters altitude. It is the most populated town in the position #2 of the whole municipality.

Last Update: 29.05.2024

Data: In El Triunfo, there is a percentage of 1% of indigenous population and 64% of the households have a cell phone. Need more statistics on El Triunfo? They are at the bottom of this page.

To locate this beautiful town inside the municipality, you must know that El Triunfo is located at 12.7 kilometers (in direction East) from the locality of Escuintla, which is the one that has more inhabitants inside the municipality.
