Walking

Darlington Mind is a five-minute walk from Darlington Town centre.

Travel by Train

Darlington Mind is a five-minute walk from Darlington's train station. Darlington is a frequent stop on the East Coast mainline route as well as on local routes Northern Rail or LNER

Car Parking

There are short and long stay off-street car parks surrounding Darlington Mind. Most Council car parks are covered by 24 hour CCTV surveillance. For further advice on parking please contact our reception team on 01325 283169 or 07572 888084 (mobile) or visit the Darlington Borough Council website.
