Futbol Club Barcelona, commonly known as FC Barcelona, is a professional football club based in Barcelona, Spain since its foundation in 1899.

The club is one of the most valuable sports team in the world. Domestically, Barcelona has won a record 75 trophies including 26 La Liga and internationally 20 European and worldwide titles including 5 UEFA Champions League titles.

FC Barcelona is one of the most widely supported teams in the world, with the largest social media following in the world among sports teams.
