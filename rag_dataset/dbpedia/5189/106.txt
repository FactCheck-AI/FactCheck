Where is Horicon, Wisconsin ?

Horicon is located in Dodge County, nestled in the southeastern part of the United States. It is situated in Wisconsin, roughly 67 miles northwest of Milwaukee and about 50 miles northeast of Madison. The city is near the Horicon Marsh, a significant freshwater marsh providing natural scenery.
