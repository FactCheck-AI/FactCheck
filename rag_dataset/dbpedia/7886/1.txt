Australian born actor Brenton Thwaites has been cast as the lead in Entry Level, an action thriller from director Franck Khalfoun. The film is set to begin shooting early next year and 13 Films is handling international sales on the project at AFM this week. Written by Benjamin Hurley, the pic follows a young man who mysteriously awakens in a super high-security office building. He is forced by an unknown stranger to complete a strictly timed series of dangerous tasks that implicate him in a deadly crime with global consequences. Craig Perry and Sheila Taylor of Practical Pictures will produce with Content Media’s Tom Butterfield. Dan Spilo of Industry Entertainment will executive produce. Thwaites stars in Disney’s upcoming sequel Pirates of the Caribbean: Dead Men Tell No Tales alongside Johnny Depp. He is repped by WME and Industry Entertainment.
