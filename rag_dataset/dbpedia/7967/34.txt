Formula 1 and Apple made the announcement about the highly anticipated movie which stars Pitt, is directed by Joseph Kosinski, produced by Jerry Bruckheimer with Lewis Hamilton’s Dawn Apollo Films company also involved.

The film, titled simply F1, will be released internationally on 25 June 2025, in North America two days later on 27 June 2025, and distributed around the world and in IMAX by Warner Bros.

Made in collaboration with Formula 1, the movie is filmed in real-time during grands prix around the world and involving the Formula 1 community, including all 10 teams, the drivers, the FIA and race promoters.

The film will see Pitt star as a former driver who returns to Formula 1, alongside Damson Idris as his rookie team-mate at APXGP, a fictional team on the grid, battling against the sport’s drivers and teams.

Production of the movie, which began in 2023, will continue at this year’s British Grand Prix and at several other races, and complete at the season-ending Abu Dhabi Grand Prix in December.

The star-studded cast includes Academy Award-nominee Kerry Condon, Academy Award-winner Javier Bardem, Emmy Award-winner and Golden Globe Award nominee Tobias Menzies, Sarah Niles, Kim Bodnia, Samson Kayo and Callie Cooke.
