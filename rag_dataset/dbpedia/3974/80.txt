İzmir

İzmir ranks 405th in number of biographies on Pantheon, behind Kumamoto Prefecture, Worcester, Massachusetts, and Oviedo. Memorable people born in İzmir include Polycarp, Édouard Balladur, and Vahide Perçin. Memorable people who died in İzmir include Zeki Müren, Quintus Servilius Caepio, and Carl Humann. İzmir has been the birth place of many actors, and soccer players and the death place of many writers, and politicians. İzmir is located in Türkiye.
