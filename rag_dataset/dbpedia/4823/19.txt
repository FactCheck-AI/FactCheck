How many minutes are there in one eighth of an hour?

There are 60 minutes in one hour. Therefore, one eighth of an hour is equal to 60/8 = 7.5 minutes, or 7 minutes 30 seconds.

How many seconds are there in 5 hours 30 minutes?

There are 60 seconds in one minute. There are 60 minutes in one hour. Therefore, 5 hours 30 minutes is equal to (5 x 60 x 60) + (30 x 60) = 19800 seconds.
