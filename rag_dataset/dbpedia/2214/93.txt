An acoustic celebration of some of the world’s great musical styles, from infectious African and Latin rhythms to far flung gems from Chinese and Indian cultures, this is an album guaranteed to broaden your musical horizons.

‘The Rough Guide to World Music Unplugged’ helps to promote a deeper understanding of musical traditions and global cultures and includes unique recordings by masters of endangered instruments and traditions. A seamless collection underlining the Rough Guide’s status as the number one compilation label.
