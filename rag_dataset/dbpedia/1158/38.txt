This is a 2022 reissue vinyl LP pressing of Deja Vu by Crosby Stills Nash & Young. The American folk-rock supergroup originally released their second studio album on March 11, 1970, via Atlantic Records. Produced by the band members, the album topped the pop album chart for one week and was ranked No. 148 in 2003 on Rolling Stone magazine's list of the 500 greatest albums of all time. Certified 7× platinum by RIAA, the album's sales currently sit at over 8 million copies. It remains the highest-selling album of each member's career to date.
