High Five Coffee is a family-owned small business in the mountains of Asheville, North Carolina. In our three locations, we serve a range of local baked goods, breakfast, lunch and snack options to accompany our traditional and house specialty coffee drinks, Our staff of baristas are trained both in-house and in partnership with the standards of Counter Culture Coffee. We are constantly chasing our goal to serve you delicious coffee with intentional and kind service. When it comes to our ideals, ensuring that you enjoy your experience is the most important part of what we do. From the flavor of our coffee to the warmth our baristas bring, you will want to give total strangers a high five after your time with us!
