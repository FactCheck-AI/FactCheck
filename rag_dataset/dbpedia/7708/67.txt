Z-4195 Marion (better known as Z) is the main protagonist in DreamWorks first computer animated 1998 film, Antz. An idealistic worker ant, Z rushes to find a sense of individuality. Along the way he falls in love with princess Bala and learned what it's like to be an ant.

Background[]

Z is the middle child of at least five million siblings. Because of this number, Z's mother didn't have time for her son because she was taking care of her other children. His father left when Z was in a larvae state. He was childhood friends with Weaver, who would become a warrior in the Queen's army. They supported each other through everything.
