Cascada (, Spanish, 'Waterfall'), stylized as CASCADA, CASCADA and cascada, is a German dance music act founded in 2004 by singer Natalie Horler and DJs/producers DJ Manian and DJ Yanou. It is best known for the hit singles "Everytime We Touch", "What Hurts the Most", "Evacuate the Dancefloor" and "Miracle". While being one of the most successful acts of the dance music genre itself, Cascada was named as the third-most successful German act of the 21st century. In 2010, Cascada's music was the second-most downloaded of all time in the dance category, after David Guetta.
