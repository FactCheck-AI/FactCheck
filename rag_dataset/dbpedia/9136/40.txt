The life of Kastamonu is a blend of traditional Turkish culture with modern elements. Lifestyle in the province shows significant differences between rural and urban areas. While life in rural areas is mostly based on agriculture and animal husbandry, modern job opportunities and educational opportunities come to the fore in the city center. Kastamonu is known for its calm and peaceful atmosphere. The traditional family structure has an important place in the relationships within the community and extended family members often come together and organize social events.

Traditional events and festivals have a big place in the social life of Kastamonu. Especially "Kastamonu Entegre Culture and Tourism Festival" is organized every year to emphasize the historical and cultural values of the city. Within the scope of this festival, many activities such as local artists, handicraft exhibitions and local food competitions are organized. In addition, traditional iftar tables and mevlits organized during Ramadan strengthen the religious and cultural ties of the community.

Local cuisine is an indispensable part of Kastamonu life. The province is especially famous for its local dishes such as meat bread, banduma and pull halva. These dishes have an important place in special occasions and festivals. In addition, products such as garlic bread and Siyez bulgur of Tosya reflect the rich agricultural products of the region.

Traditional clothing in Kastamonu is also a culture that is still being maintained by some segments. Local clothes, especially worn by women, are preferred for weddings, holidays and special occasions. Traditional handicrafts also have an important place; especially woodworking, basketry and weaving carry Kastamonu's craftsmanship tradition to the present day.

Life in Kastamonu is intertwined with the natural beauties of the region. Ilgaz Mountain and the surrounding national parks offer ideal areas for activities such as nature walks, camping and picnics. In addition, Kastamonu's historical texture and architectural structures create an atmosphere intertwined with history in the daily life of the local people. This natural and historical environment both improves the quality of life of local people and becomes an attractive destination for tourists. These features make Kastamonu a province that offers a calm, traditional and nature-intertwined life.
