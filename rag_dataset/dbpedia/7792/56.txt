Dufftown Golf Club is situated in Speyside, Banffshire where the scenery is breathtaking and visitors can enjoy the fantastic 18 hole golf course. The golf club offers training courses and seminars during the week and is ever-working to increase the interest for the game of golf. Dufftown Golf Club also hosts various golf outings throughout the year and caters to dining in the clubhouse. The fantastic 18 hole golf course at Dufftown takes advantage of the stunning and breathtaking views of the Speyside countryside. The 7th and the 10th holes of the course feature in “Britain’s 100 Extraordinary Golf Holes” and are the key reason visitors are returning again and again.
