Welcome to Sacred Heart ...

Sacred Heart is located in Pottawatomie County<1>.

While we don't have a date for the founding of Sacred Heart, you might consider that their post office opened in 1888.

Sacred Heart is 1,060 feet [323 m] above sea level.<2>.

Time Zone: Sacred Heart lies in the Central Time Zone (CST/CDT) and observes daylight saving time

Sacred Heart is located in the (405) area code.
