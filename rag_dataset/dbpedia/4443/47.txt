Spoetzl Brewery

603 E Brewery St.

Shiner, TX 77984

361-594-3852

Founded in 1909, Spoetzl Brewery is the oldest independent brewery in Texas and is well known for handcrafting fine beers which reflect the tradition of genuine Bavarian beers. Brewery tours are offered on weekdays during which time visitors can learn about the production process for Spoetzl's product line which includes Shiner Bock (their flagship beer) and Prickly Pear Lager along with their Light Blonde, Wild Hare Pale Ale, Hefeweizen and seasonal offerings such as Holiday Cheer, Shiner Oktoberfest, and more.
