Top Gun: Maverick (Music from the Motion Picture) is the soundtrack to the film Top Gun: Maverick. It was composed by Lorne Balfe, Harold Faltermeyer, Lady Gaga, and Hans Zimmer.[1][2]

The soundtrack was released on May 27, 2022, through Interscope Records in both digital and physical formats.[3]

Notes[]

There are various songs that were featured in the film but not included in the soundtrack:[15]

Hank Williams – "Your Cheatin' Heart" – Plays at the small town diner after Maverick crashes the Darkstar.

David Bowie – "Let's Dance" – Plays on jukebox at the Hard Deck bar.

T. Rex – "Bang a Gong (Get It On)" – Plays on jukebox at the bar.

Otis Redding and Carla Thomas – "Tramp" – Plays on jukebox at the bar when Rooster first appears.

Foghat – "Slow Ride" – Plays on jukebox at the bar, as selected by Hangman.

The Who – "Won't Get Fooled Again" – Plays on the first training montage.

Harold Faltermeyer - "Viper Comes Down on Mav" (from Top Gun Soundtrack)[16]

Jacob Anderson - "Taps" - Plays during Iceman's funeral.[16]
