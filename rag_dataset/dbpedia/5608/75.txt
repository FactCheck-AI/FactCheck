Larry Bruce Miller, 76 of Paragould died Monday, December 7, 2020. He was born September 11, 1944 in Cave City, Arkansas to Lowell Ray and Nona Lee Hunter Miller. He was married to Mary Wackerly on November 23, 1967. He was a retired typewriter sales and serviceman and a member of New Haven Baptist Church in Paragould. He was a loving husband, father, grandfather and friend.

He was preceded in death by his parents; two brothers, C.R. “Bobby” Miller and Charles Miller and one sister, Laura Belcher.

He was survived by his wife, Mary R. Miller for 13 days , until her death on December 20, 2020.

Survivors include one son, Tim Miller (Jennifer) of Paragould; one daughter, Jennifer McPherson (David) of Paragould; one brother Carl Miller (Patsy) of Batesville; two sisters-in-law, Shirley Miller of Little Rock and Helen Miller of Cave City; one brother-in-law, Earl Belcher of Illinois; two grandchildren, Morgan McPherson and Daniel Miller both of Paragould; several nieces, nephews, cousins and a host of other relatives and friends.

Graveside services will be Tuesday, December 29, 2020 at 2:00 p.m. at the Kyler Cemetery at Southside with Ron Pickney officiating. Arrangements are by Qualls Batesville Funeral Service.

Honorary pallbearers are Mike Miller, Dewayne Roper, Robbie Robinson and David McPherson.
