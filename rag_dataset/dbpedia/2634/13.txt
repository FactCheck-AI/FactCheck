About

Malaysian singer and film actress who won the Penyanyi Wanita Popular Award for her debut pop album.

Before Fame

Her performance in a 1993 Japanese talent show launched her musical career.

Trivia

She played a girl-next-door type in her popular, 1998 film, Puteri Impian.

Family Life

Her family lived in Perak, Malaysia at the time of her birth.
