Hundreds of friends and family members are attending the funeral of journalist and author Amnon Dankner, who died in his home on Friday. He will be buried at the Morasha cemetery in Ramat Hasharon. Amongst those attending the funeral are Yesh Atid Chairman Yair Lapid, former Prime Minister Ehud Olmert, MK Aryeh Deri (Shas) and senior Israeli media professionals. (Gilad Morag)
