The History of Ferrari watches

The history of Ferrari watches begins in 1929, when Italian entrepreneur Enzo Ferrari founded the Ferrari brand by naming his Formula One team "Scuderia Ferrari." In 1950, Enzo Ferrari was given a watch. Ferrari came up with the idea of giving watches on the most important occasions. To maintain exclusivity, he bought six watches at a time. Production of the first series of Ferrari watches started in the 1970s. The best of this series were given as gifts. In 1983, Ferrari launched a new collection of men's watches. The colors of the Ferrari logo were incorporated in the dials. The red, yellow and black symbolizing speed, strength and courage.
