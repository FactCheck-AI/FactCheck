Martinete can be obtained through the Flamenco Night event.

Set[]

Martinete is part of the Voice of Flamenco set.

The other parts of this set are Alegria, Song of Freedom, Farruca, Malaguena, Tonas, Voice of Flamenco, Cante Grande, Cante Intermedio, Cante Chico, Jondo, Seguiriya, Compas, Solea, Guajira and New Flamenco.
