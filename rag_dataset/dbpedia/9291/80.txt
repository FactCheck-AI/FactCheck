Cher is an Oscar, Emmy and Grammy-winning singer, actor and television personality. She is one of the best-selling music artists of all time, with over 100 million records sold worldwide. Cher rose to fame as one half of the husband-wife duo Sonny & Cher after their song “I Got You Babe” reached No. 1 on the US and UK charts. As a solo artist Cher has had four singles peak at No. 1 on the Billboard Hot 100 chart. Cher became a television personality in the 1970s with her CBS shows “The Sonny & Cher Comedy Hour” and “Cher.” On the big screen, Cher has starred in numerous movies including “Silkwood,” “Burlesque” and “Moonstruck,” for which she won the Academy Award for Best Actress in a Leading Role. Her first-ever holiday album “Cher Christmas” is out now.
