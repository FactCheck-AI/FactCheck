Biography

Jo Van Fleet is Notable.

Jo Van Fleet was an American theatre and film actress. She was the winner of an Academy Award for Best Supporting Actress (for East of Eden) and a Tony Award (for The Trip to Bountiful).[1]

Catherine was born in 1915. She was the daughter of Roy Hugh Van Fleet and Bessie Gardner.[2] In 1920 her father had died and she lived with her mother, sister, aunt Charlotte, and grandparents Ralph W. Gardner and Mary.[3] In 1930 she lived with her mother.[4]

In 1940 she was teaching high school and boarding in Lemoore, Kings County, California, United States.[5]

She passed away in 1996.
