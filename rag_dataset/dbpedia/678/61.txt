Biography

Amir Jafari is an actor who was born in 1974 in Tehran, Iran. He started his career in television and starred in “Bedoone Sharh” series by Mehdi Mazloomi. His most notable activities are “Murphy's Law” by Rambod Javan, “13” by Houman Seyedi, Hasan Fathi’s “Postchi se bar dar nemizanad”, “Shahrzad” series and “Mive-ye mamnooe” series.
