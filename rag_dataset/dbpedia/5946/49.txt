Oando Trading DMCC (OTD) is a supply and trading company and a fully-owned subsidiary of Oando PLC. OTD is a key participant in international oil markets with a significant presence in the West African region, and direct access to major energy markets across Africa via its offices in the United Arab Emirates and South Africa.

In the past year, the company exported 57 million barrels of crude oil jointly with its JV partners, and traded just under 3 million metric tonnes of refined petroleum products.
