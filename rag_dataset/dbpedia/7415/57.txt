About

Musician best known as the singer and drummer for the band The Cowsills. He has also played with the touring incarnation of The Beach Boys.

Before Fame

He was previously a member of the band Tommy Tutone, who scored exactly one hit with the song 867-5309/Jenny.

Trivia

He played on The Beach Boys' 2012 comeback album That's Why God Made the Radio.

Family Life

He married Vicki Peterson from the band The Bangles in 2003.
