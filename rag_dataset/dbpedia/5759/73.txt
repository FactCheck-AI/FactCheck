Cafe Drøbak is located in the middle of the square in the center of Drøbak in one of the city's oldest wooden houses.

The popular cafe offers hot dishes and home-baked cakes. In high season they make their own ice cream - Guri Malla Ice Cream and also have a large selection of both scoop ice cream and soft ice cream.

Cafe Drøbak also has a cozy backyard where there are often concerts and an outdoor terrace on Drøbak square. The green tree house is also very nicely decorated for Christmas with strings of lights, Christmas trees and decorations. They also have tradtion to celebrate Christmas in July with norwegian traditional christmasfood.
