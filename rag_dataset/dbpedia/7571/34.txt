Profession: Comedian and Actress

Biography: Sarah Silverman is an American stand-up comedian, actress, producer and writer. She is known for her satirical comedy addressing social issues such as racism, sexism and religion. She has also starred in numerous films and television shows.

Silverman began her career as a writer and performer on Saturday Night Live in the 1990s. She then went on to create and star in her own comedy sketch show, The Sarah Silverman Program. She has since appeared in films such as Wreck-It Ralph and A Million Ways to Die in the West.

Born: December 1, 1970

Birthplace: Bedford, New Hampshire, USA

Age: 53 years old
