Port Louis is located on the northwest coast of the island of Mauritius, in the Indian Ocean. It is the capital and largest city of Mauritius, known for its multicultural population and colonial architecture. Tucked away in the Indian Ocean, Port Louis is the bustling capital of Mauritius. The city blends French colonial charm with a vibrant multicultural atmosphere. Port Louis is a hub of economic, cultural, and administrative activities in Mauritius.
