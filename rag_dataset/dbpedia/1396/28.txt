26K – Wooden cross, Tau

Tau is the nineteenth letter of the Greek alphabet and is translated as T in French.

This figure, in the shape of a T, is the distinctive symbol used by the Franciscans Brothers (A member of the Order founded by Saint Francis of Assisi) to represent the cross.

This model, with an overlay of black walnut, is made of maple.

Including cord necklace– 29’’ (74 cm)

Height: 1”(2,5 cm)

Finish: satin varnish
