37A – Wooden cross, Tau

Tau is the nineteenth letter of the Greek alphabet and is translated as T in French.

This figure, in the shape of a T, is the distinctive symbol used by the Franciscans Brothers

(A member of the Order founded by Saint Francis of Assisi)

to represent the cross.

The TAU crosses is made with cherry wood, engraved and laser cut

Including cord necklace – 29’’ (74 cm)

Height: 1 1/4” (3,2 cm)

finish: satin varnish
