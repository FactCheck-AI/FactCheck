We all know about East Texas, West Texas and Central Texas. And some refer to North Texas and South Texas.

Another area not found on most maps or websites is what we call "Middle Texas", an imaginary space just to west of, and adjacent to, East Texas. We have produced the map below to help us remember those county names and county seats close to us here in East Texas.
