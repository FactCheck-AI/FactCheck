GRAND DUCHY OF MECKLENBURG-STRELITZ 1815-1918:

HOUSE OF PRIBISLAW (Haus Pribislaw)

1815-1816: CHARLES II (Karl Ludwig Friedrich)

Born in 1741 at Mirow.

Father: Prince Karl Ludwig of Mecklenburg-Strelitz. Mother: Princess Elisabeth of Saxe-Hildburghausen.

Married firstly in 1768 at Darmstadt Frederica of Hesse-Darmstadt (*1752 Darmstadt,+1782 Hanover).

Married secondly in 1784 at Darmstadt Charlotte of Hesse-Darmstadt (*1755 Darmstadt,+1785 Hanover).

His issue who reigned:

-Charlotte (*1769,+1818; Duchess of Saxe-Hildburghausen; daughter of Frederica),

-Louise (*1776,+1810; Queen of Prussia; daughter of Frederica),

-Frederica (*1778,+1841; Queen of Hanover; daughter of Frederica),

-GEORGE (*1779,+1860; son of Frederica).

Died in 1816 at Neustrelitz.

Buried with his both consorts in the Church of St John of Jerusalem at Mirow.

1816-1860: GEORGE (Georg)

Born in 1779 in Hanover.

Father: Grand Duke Charles II of Mecklenburg-Strelitz. Mother: Frederica of Hesse-Darmstadt.

Married in 1817 at Kassel Princess MARIE of Hesse-Kassel (*1796 Hanau,+1880 Neustrelitz).

His issue who reigned: FREDERICK WILLIAM (*1819,+1904).

Died in 1860 at Neustrelitz.

Buried with his consort Grand Duchess Marie in the Church of St John of Jerusalem at Mirow.

1860-1904: FREDERICK WILLIAM (Friedrich Wilhelm)

Born in 1819 at Neustrelitz.

Father: Grand Duke George of Mecklenburg-Strelitz. Mother: Grand Duchess Marie of Hesse-Kassel.

Married in 1843 in London Princess AUGUSTA of Great Britain and Hanover (*1822 Hanover,+1916 Neustrelitz).

His issue who reigned: ADOLPHUS FREDERICK V (*1848,+1914).

Died in 1904 at Neustrelitz.

Buried with his consort Grand Duchess Augusta in the Church of St John of Jerusalem at Mirow.

1904-1914: ADOLPHUS FREDERICK V (Adolf Friedrich V.)

Born in 1848 at Neustrelitz.

Father: Grand Duke Frederick William of Mecklenburg-Strelitz. Mother: Grand Duchess Augusta of Great Britain.

Married in 1877 at Dessau Princess ELIZABETH of Anhalt (*1857 WÃ¶rlitz,+1933 Neustrelitz).

His issue who reigned: ADOLPHUS FREDERICK VI (*1882,+1918).

Died in 1914 in Berlin.

Buried with his consort Grand Duchess Elizabeth in the Church of St John of Jerusalem at Mirow.

1914-1918: ADOLPHUS FREDERICK VI (Adolf Friedrich VI.)

Born in 1882 at Neustrelitz.

Father: Grand Duke Adolphus Frederick V of Mecklenburg-Strelitz. Mother: Grand Duchess Elizabeth of Anhalt.

Unmarried.

Died in 1918 in Neustrelitz (suicide).

Buried on Lovers' Island in Mirow.
