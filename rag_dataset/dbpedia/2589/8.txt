Joel C. Moritz, 69, of Collins Center, passed away on Friday, July 21, 2023 at his home.

He was born in Gowanda on Jan. 6, 1954, the son of the late Millard and Marion (nee Kriegel) Moritz. He was married to the former Debra Simmons.

Joel was an electrician with Winsmith in Springville for 38 years. He was an avid outdoorsman and loved to fish and hunt. He was great with small engines and was known as being able to fix anything.

In addition to his parents, he was preceded in death by a sister Mary Moritz.

He is survived by his loving wife Debra Simmons, who he just celebrated 50 years of marriage with in February; his son Jason (Roxanne) Moritz of Collins; two daughters Stephanie (Mike) Cieszynski of Collins and Jessica (Jeffrey) Fort of Gowanda; four grandchildren Jacob and Evan Cieszynski and Hailey and Lexie Fort; a brother Jerrold (Charlotte) Moritz of Utah and two sisters Joyce Kota of Rochester and Janice Moritz of Florida and several nieces and nephews.

Private funeral services will be held.
