Josh Homme has revealed that the new Queens Of The Stoneage album is “all about wiggling hips” and will recall the sounds associated with their last studio album “Era Vulgaris.” He went onto say that he and the band – Troy Van Leeuwen, Nick Oliveri, Joey Castillo and Dean Fertita – are please the record will be released on indie label Dangerbird so they can make music “just for the sake of it.” No release date for the unnamed album has been set, but keep your eyes peeled for early next year.
