Synopsis

The Dadasaheb Phalke International Film Festival Awards 2024 witnessed Shah Rukh Khan's victory as he clinched the Best Actor award for his role in Jawan, alongside Nayanthara who secured the Best Actress award for the same film. Sandeep Reddy Vanga was honoured with the Best Director accolade for Animal, while Vicky Kaushal's performance in Sam Bahadur earned him the Best Actor (Critics) award.

The esteemed Dadasaheb Phalke International Film Festival Awards 2024 recently concluded, showcasing a blend of talent and recognition within the film industry. Among the notable winners were Shah Rukh Khan and Sandeep Reddy Vanga, highlighting their exceptional contributions to cinema.

'Jawan' Shines Bright At Dadasaheb Phalke Awards

Shah Rukh Khan emerged victorious, securing the coveted Best Actor award for his remarkable portrayal in 'Jawan', a film that captivated audiences with its compelling narrative. Sharing the limelight, Nayanthara, his co-star in the same film, was bestowed with the Best Actress award for her stellar performance.

Anirudh Ravichander's musical prowess in 'Jawan' was duly acknowledged, earning him the Best Music Director title. Additionally, Sandeep Reddy Vanga's directorial brilliance in Animal earned him the prestigious Best Director accolade, adding another feather to his cap.

Other Notable Honorees

The critics' choice resonated with Vicky Kaushal's performance in Sam Bahadur, which earned him the Best Actor (Critics) award, further cementing his reputation as a versatile performer.

The event also recognised outstanding contributions across various mediums. Television luminaries such as Rupali Ganguly and Neil Bhatt were honored for their exemplary work, with Ghum Hai Kisikey Pyaar Meiin emerging as the Television Series of the Year. Karishma Tanna's commendable performance in Scoop garnered her the Best Actress in a Web Series award.

Acknowledging the industry's stalwarts, Moushumi Chatterjee and K.J. Yesudas were rightfully celebrated for their enduring impact on Indian cinema and music, respectively.

Further, the awards ceremony witnessed heartfelt reunions and memorable moments on the red carpet, with stars like Shahid Kapoor, Kareena Kapoor, and Rani Mukerji gracing the event with their presence. Notable encounters, including Shahid and Kareena's fleeting eye-locking moment, added to the evening's charm. Check out the full winners list:

Best Actor: Shah Rukh Khan, 'Jawan'

Best Actress: Nayanthara, 'Jawan'

Best Actress: Rani Mukerji, 'Mrs Chatterjee vs Norway'

Best Director: Sandeep Reddy Vanga, 'Animal'

Best Actor (Critics): Vicky Kaushal, 'Sam Bahadur'

Best Music Director: Anirudh Ravichander, 'Jawan'

Best Playback Singer (Male): Varun Jain, 'Tere Vaste from Zara Hatke Zara Bachke'

Best Actor in a Negative Role: Bobby Deol, 'Animal'

Best Actress in a Television Series: Rupali Ganguly, 'Anupamaa'

Best Actor in a Television Series: Neil Bhatt, 'Ghum Hai Kisikey Pyaar Meiin'

Television Series of the Year: 'Ghum Hai Kisikey Pyaar Meiin'

Best Actress in a Web Series: Karishma Tanna, 'Scoop'

Outstanding Contribution to the Film Industry: Moushumi Chatterjee

Outstanding Contribution to the Music Industry: KJ Yesudas
