Joseph Robert Andrews of Brookfield, CT died on Wednesday June 12,2019 from an overdose. Joseph was born on January 23,1987 and was a kind charismatic young man. He was a graduate of Henry Abbot Technical High School and was a talented tradesman.

He worked as an electrician, auto mechanic, and dairy farmer. Joseph loved music, cars, and animals. He is predeceased by his grandparents Mary and Joseph Andrews and Enid and Robert Whittingham.

He leaves behind and will be dearly missed by his parents Wendy and David Andrews, his brother Jeffrey Andrews, his sister Amy Martone, brother-in-law Chris Martone, two nieces Sadie and Erin, aunts, uncles and cousins. A private service for immediate family is planned.
