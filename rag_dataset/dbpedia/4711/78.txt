Western Canada-based Pizza 73 recently opened new franchises in Edmonton, Didsbury and Wetaskiwin, Alta.

“We’ve always believed in organic, sustained and carefully planned growth,” says Pat Finelli, chief marketing officer (CMO) for Pizza 73. “We’re very excited to meet our new customers and build relationships, one order at a time, in all communities.”

Established in 1985, it serves more than 20 specialty pizzas with a wide variety of toppings, chicken wings, jalapeno poppers, oven-baked pasta, curly fries and onion rings. Pizza 73 is the sister company of Eastern Canada’s Pizza Pizza.
