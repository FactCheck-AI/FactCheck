The Security Council voted unanimously this afternoon to recommend to the General Assembly the admission of Botswana and Lesotho to the United Nations.

Chief S.O. Adebo (Nigeria) making the first statement in support of the two draft resolutions before the Council. On the left is Mr. F.H. Corner (New Zealand), and at the right is Apollo K. Kironde (Uganda).

14 October 1966

United Nations, New York
