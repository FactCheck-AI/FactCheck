Modern Family star Sarah Hyland has revealed her character on the hit comedy show will ‘come out’ as bisexual this season.

The actress, who plays Haley Dunphy on the series, took to Twitter on Wednesday (01Nov17) to make the big reveal after one fan asked her if it was true her character is bisexual.

“I don’t know what the writers would say? But I confirm,” she responded.

Haley has yet to date a woman on the show and she didn’t offer up any plot details.
