Valletta is the capital city of Malta. Geographically, it is located in the South Eastern Region, in the central-eastern portion of the main island of Malta. The historical city has a population of 6,444 as of March 2014,while the metropolitan area around it has a population of 393,938. Valletta is the southernmost capital of Europe.
