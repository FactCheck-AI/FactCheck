Channel 3 Records have launched preorders for the NES soundtracks to Double Dragon and Double Dragon II: The Revenge together on vinyl.

The release will be a single 180g LP in three different color variants – a “Double Dragon” gold variant (100 copies), “Jimmy” red splatter variant (450 copies), and a “Billy” blue splatter variant (450 copies).

It will be housed in a gatefold jacket with game art housed inside a gold-foil slipcover.
