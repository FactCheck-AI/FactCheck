Robert Ford is a two-time Emmy-winning documentary producer with over 25 years of experience in film and TV.

Robert's various credits include 'Super/Man: The Christopher Reeve Story' (Opening Night Film of Sundance 2024), 'The Deepest Breath' (Netflix top 10 in 39 countries), 'Rising Phoenix', 'Formula 1: Drive To Survive', 'Citizen K' and many more.

His films and series have been distributed by Warner Bros, Universal, A24, Netflix, Amazon Prime, BBC, and played at major festivals like Sundance, Tribeca, CPH:DOX and Venice.
