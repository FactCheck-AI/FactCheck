Prema Baraha is a 2018 Kannada romantic drama movie starring Chandhan, Aishwarya Arjun, Arjun Sarja and others. The story revolves round Sanjay and Madhu, two journalists who hate each other, who are forced to work together for a common objective of covering the Kargil war. Circumstances however change when Sanjay and Madhu fall in love with each other.
