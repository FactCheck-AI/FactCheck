Tholi Prema Movie is directed by the Venky Atluri and produced by BVSN Prasad under the banner Sri Venkateswara Cine Chitra, the movie cast includes Varun Tej, Raashi Khanna, Sapna Pabbi, Priyadarshi, Suhasini Maniratnam and Darbha Appaji Ambarisha. SS Thaman composed the music for Tholi Prema.
