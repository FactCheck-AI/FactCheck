Serial queen Linda Stirling takes the title role (Zorro, not the whip!) in this tale of a feisty newspaper girl who assumes her murdered brother's masked identity to fight outlaws opposed to statehood for the Idaho Territory. Features six cliffhanging episodes. With George J Lewis and Lucien Littlefield. Based on the character created by Johnston McCulley who first appeared, serialised in print, in a 1919 pulp magazine.
