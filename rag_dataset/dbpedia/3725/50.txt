FC Omniworld[]

2001-2010[]

Almere City was founded as FC Omniworld in 2001.

AFC Almere City[]

2010[]

The club was renamed AFC Almere City in 2010. The name change only lasted a few weeks however, due to the similarity of the name with AFC Ajax.

Almere City FC[]

2010-2011[]

The club was renamed to Almere City FC a few weeks after the name change to AFC Almere City.
