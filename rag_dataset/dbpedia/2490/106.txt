Duane R. Carter, 71, of Bloomington, passed away at 4:27 p.m. Thursday (March 22, 2018) at Heritage Health, Bloomington.

Graveside service will be at 1 p.m. Friday at Park Hill Cemetery, Bloomington. The family will receive friends at a celebration of life gathering from 2:30 to 6 p.m. Friday at the VFW Post 454 in Bloomington. Cremation rites have been accorded by Carmody-Flynn Funeral Home, Bloomington.

In lieu of flowers, memorials may be made to the VFW Post 454, Bloomington.

He was born May 31, 1946, in Colfax, son of Homer and Irene Wagoner Carter. He married Judy Wheeler on Jan. 15, 1966. She preceded him in death in January 2009. He was also preceded in death by one brother, George Carter.

Surviving are his sons, Mark Carter and Eric Carter, both of Bloomington; one grandson, Alex Carter, Bloomington; and one sister, Helen Andreae, Sibley.

He was a U.S. Army veteran and a member of the VFW Post 454, Bloomington, and the Waynesville American Legion Post 1189.

Duane worked for over 40 years at Bridgestone-Firestone, retiring in 2009.

He enjoyed spending his free time fishing and shooting pool with his friends.

To express condolences, please visit www.carmodyflynn.com.
