Description

Located in the Northeast of Brazil is the state of Bahia. Its capital city is Salvador, which was once the Capital of Brazil. The History of the colonial settlements and the African slavery has produced an amazingly rich and fascinating culture. The city also has beautiful beaches and excellent cuisine.

Salvador is a magical place, where the rhythm and the musicality of the Brazilian people shines from its historical roots. From Salvador you can explore the coast of Bahia including Porto Seguro which was the birthplace of Brazil.

Praia do Forte

More than 12 km of paradisiac beaches, with natural pools formed by reefs along the coast and the coconut trees make Praia do Forte an invitation to relaxing. The Tamar Project (Conservation of Marine Turtles that lay eggs on these beaches), a medieval castle -the only in Brazil, besides the charming old fishing village are attractions not to be missed.

Morro de São Paulo

Located in the island of Tinharé, about 250 Km South of Salvador, Morro de São Paulo has miles of paradisiac calm beaches, forming natural pools, great for snorkeling and swimming. During the summer, the nightlife is animated.

Activities

Relaxing on the beaches, enjoying the water-sports, city tours to the main attractions and rich local culture of Salvador, eat the local food, visits to Praia do Forte and Itaparica Island.
