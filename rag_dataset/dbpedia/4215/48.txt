Ragdoll Productions is a British production company that produces children's shows. It was founded in 1984 by Anne Wood. The slogan for the company is "Works for Children". It made shows like Teletubbies, Pob's Programme, Playbox, Rosie and Jim, Tots TV, Brum, Tronji, Badjelly the Witch, Open a Door, Boohbah, In The Night Garden, Dip Dap, The Adventures of Abney and Teal, Twirlywoos and B.O.T. and the Beasties. On 16 September 2013, Ragdoll was sold to a Canadian production company DHX Media (now WildBrain), DHX currently holds the rights to every Ragdoll show, except for Pob's Programme and Playbox. However, Ragdoll Worldwide still producing new shows.
