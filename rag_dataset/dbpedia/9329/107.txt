Zemun, Belgrade, Serbia

Latitude and longitude coordinates are: 44.854897, 20.394058.

Zemun is a famous satellite municipality of Belgrade, the oldest and the most populated outskirts area of the capital city of Serbia. The first settlement appeared in the area in the epochs of neolithic eras. The town is located on three hills in the area where the Danube river widens and becomes more beautiful. There is a great number of points of interest, including local churches, squares, bridges, historic buildings, etc.
