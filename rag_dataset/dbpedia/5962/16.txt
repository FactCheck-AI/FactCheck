Course Information

The SMSTS Site Management 2 Day Refresher training is a crucial course for site managers to update their health and safety knowledge. It is designed for safety-critical industries and is a recognised award from CITB, valid for 5 years. This course allows managers to refresh their skills and effectively manage potential hazards in the workplace.

Who Should Attend?

Construction site managers and senior site supervisors or anyone who currently holds a valid and in date Site Management Safety Training Scheme SMSTS certificate. The certificate has to be renewed every five years by attending this SMSTS Refresher course. Please note that expired certs and grace periods for expired certificate renewals are no longer able to join this course.
