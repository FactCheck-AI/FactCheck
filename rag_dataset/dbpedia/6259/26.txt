Christine Stückelberger (born 22 May 1947) is a Swiss retired equestrian who won an individual gold medal in dressage at the 1976 Summer Olympics. She was the first, and along with ski jumper Simon Ammann, only Swiss sportsperson to compete at six Olympics: 1972, 1976, 1984, 1988, 1996, and 2000.

Birgit Wientzek Pläge (born 5 May 1971) is a Swiss dressage rider and trainer. She competed at the World Equestrian Games in Tryon 2018 and at the European Championships in 2003, 2015 and 2019 and qualified for the Olympic Games in Tokyo as an individual rider for Switzerland. Her husband Christian Pläge competed at the 2004 Olympic Games in Athens.
