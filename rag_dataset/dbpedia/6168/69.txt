Brenna Duke is the primary administrator working with NCAA Bylaws 14 and 15 for all sport programs.

Duke worked as an academic advisor for four years prior to beginning her role for NAU Athletics as Eligibility Coordinator. She was promoted to Director of Eligibility in January 2020, then to Assistant Athletic Director of Student-Athlete Services (Eligibility & Scholarships) in July 2023. She was nominated in 2022 by NAU Athletics to participate in the second-ever cohort of the Big Sky U professional development program for aspiring ADs and SWAs. She was nominated for the “Guiding Axe” award for student-athletes by SAAC in 2023.

Duke has lived in many college towns including Eugene, Ore., Chapel Hill, N.C., Bloomington, Ind., Athens, Ohio and is now settled in Flagstaff.

Hailing from North Carolina originally, Duke attended the University of North Carolina at Chapel Hill for her undergraduate degree, and earned her master’s degree in Sport Psychology at the University of Denver.

She is active with the broader NAU Community, serving on the University Budget Advisory Board, the Staff Advisory Council as a voting Councilmember, and the Commission on the Status of Women.
