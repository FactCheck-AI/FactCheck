Biography

Elias's birth was registered in the Jul-Aug-Sep quarter of 1872 in the Bristol district.[1] He was the son of John Leach and his wife Elizabeth Leaf .

Elias Leach married Elsie Maria Kingdon in 1898 and their marriage was registered in Bristol, England.[2]

Their children included:

John William Elias Leach, born 19 Feb 1899[3]. and christened 5 Mar 1899 in Bristol, England.[4]

Archibald Alec (Leach) Grant (1904-1986) who became the famous actor Cary Grant.

The 1911 UK Census shows Elias is a 38 year old Tailor's Presser, living with his wife Elsie Marie, his son Archibald, and 3 nieces, in Bishopston, Bristol. Elsie reports that her 13 year marriage to Elias has produced 2 children, 1 of whom is still alive. [5]

He died in 1935 at the age of 63.[6]
