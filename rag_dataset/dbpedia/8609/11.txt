Maria Constantina Cocea was a famous Romanian film and theater actress .She was born on November 27, 1912 in Bucuresti, Romania. She graduated from Institute of Theatre in Paris.In 1934, she plays first role in the movie La jeune fille d'une nuit.Other notable roles in films : Night in May (1934) and Neamul Soimarestilor (1965). Play on the stage of the theater ''National '' in Bucuresti. She was married to Ion Tobosaru. She died on October 28, 2008 in Bucharest.
