Skogn, Norway

Norske Skog Skogn is the largest producer of newsprint in Norway. It was founded in 1962 and the first newsprint machine started its production in 1966. Norske Skog Skogn is one of the largest newsprint mills in Europe. Serving demanding customers worldwide, the mill has its own port facilities, and operates a twice-a-week regular line to the UK and Continental Europe.
