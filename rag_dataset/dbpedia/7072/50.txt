Cheryl Henson is a puppet builder and philanthropist who serves as the president of the Jim Henson Foundation and a board member of The Jim Henson Company. Cheryl began her puppet-building career on “The Muppet Show” alongside her father, the legendary puppeteer Jim Henson. You can now see Cheryl in the new Ron Howard documentary about her father, “Jim Henson: Idea Man,” streaming now on Disney+.
