Lynchburg, VA, USA

Latitude and longitude coordinates are: 37.412762, -79.146042.

Lynchburg is a city in central Virginia, situated near the James River, in the highlands of the Blue Ridge Mountains. The city has the population close to 85,000 people, with the population of the metropolitan zone close to 275,000 people. Lynchburg is a center of business and health care, with a few large hospitals and clinics located there. There is a university and a large Academy of Arts in the city. Lynchburg is a small tourist destination for those who are interested in history and music.
