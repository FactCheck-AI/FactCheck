60,000 irregular cigarettes of foreign origin seized

06/11/2022 El Nuevo Diario (República Dominicana)

They seize 60,000 irregular cigarettes of foreign origin EL NUEVO DIARIO, DAJABÓN- Members of the Specialized Land Border Security Corps (CESFronT), seized this Friday in Dajabón 60,000 units of irregular cigarettes of foreign origin. This reportedly occurred when smugglers introduced cigarettes into Dominican territory by the La Vigía community, belonging to the municipal district of Cañongo in Dajabón. They were distributed in 12 boxes of the aforementioned Point brand cigarettes. The operation was carried out by CESFRONT members assigned to service at the Border Operations Base "Batalla de Sabana Larga" in the aforementioned border province.
