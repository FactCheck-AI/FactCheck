Cingireddy Narayana Reddy (CiNaRe)

The world of Telugu language and literature has lost you. The void caused by your demise can never be filled.

Professor, Poet, Writer, Lyricist, Jnanapith awardee and former member of Rajya Sabha

(b.29 July 1931 Hanumajipet,Rajanna Sircilla district erstwhile Karimnagar district – d. 12 June 2017 Hyderabad )

Nee madhi challaga…swamyee nidura po devuni needalo….vedana marichi po…

నీ మది చల్లగా స్వామి నిదురపో… (Nee madhi challaga.swamyee nidurapo..)

దేవుని నీడలొ వేదన మరచిపో… (Devuni needalo vedana marachipo..)

ever heard this beautiful song oozing with telugu’ness ..most of you probably did many many times. These lyrics are written by none other than Gnanpeeth Award winner Dr. Carre Narayana Reddy.He had nearly 3,500 film songs to his credit.

popularly known as Cinare was an Indian Telugu and Urdu poet and writer.C Narayana Reddy did his MA and PhD in Modern Traditions of Telugu from Osmania University in Hyderabad and also served as a professor there. He also served as the Vice-Chancellor of Telugu University.

He entered the film industry in 1962 by writing all the songs for the movie Gulebakavali Katha. His song Nannu dochu unduvate…vannela dorasani gained immense popularity.His magnum opus Vishwambhara, which was translated into several other Indian languages, fetched him Jnanpith award in 1988.
