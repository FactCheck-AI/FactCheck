Known for his witty and humorous role as Sahil in the hugely popular and love comedy show Sarabhai v/s Sarabhai, actor Sumeet Raghavan will soon be seen in another popular show. Titled ‘Wagle Ki Duniya- Nayi Peedhi Naye Kissey’ is a remake of the hit 1988 show. The actor shared the promo of the show on social media where he seen sanitizing his hands, wearing gloves and a mask, going by today’s pandemic situation.

The show is aiming at brining some comic relief and cheerfulness in the stressful and depressing times of the Covid-19. Sumeet has also done character roles in several movies including the Akshay Kumar starrer ‘Holiday: A Soldier Is Never Off Duty’.
