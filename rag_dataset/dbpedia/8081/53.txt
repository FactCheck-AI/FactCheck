In this beloved action thriller directed by Alfred Hitchcock, advertising executive Roger Thornhill (Cary Grant) finds himself in a case of mistaken identity, as members of a strange organisation mistakenly identify him as a man by the name of George Kaplan. With his options limited, he flees across the country pursued by enemies of the government who are convinced that he is a secret agent.

He finds a friend in Eve Kendall (Eve Marie Saint), who helps to conceal him during a perilous train journey, but he soon discovers that she is not all she seems...
