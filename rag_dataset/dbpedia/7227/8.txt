Apple Park, Cupertino, CA, USA

Latitude and longitude coordinates are: 37.334606, -122.009102.

Apple Park is the headquarters of Apple Inc., located in Cupertino, California, United States, and about 2 miles northeast of the Apple Campus. The building was designed by British architect Norman Foster, constructed in 2014 in a Neo-futurism style, and opened in April 2017. It has 4 floors and a floor area of 2,820,000 sq ft (262,000 m2), houses more than 12,000 employees. About 80 percent of the park consists of trees and plants which are indigenous to the area. It features seven cafes, an artificial pond, a fitness center, a hiking area, and an auditorium. Apple Park is one of the most energy-efficient buildings in the world and is powered entirely by renewable energy. The solar panels of the campus are also among the largest solar roofs worldwide. Steve Jobs Theater hosts the annual September Apple event which introduces new iPhones and other Apple products to the customers.
