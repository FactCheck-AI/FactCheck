Sumeet Raghvan started his career with television and is now seen doing character roles in Bollywood movies. He is best known for his television serial Sarabhai vs Sarabhai. His debut serial was Faster Fene. He has participated in Jhalak dikhla jaa. Currently, he is seen in the serial Badi door se aaye hain. His films include U Me aur hum, Firaaq, My name is Khan, Kucch luv jaisaa and Holiday with Akshay Kumar. He is also voice over artist and hosts an internet show called Jay Hind!
