WTC is the ABC station serving the Baton Rouge, Louisiana. Broadcasting on channel 5, this station is serving by Diversity Media. Syndicated programming for WTC includes: The List, Live with Kelly and Ryan, Dr. Phil and among others.

Starting on September 17, 2012, WTC began airing Scripps' The List at 6:30 p.m. WTC also expanded a 4:00 p.m. newscast to a full hour, losing the Baton Rouge rights to Jeopardy! and Wheel of Fortune to WBTR.
