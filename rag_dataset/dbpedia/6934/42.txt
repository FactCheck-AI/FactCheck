Hattingen is a city in Germany located in the North Rhine-Westphalia state, near Essen. Hattingen, nestled in the Ruhr area, is a town with a blend of historical charm and outdoor attractions. Known for its medieval architecture and cultural events, it offers a picturesque living environment. The Henrichshütte is an industrial museum showcasing the region’s steel history. The Altstadt (Old Town) features well-preserved half-timbered houses. Hattingen’s proximity to the Ruhr River adds to its scenic appeal.
