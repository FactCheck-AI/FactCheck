The limited edition John Addison Engineer is a classic pattern named after the brand’s founder and is crafted with waxed leathers from Chicago’s Horween tannery. Featuring handmade Goodyear Welt construction and Vibram sole plate for added traction and oil resistant properties. This 8” boot features leather lining, a padded leather foot bed and double buckle closures for a secure fit. Enjoy learning more about our 160th anniversary with an individually numbered card with information on our history and the heritage of this boot, included with the purchase.
