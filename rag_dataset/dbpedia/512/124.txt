Uzhhorod, Zakarpattia Region, Ukraine

Latitude and longitude coordinates are: 48.621025, 22.288229.

Situated on the river Uzh, Uzhhorod is the capital city of Zakarpattia Region, which is one of the westernmost parts of Ukraine. Historically, this part of the country used to belong to other nations, including Polish, Hungarian, Czech, Romanian, and others. Situated at the foot of the Carpathian Mountains, the city is a transportation and a commercial spot, with the population close to 115,000 people. There are many things to see in Uzhhorod, including some beautiful architecture in the city center.
