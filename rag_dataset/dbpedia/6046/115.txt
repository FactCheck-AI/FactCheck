China Sinopharm International (Shanghai) Co., Ltd. is a comprehensive commercial service enterprise centering international pharmaceutical business. It is a wholly-owned subsidiary under China Sinopharm International Corporation and is affiliated to China National Pharmaceutical Group Corporation (referred to as "Sinopharm Group"), a Fortune Global 500.
