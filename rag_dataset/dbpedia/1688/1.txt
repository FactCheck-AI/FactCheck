2002/03 was Manchester City's 101st season of competitive football - its 74th in the top division of English football and its 6th in the English Premier League. The leading goalscorer this season was Nicolas Anelka who scored 14 goals from just 41 appearances. Nicolas Anelka also made the most appearances for the club this season with 41 games played. The club had 13 different goalscorers and used 26 different players including 10 debutants. Debutants this season were David Sommeil, Djamel Belmadi, Joey Barton, Marc Vivien-Foe, Mikkel Bischoff, Nicolas Anelka, Peter Schmeichel, Robbie Fowler, Stephen Jordan and Sylvain Distin.
