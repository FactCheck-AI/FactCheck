A senior member of Germany’s nationalist party who opposed Muslim immigration said that he had converted to Islam because of the “moral decline” of the Protestant church.

Arthur Wagner, 48, who had been active in the church community and sat on the Alternative for Germany (AfD) legislative board in Brandenburg, said that he had changed his name to Ahmad in a mosque ceremony.

He said he abandoned Christianity because of the church’s support for same-sex marriage and the sight of pastors attending gay pride celebrations with children.
