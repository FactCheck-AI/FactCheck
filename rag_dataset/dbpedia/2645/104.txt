Tigmanshu Dhulia dons many hats – actor, director, screenplay writer and casting director. His acting talent was seen in Gangs of Wasseypur, while his direction was applauded for Paan Singh Tomar and Sahib Biwi aur Gangster. The former won the National Award for best feature film in 2012. Dhulia hails from Uttarakhand, while his education was completed at Allahabad and New Delhi at the National School of Drama. He was the casting director for Shekhar Kapur’s Bandit Queen. He has also worked on many TV series including Star Bestsellers. His first feature film as a director was Haasil and the most recent one was the Kangana Ranaut starrer Revolver Rani.
