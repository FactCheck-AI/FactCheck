Filmmaker Tigmanshu Dhulia is known to be multifaceted. The director has acted in many films and even in a TV show. Now, Dhulia will feature in Ekta Kapoor produced television show ‘Kuch Toh Hai Tere Mere Darmiyaan’ and will play the role of a filmmaker. To be seen this month in the new serial, Tigmanshu was last visible in ‘Hero’ on the silver screen. The director-actor acted as Home Minister Bharat Choudhary in his last small screen outing in TV series ‘Yudh’ featuring Amitabh Bachchan in the lead role.
