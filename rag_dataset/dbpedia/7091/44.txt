The Clock

Judy Garland, Robert Walker, James Gleason (starring)

Vincente Minnelli (director)

Robert Nathan, Joseph Schrank (screenwriters)

Beverly Hills, CA: Metro-Goldwyn-Mayer [MGM], 1945. Four vintage oversize borderless reference photographs from the 1945 film. Printed mimeo snipes affixed to the versos.

A soldier on 48-hour leave spends the day with a woman he meets in Pennsylvania Station, gradually falling in love with the woman. Judy Garland's first dramatic role, and her first starring film performance in which she did not sing.

13 x 10 inches. About Near Fine.
