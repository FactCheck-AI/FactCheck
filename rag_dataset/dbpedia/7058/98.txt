Gina Torres is an American actress born on April 25, 1969 in New York City, NY. She is best known for her roles as Cleopatra in Xena: Warrior Princess, Jessica Pearson in Suits, and Zoe Washburne in Firefly and its 2005 film sequel Serenity.
