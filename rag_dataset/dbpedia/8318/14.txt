Mini Bio (1) Alexandra Chando was born on July 28, 1986 in Pennsylvania, USA. She is an actress, known for As the World Turns (1956), The Lying Game (2011) and The Talent Show (2011).

Trivia (10)

edit

She had big hopes of becoming an actress, before she had signed on to As the World Turns (1956) in 2006.

Was featured on MTV'S True Life: I'm Getting My Big Break (2006).

In her spare time she enjoys singing, water sports, cooking and yoga.

Native of Pennsylvania.

Her roommates are Emme Rylan and Mandy Bruno Bogue, from sister-soap, Guiding Light (1952).

Currently playing "Maddie Coleman" on As the World Turns (1956). [July 2005]

She is still doing a few episodes of As the World Turns (1956). [October 2009]

Currently playing twin sisters on ABC Family's The Lying Game (2011). [October 2010]

Is best friends with actor Martin Spanjers.

Auditioned for the role of Elena Gilbert/Katherine Pierce in The Vampire Diaries (2009), which went to Nina Dobrev.
