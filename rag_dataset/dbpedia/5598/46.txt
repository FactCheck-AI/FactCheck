Information

Kirarin☆Revolution Song Selection 4 is the fourth mini-album released with songs from the anime series Kirarin☆Revolution. It was released in two formats, limited CD+DVD and regular CD only. It reached #78 on Oricon charts and charted for 2 weeks.
