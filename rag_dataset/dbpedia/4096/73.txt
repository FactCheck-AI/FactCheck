About this Item

Offered is an original Christmas card from Harry Joe Brown (1890-1972). Printed on creme parchment paper, the front cover shows an illustration of a candelabra against the backdrop of a window (in gold, green and yellow); the inside message is machine-printed and says "-for a joyous XMas and happiness in 1933 - Harry Joe Brown." Harry Joe Brown was an American film producer and director who was involved in the production of several western films in the 1950s through a company he created with actor Randolph Scott; he is also known for producing the American debut films of both Errol Flynn in Captain Blood and Sean Flynn in the Son of Captain Blood. Mr. Brown was married to actress Sally Eilers. Seller Inventory # 010252
