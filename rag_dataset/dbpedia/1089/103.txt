Sandra Martinez, 54, passed away on January 20, 2016. A memorial service will be held on Friday, January 29, 2016, at 10:00 AM at Bobbitt Memorial Chapel. Burial will follow immediately at Mt. View Cemetery both in San Bernardino. Sandy was born and raised in San Bernardino CA, graduated from Pacific High School in 1979 and continued her education at SBVC. Sandy is survived by her sister Sylvia and Arthur “Tudie” Garcia and brother Efren “Butch” Martinez. Sandy is preceded in death by her parents Efren and Helen Martinez, her sisters, Eleanor Negrete and Lucille Olivares.
