Double Dragon is a 1987 beat 'em up developed by Technos Japan. The game is a spiritual and technological successor to Technos' earlier beat 'em up Renegade, but introduced several additions such as two-player cooperative gameplay and the ability to arm oneself with an enemy's weapon after disarming them. The player takes control of martial artist Billy Lee, or his twin brother Jimmy as they fight their way into the turf of the Black Warriors gang in order to rescue their common love interest Marian...
