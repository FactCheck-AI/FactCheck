Biography

Whiskey-voiced Becky Hobbs was born and raised in Oklahoma. Her songs have been recorded by George Jones, Conway Twitty, Loretta Lynn, Glen Campbell, Emmylou Harris and Alabama. She co-wrote the powerful evergreen, ANGELS AMONG US. The "Beckaroo" pounds the piano like The Killer, yet she can melt your heart with her ballads. Her new album pays tribute to her 5th-great grandmother, Nancy Ward, Beloved Woman of the Cherokee. The 17 songs will be featured in the upcoming musical NANYEHI-BELOVED WOMAN OF THE CHEROKEE.
