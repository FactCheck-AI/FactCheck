Description

The Apple iPad Air (2022) or 5th generation is the first Apple device powered by the next-gen Apple M1 chipset. Other specs include a 10.9-inch Liquid Retina display, 12MP main camera, USB Type-C and 12MP front Selfie camera. The Apple iPad Air (2022) is available for a starting price of $599 (Wi-Fi only model with 64GB storage).
