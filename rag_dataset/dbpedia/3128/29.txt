Julie Sanchez is the Marketing Director of the International Surf Lifesaving Association (ISLA).

Sanchez holds a B.A. in Marketing Management from California State Polytechnic University, Pomona, and has 10+ years experience in the advertising industry. She is currently working at a top U.S Hispanic advertising agency, managing top brands in development and execution of award-winning national marketing campaigns. She has seen ISLA grow from a small non-profit, to an international organization with partners and supporters worldwide. She has traveled with ISLA to several international trips as a volunteer and also serves as a translator for teams in Latin America. Her passions outside of helping others include soccer, sun and any kind of outdoor adventures with family and friends. The ISLA is an international organization that advances professional lifesaving development to areas in need around the globe. ISLA aids people in championing the aquatic safety situation in their own coastal communities. This is accomplished through lifeguard training programs, lifeguard exchanges, equipment donations, purchasing connections, and utilizing the latest technology to sustain a global network of lifeguards that share information, techniques, stories, and culture.
