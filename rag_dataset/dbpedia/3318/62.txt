Charles Junior Adams

Charles Junior Adams, age 85, of Buckhannon, WV, died Saturday, August 26, 2023, at Mansfield Place in Philippi, WV. He was born May 1, 1938, in Tallmansville, WV, a son of the late Garlton Adams and Ruth Reeder. On January 24, 1964, he married Juanita Campbell who survives. He is also survived by one daughter, Anita White and husband Rick of Buckhannon, WV; one son, Brent Adams and wife Melissa of Burlington, Ky; two grandsons, Ethan White and wife Carolyn of Cockeysville, MD and Jacob Adams of Burlington and one granddaughter, Alexis Adams. Mr. Adams served in the U.S. Army from 1961 to 1963 and worked at Moore Business Forms as a Press Operator. In honoring his wishes, Mr. Adams will be cremated. Online condolences may be made to the family at www.polingstclair.com.
