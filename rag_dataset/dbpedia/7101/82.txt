In the dynamic world of technology, it is not uncommon to come across individuals who have made significant contributions to the industry. One such individual is Suresh Sambandam, the founder and CEO of OrangeScape, a global top 10 company in the Google cloud ecosystem. Sambandam’s journey in the tech industry is a testament to his visionary leadership and relentless pursuit of innovation. This article delves into the journey of this visionary CEO in the tech industry.

Early Life and Education

Suresh Sambandam was born and raised in India. He holds a degree in Mechanical Engineering from the College of Engineering, Guindy. His interest in technology was sparked at a young age, and he was always fascinated by the potential of software to solve complex problems.

Professional Journey

Sambandam started his career as a software engineer at Hewlett-Packard. He then moved on to work at Selectica, where he played a key role in building a 0 million company from scratch. Despite his success, Sambandam felt the urge to start his own venture and thus, OrangeScape was born in 2004.

OrangeScape and KiSSFLOW

OrangeScape is a cloud-based platform that allows businesses to develop their own applications. The company has been recognized as a global top 10 company in the Google cloud ecosystem. In 2012, Sambandam launched KiSSFLOW, a workflow automation tool, which has been used by over 10,000 companies worldwide. Under Sambandam’s leadership, OrangeScape and KiSSFLOW have grown exponentially, serving clients like Unilever, Schlumberger, and Dominos.

Leadership Style

Sambandam is known for his visionary leadership style. He believes in the power of innovation and encourages his team to think outside the box. He is also a strong advocate for customer-centricity and believes that understanding customer needs is key to developing successful products.

Awards and Recognition

Over the years, Sambandam has received numerous awards and recognition for his contributions to the tech industry. He was named among the ‘Top 10 Influential People in IT’ by ITWeb. In 2018, he was awarded the ‘Entrepreneur of the Year’ by the Entrepreneurs Council of India.

Conclusion

Suresh Sambandam’s journey in the tech industry is a story of vision, innovation, and leadership. His contributions to the industry have not only transformed the way businesses operate but have also inspired many aspiring entrepreneurs. As he continues to lead OrangeScape and KiSSFLOW towards new horizons, the tech industry eagerly awaits his next big innovation.
