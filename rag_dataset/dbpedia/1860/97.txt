Los Cristianos, Santa Cruz de Tenerife, Spain

Latitude and longitude coordinates are: 28.048906, -16.711597.

Los Cristianos is a small town situated to the south of Santa Cruz de Tenerife island, which is a part of Spain. It is a very popular tourist destination, famous with its beaches like Las Vistas Beach, Playa de Los Cristianos, and others. There are plenty of excellent luxury and cheaper affordable hotels, plenty of restaurants, cafes and bars, various entertainment facilities, especially for children and young people including great and modern water sports equipment.
