Kalka to Shimla by Train

Kalka has a narrow gauge line toward Shimla in North West India. Mostly the route from Kalka to Shimla is hilly; therefore one can enjoy breathtaking views of mountains and surrounding villages. [hana-code-insert name=’rectangle’ /]Many trains travel between Kalka and Shimla that covers the total distance of 90 Kms in round about 5 to 6 hours.

Kalka to Shimla train route

Kalka Shimla railway track is one of the oldest hill railway track built by the British in India. It was built to connect Shimla, the former summer capital of India during British period. Now the capital of Himachal Pradesh. One can enjoy the panoramic views of mountains and villages during their journey from Kalka to Shimla.

Kalka to Shimla train stations

While travelling from Kalka to Shimla by train one has to pass through Dharampur, Solan, Kandaghat, Shoghi, Taradevi, Barog, Salogra, Summerhill and than finally toward your destination Shimla. Enjoy the picturesque views of green lush valleys and majestic mountains.

Tunnels:

Kalka Shimla railway track runs through 103 tunnels. In which Barog tunnel said to be the longest tunnel which is associated with local tales and peoples belief.
