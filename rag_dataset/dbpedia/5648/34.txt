Erlend Øye is a Norwegian musician from Bergen, best known for being part of the indie folk duo Kings of Convenience together with Eirik Glambek Bøe. He has released one solo album, Unrest, in 2003 and a mix-CD in the DJ-Kicks series in 2004.He was born on 21 November 1975 in Bergen and formed the band Skog together with some friends in the mid-1990s. Erlend is also currently working on a new solo album recorded in Iceland, which he plans to release in May.
