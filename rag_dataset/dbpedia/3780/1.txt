The writer and journalist Mr. Mohammed Al Hammadi- Editor in Chief of Al Ittihad Newspaper received Dr. Noor El Deen Atatreh- The Chancellor of Al Ain University, during his visit to Al Ittihad Newspaper which is located at Abu Dhabi Media City. The aim of the visit is to discuss the aspects of cooperation between the two institutions, as a part of combining the theoretical side with the practical side by giving the College of Communication and Media students the opportunity to make the practical training at Al Ittihad Newspaper which is considered as one of the most prestigious newspapers in UAE which providing useful and elegant content by an elite of writers and journalists, which would reflect positively on the media students and enhance their skills, in addition to coordinate the scientific visits for the students to the newspaper.

For his part, Mr. Al Hammadi welcomed Dr. Atatreh praising the university's developments and achievements, he also took him on a tour of the newspaper department explaining to him the mechanism of a journalists work. Dr. Atatreh visited different departments such as Local News department, Sports department, the Archive, and the Smart Room which is characterized as the only one in the region.
