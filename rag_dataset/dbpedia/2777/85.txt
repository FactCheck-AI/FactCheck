Valis: The Fantasm Soldier Collection II is a side-scrolling action video game collection developed and published by Edia.

About

Valis: The Fantasm Soldier Collection II includes Valis IV (PC Engine), Syd of Valis (Genesis), Valis: The Fantasm Soldier (Genesis), and Valis: The Phantasm Soldier (MSX).

The “Sound Mode,” “Visual Mode,” and “Rewind Function” included in the original Valis: The Fantasm Soldier Collection will also return.
