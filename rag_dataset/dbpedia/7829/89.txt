34. What is Cheryl's real name?

The Girls Aloud singer was born on 30th June 1983 as Cheryl Ann Tweedy. The star's name changed to Cheryl Cole after her marriage to footballer Ashley, and then again to Cheryl Fernandez-Versini after her marriage to French restauranteur Jean-Bernard in 2014, before going by just 'Cheryl' after their split.
