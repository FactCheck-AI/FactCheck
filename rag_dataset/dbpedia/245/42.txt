Yorkton Climate Summary

Located at an elevation of 504 meters (1653.54 feet) above sea level, Yorkton has a Humid continental, no dry season, warm summer climate (Classification: Dfb). The district’s yearly temperature is 2.18ºC (35.92ºF) and it is 0.09% higher than Canada’s averages. Yorkton typically receives about 18.37 millimeters (0.72 inches) of precipitation and has 30.55 rainy days (8.37% of the time) annually.
