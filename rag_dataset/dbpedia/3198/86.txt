James Donald “Don” Huber, Jr. was born in Luling, Caldwell County, Texas on February 17, 1947 to James Donald Huber, Sr. and Vickie Hunter Huber. He passed away on September 16, 2018, at St. Peter’s Hospital in Helena, Lewis and Clark County, Montana.

He was preceded in death by his parents. He is survived by his sister Kathleen Huber Bruce (James), two nephews; Harold James Bruce and Michael Wayne Bruce as well as many cousins.

James will be cremated and no services will be held at this time.
