Want to know which films and stars are up for BAFTAs at this year's awards?

With the star-studded ceremony taking place this weekend, all eyes will be on the actors and movies leading the BAFTAs pack.

Hosted by David Tennant, the show will also feature special performances from Sophie Ellis Bextor and Hannah Waddingham.
