Melissa Arnette Elliott (born July 1, 1971) better known as Missy Elliott is an American rapper, singer, songwriter and record producer. She won four Grammy Awards and sell over 30 million records in the United States. She is the best-selling female rapper in Nielsen Music history, according to Billboard in 2017.
