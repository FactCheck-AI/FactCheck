Woodburn I at Eastgate View Place is situated on 7.82 acres within the East Gate Planning District. Located at the southern gateway into Loudoun County along U.S. Highway 50 (John Mosby Highway), it is within close proximity to Washington Dulles International Airport and major residential communities. Its location provides an unobstructed view from Route 50.

This site was planned and rezoned for three (3) Class A 2-story office buildings totaling 101,000 sf.

Building A - 25,000 sf

Building B - 38,000 sf
