Where is Woodburn, Kentucky ?

Woodburn lies in Warren County. Geographically, it is situated in the southern part of Kentucky, USA. It is located south of Bowling Green, an important city in the region. Louisville, another major city, is northeast. The city is part of the south-central segment of the state, close to the Tennessee border.
