History

With a history of over 85 years Newsweek is one of the world’s most well respected and globally recognised media brands. It provides an indispensable guide to politics, business and individual pursuits to an affluent and educated global audience.Newsweek was founded in 1933 as one of the first American news magazines; it has since grown to become one of the most iconic brands in international media.

Content

Newsweek stands out through the quality of its writing and its global coverage. The International Investor's features on the world’s most dynamic economies follow that same style: by conducting face to face interviews and reporting first-hand the comments and aspirations of local businessmen we are able to recount the realities of the local political and business environment.
