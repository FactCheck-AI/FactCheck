The Market Common district is popular among homebuyers as this community is one of the newer areas of Myrtle Beach and boasts a variety of new neighborhoods with beautiful homes.

The Market Common falls right in between U.S. 17 Business and U.S. 17 Bypass, providing fast and easy access to the city of Myrtle Beach, as well as other areas like Socastee, Surfside, Murrells Inlet, and Pawleys Island.

Those living in The Market Common will love the area’s shopping district, which features national chains like Barnes & Noble, Victoria’s Secret, Bath & Body Works, and more, as well as local boutiques, candy shops, and cafes. There is also a movie theater here and tons of restaurants to choose from. Also nearby are grocery stores, doctors’ offices, and gas stations.

The Market Common community is made up of quite a few neighborhoods that anyone would love to live in. From townhomes to single-family homes and condominiums, there is something for every homebuyer here (note: price ranges for each neighborhood differ).
