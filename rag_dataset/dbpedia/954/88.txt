Giovanni Battista Piranesi was an Italian Old Masters artist who was born in 1720. His work is currently being shown at multiple venues like Minneapolis Institute of Art. Numerous key galleries and museums such as MASI Lugano, LAC have featured Giovanni Battista Piranesi's work in the past.Giovanni Battista Piranesi's work has been offered at auction multiple times, with realized prices ranging from 10 USD to 358,000 USD, depending on the size and medium of the artwork. Since 1998 the record price for this artist at auction is 358,000 USD for An architectural fantasy: the entrance to a palace with a monumental staircase by a fountain, sold at Christie's New York in 2001. Giovanni Battista Piranesi has been featured in articles for ArtDaily, The Art Newspaper and The Magazine Antiques . The most recent article is 7 Things You Should Know About Giovanni Battista Piranesi written for Daily Art Magazine in April 2024. The artist died in 1778.
