Ben Osborn announced as the new president of the Association of the British Pharmaceutical Industry

The Association of the British Pharmaceutical Industry has announced that its new president is Ben Osborn, the UK country manager of American pharmaceutical giant Pfizer.

He takes over from outgoing president Haseeb Ahmad and will lead on overseeing the ABPI, its board and its code of practice, which is administered by the Prescription Medicines Code of Practice Authority.
