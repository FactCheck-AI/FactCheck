The Guinean Franc is the currency in Guinea (GN, GIN). The Central African CFA is the currency in Cameroon (CM, CMR), Central African Republic (CF, CAF), Chad (TD, TCD), Congo (CG, COG), Equatorial Guinea (GQ, GNQ), and Gabon (GA, GAB). The Guinean Franc is also known as Franc Guineen. The Central African CFA is also known as Communaute Financiere Africaine BEAC Francs. The symbol for GNF can be written FG. The symbol for XAF can be written CFAF. The Central African CFA is divided into 100 centimes. The exchange rate for the Guinean Franc was last updated on June 5, 2023 from MSN.
