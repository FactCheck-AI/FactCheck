Welcome to the American University Washington College of Law Campus!

Located 1 block from the AU-Tenleytown Metro Station, and featuring 5 courtrooms, light-filled classrooms, and state-of-the-art technology, our 8.5 acre, LEED Gold certified law campus is the perfect location to complete your legal education! Our location in Northwest, Washington, DC provides direct access to the heart of the nation’s capital, allowing students to complete hundreds of externships and internships each year and making our campus a hub for top speakers and high-level events. At American University Washington College of Law, we strive to Champion What Matters.

LEED Gold Campus

American University Washington College of Law’s spectacular campus received its LEED Gold certification in spring 2017. The design of the campus places a high priority on environmentally sustainable development principles, while also creating a functional, dynamic, and healthy space to house the school’s wide variety of programs. Some stand-out green features include a campus with 240,000 square feet of green space, space for 200 bikes, and energy efficient systems that reduce energy consumption by 20%

At American University Washington College of Law, students, faculty, and staff join a community committed to inclusive excellence and their well-being.
