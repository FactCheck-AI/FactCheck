Who is Ilkay Gündogan?

Ilkay Gündogan is 33-years-old, born on 24 October 1990, and is a both-footed professional football player from Germany. Born in Gelsenkirchen, Ilkay Gündogan has also played in FA Cup for Man City, in Bundesliga for Dortmund and in Bundesliga for Nürnberg. He currently plays as a Midfielder, Attacking Midfielder (Centre) in Primera Division for club Barcelona.
