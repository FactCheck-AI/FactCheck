The Recording Industry Association of Japan announced that BTS’s “Film Out” and “Lights” were certified platinum with over 100M accumulated streams. The Japanese version of “Spring Day” was certified gold with over 50M streams.

They also previously received platinum certification for “Butter” and “Permission To Dance”. In particular, “Butter” received platinum certification just 72 days after its release, setting the record for the shortest time in history to receive a platinum certification. BTS now has eight songs that are certified platinum.
