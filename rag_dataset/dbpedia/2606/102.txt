Ron O'Donnell is a British Postwar & Contemporary artist who was born in 1952. Numerous key galleries and museums such as City Art Centre have featured Ron O'Donnell's work in the past.Ron O'Donnell's work has been offered at auction multiple times. Among the artist's sold works is "BONJOUR MONSIEUR BYRNE", which realized 45 USD at McTear's in 2024

The exhibition Documentary Genealogies. Photography 1848-1917 explores, throughout seven rooms, the historical background of documentary photography between the revolutions of 1848 and 1917.
