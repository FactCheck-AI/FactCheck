Originally released in 1960, 'Ella Wishes You a Swinging Christmas' is one of the greatest Christmas albums ever made, and it never ages. Ella, along with a studio orchestra conducted by Frank DeVol, puts her unique stamp on popular Christmas songs including 'Jingle Bells', 'Santa Claus Is Coming to Town', 'Have Yourself a Merry Little Christmas' and many more (12 tracks in total).
