Mr. Edwin Lavelle Smith, 83, of Waynesboro, MS passed from this life Saturday, December 14, 2019 at Wayne General Hospital, MS. He was born in Waynesboro on Tuesday, November 24, 1936 to Edwin O. Smith and Mattie C. Smith. He was a Cattle Farmer. Mr. Lavelle is preceded in death by his father, Edwin O. Smith and mother, Mattie C. Smith. He is survived by his wife, Virginia Smith, Waynesboro, MS; 3 Step Children and 2 Step Grandchildren; brother, Willis G. Smith, Waynesboro, MS; 2 sisters, Irma Jean Chapman, Lucille Lindsey, both of Waynesboro, MS. Visitation for Mr. Lavelle will be held from 11:00 AM to 1:30 PM, Tuesday, December 17, 2019 at Freeman Funeral Home, 1305 Skyland Drive, Waynesboro, MS. A Graveside Service will be held at 2:00 PM Tuesday, December 17, 2019 at Corinth Cemetery, 2426 Camp Eight Road with Rev. Jason Merritt and Rev. Norman Nowell officiating.
