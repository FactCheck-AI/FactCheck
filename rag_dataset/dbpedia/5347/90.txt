Chelsea Clinton, the daughter of former U.S. President Bill Clinton and former U.S. Secretary of State Hillary Clinton, is the vice chair of the Clinton Foundation. She previously served as a special correspondent for NBC News and worked at McKinsey & Company and Avenue Capital Group. Additionally, Clinton is the author of three books and teaches at Columbia University's Mailman School of Public Health. Her next book, “She Persisted in Sports: American Olympians Who Changed the Game,” will be available in September 2020.
