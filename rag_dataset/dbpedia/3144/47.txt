As an HMC (the Headmasters' & Headmistresses' Conference) School, Oswestry School is part of a thriving and proactive association of leading Heads. HMC is a kite mark of global quality, innovation and excellence in education. Standards in HMC independent schools are exceptionally high and parents can be assured that they are choosing the very best education for their child. Published examination results, league tables in national newspapers and ISI (Independent School Inspectorate) reports all confirm the quality of education on offer. 95% of HMC school pupils go on to higher education.

All HMC member schools are regularly inspected and accredited against both qualitative and quantitative membership criteria in order to ensure that the HMC kite mark continues to exemplify quality, innovation and excellence in education.
