Biography

2015 Cross Country: Posted a season-best time of 29:37.8 at the JSU Foothills Invite...Finished in 29:45.4 at the Alabama Crimson Classic in he 8.042K...

2015 Track and Field: Ran the 3000m Steeplechase in 10:18.19 at the Georgia Invite...Completed the 3000m at the UT Challenge in 9:48.20...Finished the Mile in 4:43.00 at the SIU Invite...Posted a time of 2:00.80 in the 800m at the Battle of the Boulevard...

2014 Cross Country: Ran a time of 17:32.7 in the 5K at the Belmont Opener...In the 8K at the Greater Louisville Classic, finished with a time of 27:27.4...Placed 28th at the Frontrunner Invite with a mark of 17:51.5...

2014 Track and Field: Ran a personal-best 800 meters (2:01.55) at the Southern Miss Cupid Classic… Claimed personal bests in the mile (4:41.08), 3000 meters (9:53.59), and the 3000-meter steeplechase (10:13.90).

High School: Ran for Steve Prince at Bearden High School… Was a four-year letter winner in cross country and track and field… Qualified for the State Cross Country Championships four times… PR’s: 2:02 800m, 4:50 1600m, 6:35 2k Steeple, 17:15 5k

Personal: Son of Karen and Roland Shankles… He has five brothers (Charlie, Steve, Andrew, Peter, and David) and one sister (Molly)... Plans to major in electrical engineering.
