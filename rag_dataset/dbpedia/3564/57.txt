24 km from Olbia Harbour, B&B La Chintina is a recently renovated property situated in Sant Antonio Di Gallura and features air-conditioned rooms with free WiFi and private parking. It is located 34 km from Isola di Tavolara and provides a 24-hour front desk. The bed and breakfast has family rooms as well as facilities for disabled guests. Overlooking the mountain, units in this bed and breakfast are accessible via private entrance and equipped with a flat-screen TV and a private bathroom with a walk-in shower and a hair dryer. A patio with garden views is offered in every unit. At the bed and breakfast, every unit comes with bed linen and towels. A minimarket is available at the bed and breakfast. Guests can also relax in the garden. San Simplicio Church is 17 km from the bed and breakfast, while Church of St. Paul the Apostle is 17 km from the property. The nearest airport is Olbia Costa Smeralda Airport, 22 km from B&B La Chintina.
