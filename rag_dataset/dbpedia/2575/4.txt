Kevin Dale Brown

53 of Plainfield, Indiana, passed away on November 25, 2022. Kevin was born on June 6, 1969, in Danville, Illinois to the late Orville Lee and Darlene Brown. He was a retired 1st Sargent in the U.S. Army. He was a member of Second Chance Church the American Legion and a life member of Combat Veterans Motorcycle Association. He loved his wife, family and riding his Harley. He was preceded in death by his son, Ty Sawyer Lee Brown and his parents. He is survived by his wife, Deanna Brown; children, Sarah, Haley, Alexis and Eryn Pearce; granddaughter, Laila Rogers; sister, Cathy Sledge Kieta and several nieces and nephews. Visitation will be Friday, December 9, 2022, from 4 pm to 9 pm at Second Chance Church, Plainfield, IN. Funeral service will be Saturday, December 10, 2022, at 11 am with additional visitation from 10 am to 11 am at Second Chance Church. Burial will follow at Floral Park Cemetery.
