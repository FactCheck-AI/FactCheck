Easton Corbin is in the midst of a busy summer. The Florida native is spending much of his time on the road performing at fairs and festivals across the country in support of his latest release, “Somebody’s Gotta Be Country.” Additionally, he has spent quite a bit of time in the studio working on new music.

Recently, while he was at home in Nashville, Corbin took time to complete the Country Now Pop Quiz, revealing several fun facts about himself.
