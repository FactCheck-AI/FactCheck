Duke of Shrewsbury was a title in the Peerage of England, It was created for Charles Talbot, 12th Earl of Shrewsbury.

The title became extinct upon the death of his grandson, George Talbot, who had no surviving sons. He was succeeded by his kinsman George Talbot in the earldoms of Shrewsbury and Waterford.
