Barbara Joan "Barbra" Streisand (born April 24, 1942) is an American singer, actress and director. With a career spanning over six decades, she has achieved success in multiple fields of entertainment. She is also recipient of the EGOT status for being awarded an Emmy, Grammy, Oscar, and Tony.
