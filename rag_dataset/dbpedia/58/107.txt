WCBC Radio is a trusted local radio station in Cumberland, MD, providing up-to-date news, sports, weather, and community events. With a strong focus on delivering accurate and timely information, WCBC keeps listeners informed and engaged with their comprehensive coverage of local news stories, including recent reports on a state police crash and criminal summons served in the area.

As a reliable source of information, WCBC Radio also highlights noteworthy events and achievements in the community, such as Allegany College of Maryland being recognized as a Voter-Friendly Campus and the Appalachian Regional Commission accepting applications for its POWER Initiative. With their commitment to keeping the public informed, WCBC Radio is a go-to station for residents of Cumberland and the surrounding areas.
