The Maryland Sports Radio Network boasts one of the strongest coverage areas in collegiate sports by providing coverage throughout Maryland, Washington, D.C., and Northern Virginia and reaches into West Virginia, Pennsylvania and Delaware. Maryland Sports Properties, a division of PlayFly Sports, manages the Maryland Sports Radio Network on behalf of the department of athletics.
