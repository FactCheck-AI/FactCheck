More than 60 speakers have now been confirmed for NEM Dubrovnik 2023, which will take place from at the Dubrovnik Palace Hotel from June 5 -8.

They include representatives of leading global and European companies such as A1 Croatia, CME, Czech Television, Deutsche Telekom, European Broadcasting Union (EBU), Croatian Radiotelevision (HRT), Mediawan, Nova TV, Sky EU Office, Prva TV and B92, RTL Croatia, Telekom Srbija, Viaplay Group.

Among others are:

• Branko Cakarmis, General Director, Pro Plus

• Sinisa Duranovic, Member of the Management Board & CCO, Croatian Telecom

• Adrian Jezina, CEO, Telemach Croatia and Slovenia

• Anna Lenerova, Group Head of Content, O2 Czech Republic (member Of PPF Group)

• Gregoire Polad, Director General, Association Of Commercial Television And Video On Demand Services In Europe

• Pavel Stantchev, TV2 Media Group and Planet TV

• Marcin Skabara, Programming Strategy Director, TVP (Telewizja Polska S.A.)

• Peter Wassong, Lead TV Content Europe, Deutsche Telekom AG

Dimitris Michalakis, Cosmote TV Executive Director at Hellenic Telecommunications Organisation, has joined the list of keynote speakers, making a total of 12 confirmed.
