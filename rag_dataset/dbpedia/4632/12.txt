MUMBAI: Telly actress Sanaya Irani is a prominent Indian television, movie and web series actress who has left behind quite a long and prominent trail of hers in the showbiz industry.

Irani hails from a Mumbai-based Parsi household and had harnessed seven years of her former education from an Ooty-based boarding school. Following this, she graduated from the Sydenham College of Commerce and Economics, Mumbai and was further pursuing an MBA degree before entering into the glamour world for modelling.

Sanaya's first modelling portfolio was done by the renowned Bollywood photographer-turned actor - Boman Irani.

Irani made her acting debut in Bollywood with her 'accidental' cameo role in the famous Yash Raj Film's movie Fanaa (2006), that starred Aamir Khan and Kajol as the lead actors, while she played the supporting role of Mehbooba.

The actress also spent her initial years of showbiz life by doing TV commercials with Bollywood stars like Shahrukh Khan, Kareena Kapoor and other Bollywood stars before entering television.

For the unversed, she made her MV debut with Jagjit Singh's classic - 'Tumko Dekha'.

Sanaya debuted on the small-screen with the SAB TV drama series - Left Right Left in 2007. After this, there was no stopping Sanaya from claiming immense fame and leaving a prominent mark as a telly actress in the industry by bagging important roles for magnus TV shows, one after the other.

She has also participated in many reality shows like Nachle Ve with Saroj Khan, Meethi Choori No.1, Welcome - Baazi Mehmaan-Nawaazi Ki, Jhalak Dikhhla Jaa 8, Aaj Ki Raat Hai Zindagi, Box Cricket League 2, Nach Baliye 8 (contested with husband Mohit Sehgal), Kitchen Champion 5 and Khatra Khatra Khatra and has claimed prominent positions in most of them.
