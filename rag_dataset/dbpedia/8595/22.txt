The "Team of the FA Cup" is made up of the winners of the competition's "Player of the Round" award from the first qualifying round up to the semi-finals.

Five players are shortlisted in each round, with the fans then choosing the winner via a vote on the FA's website.

The full team for 2009/10 is: Bobby Traynor, Mark Danks, Adam Webster, Danny Kedwell, Richard Brodie, Leon Legge, Jermaine Beckford (twice), Gareth Bale, Frederic Piquionne, Didier Drogba.

SEMI-FINALS

Didier Drogba (Chelsea) - scored in the 3-0 win against Aston Villa, his fifth Wembley goal for Chelsea.

Other contenders: Gareth Bale (Tottenham), David James (Portsmouth), Aaron Mokoena (Portsmouth), Ricardo Rocha (Portsmouth).

QUARTER-FINALS

Frederic Piquionne (Portsmouth) - scored both goals in the 2-0 victory over Birmingham at Fratton Park.

Other contenders: Alex (Chelsea), John Carew (Aston Villa), Heurelho Gomes (Tottenham), Shane Long (Reading).

FIFTH ROUND

Gareth Bale (Tottenham) - starred on the left flank as Spurs knocked out Bolton after a replay.

Other contenders: Darren Ambrose (Crystal Palace), Jussi Jaaskelainen (Bolton), David James (Portsmouth), Jamie O'Hara (Portsmouth).

FOURTH ROUND

Jermaine Beckford (Leeds) - picked up his second award of the season after scoring twice against Tottenham to earn Leeds a replay.

Other contenders: Graham Dorrans (West Brom), Ricardo Fuller (Stoke), Gylfi Sigurdsson (Reading), Wayne Thomas (Southampton).

THIRD ROUND

Jermaine Beckford (Leeds) - scored the only goal of the game as Leeds knocked out Manchester United.

Other contenders: Joe Lewis (Peterborough), Aaron Ramsey (Arsenal), Jon Parkin (Preston), Daniel Sturridge (Chelsea).

SECOND ROUND

Leon Legge (Brentford) - scored the only goal of the game as the Bees beat League One rivals Walsall.

Other contenders: Lee Harper (Kettering Town), David Lucas (Swindon Town), Vincent Pericard (Carlisle United).

FIRST ROUND

Richard Brodie (York City) - scored twice in their 3-2 victory over League Two side Crewe.

Other contenders: Mat Bailey (Northwich Victoria), Ali Chabaan (Staines Town), Exodus Geohaghon (Kettering Town), Chris Holland (Bath City).

FOURTH QUALIFYING ROUND

Danny Kedwell (AFC Wimbledon) - scored one and made another as the Dons reached the first round proper by beating Crawley 3-1.

Other contenders: Harry Arter (Woking), Alan Pouton (Sutton United), Ollie Price (Paulton Rovers), Gareth Williams (Bromley).

THIRD QUALIFYING ROUND

Adam Webster (Hinckley United) - scored four goals to help Hinckley thrash Cambridge City 5-0.

Other contenders: Mark Danks (Northwich Victoria), Craig Henney (Aylesbury), Ben Highmore (Chippenham Town), Richard Knight (Oxford City).

SECOND QUALIFYING ROUND

Mark Danks (Northwich Victoria) - bagged five goals as the Vics thrashed Bardon Hill Sports 8-0.

Other contenders: Jonathan Hedge (FC Halifax Town), Florin Pelecaci (Bath City), Kevin Slack (Bishop's Cleeve), Michael Tait (Blyth Spartans).

FIRST QUALIFYING ROUND

Bobby Traynor (Kingstonian) - his hat-trick helped the K's beat Bognor Regis Town 4-1.

Other contenders: Nathan Jack (AFC Totton), Chris Mortlock (Pegasus Juniors), Ryan O'Toole (Uxbridge), Alex Taylor (Kendal Town).
