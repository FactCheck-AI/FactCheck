May 26, 1965 - November 29, 2021

Frank Castillo passe away November 29, 2021 in Casa Grande. He was 56.

Frank was born in Casa Grande to Frank and Mary Castillo. He enjoyed spending time with his family and friends, listening to music, and at times cooking the best french toast and Chilaquiles for his nieces and nephews.

He is survives by his mother Mary Castillo; children Veronica Zaragoza, Briana Ruiz, Angelica Zaragoza, Ruben Lopez, Frank Lopez, and Francisco Castillo; sisters Sally Montijo (Hector) and Patricia Castillo; brothers Martin Castillo and Steven Castillo and 17 grandchildren.

He is preceded in death by his father Frank Castillo.
