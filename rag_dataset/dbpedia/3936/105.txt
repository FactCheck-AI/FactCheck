Hawkeye Search is a custom-built search engine for montclair.edu that indexes four types of information:

Selected Pages – Displayed with a title and brief summary.

Departments – Displayed with location and phone number.

People – Displayed with title, email and phone number.

Academic Programs – Displayed with a brief summary and list of programs offered.

Hawkeye Search powers a handful of site features – most notably the main menu’s live search, homepage search area and academic program finder.

To request items for inclusion in Hawkeye Search please email digitalcommunications@montclair.edu. We will need to know: the page URL, name and summary. Please also include any additional keywords that should be connected with the item.
