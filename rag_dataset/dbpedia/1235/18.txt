Kenji Fujimori, son of former president Alberto Fujimori, picked up his father’s national identity card (Documento Nacional de Identidad – DNI) at the Peruvian external affairs office in Lima on Monday July 4.

Kenji Fujimori stated that his father will return to Perú “antes de lo que todos se imaginan” and will be a candidate at the 2006 Peruvian general elections.
