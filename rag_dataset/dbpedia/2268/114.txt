William “Bill” E. Williams, 95, of Lewes, passed away Sunday, Feb. 27, 2022. Bill was born in Philadelphia to his mother Ruth (Beeker) and father Nye Williams. He is predeceased by his wife, Mary Jane “Jane” W. Williams (White); his son, William N. Williams; and his sister, Blanche Spohrer. He is survived by his daughter, Wendy A. Williams; and his grandson, William A. Williams.

Bill spent his adult working life living in Haddonfield, N.J. He moved to Lewes in 1990. He enjoyed every moment of his life in Lewes, including his time with his friends at St. Peter’s Episcopal Church and his treasured Cape Henlopen State Park.
