Relax Inn Bloomsburg is a hotel located in Bloomsburg, Pennsylvania. Free Wi-Fi access is available. Each simply furnished room includes a microwave and refrigerator. Air conditioning is also provided. Bloomsburg Relax Inn has a 24-hour front desk. Laundry facilities and vending machines are located on site. The property offers free parking. Bloomsburg Sportsplex is just 220 m from the hotel. Bloomsburg University is 4.5 km away and Robert B Redman Stadium is 6.2 km away.
