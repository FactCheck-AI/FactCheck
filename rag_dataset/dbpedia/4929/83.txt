Vivid Dreams Park, located at 4960 Vincent Hames in El Paso, will be renamed to Frank Castillo Park.

El Paso City Council approved the name change Tuesday.

was an El Paso native who, along with his brother, spent hours playing at city parks. Castillo was an all-city pitcher at Eastwood High School and was drafted by the Chicago Cubs in 1987.

Castillo played for several Major League Baseball teams over 13 seasons, including the 2004 World Series Champion Boston Red Sox.

Castillo died in a drowning accident in 2013.
