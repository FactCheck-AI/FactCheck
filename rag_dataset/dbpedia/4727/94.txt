Good Ubuntu Touch phone with 1.3GHz Quad-core processor provides good performance and great system execution of the Bq Aquaris E4.5 (Ubuntu).

An advantage of the Bq Aquaris E4.5 (Ubuntu) is the possibility of using two mobile carriers, a Dual-SIM device with two SIM card slots.

Good connectivity of this device includes Bluetooth 4.0 + A2DP, WiFi 802.11 b/g/n [wifi4] (2.4GHz), but it lacks NFC connection.

Including the battery, the Bq Aquaris E4.5 (Ubuntu) phone has 123 grams and it's a very thin device, only 9mm.
