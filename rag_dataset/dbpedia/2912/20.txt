Dorset is home to several aviation and aerospace companies that specialize in the design, development, and manufacture of aircraft and aerospace products. These companies work on a variety of projects related to aviation, including aircraft engines, aircraft systems, avionics, and aircraft maintenance and repair services. Some of the leading aviation and aerospace companies in Dorset include Cobham Aerospace Communications, Meggitt Avionics, and BAE Systems. These companies employ highly skilled professionals and utilize state-of-the-art technology to produce cutting-edge aviation and aerospace products that are used by airlines, defense contractors, and other organizations around the world.
