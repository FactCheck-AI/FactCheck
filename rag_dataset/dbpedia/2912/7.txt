Meggitt Aircraft Braking Systems provides aircraft braking systems to a diverse group of customers including airline operators, aircraft constructors, private aircraft owners and charter operators, governments and military operations and distributors and repair stations. Meggit is located in Akron, Danville and Gardena in the United States; Coventry in the UK plus Fresnillo and Queretaro in Mexico, with repair stations in USA, Europe and Asia. The company occupies the lead position in aircraft wheels, brakes and brake control systems with its products on an active fleet of over 30,000 aircraft. Meggit is a significant braking system supplier to regional jets, business jets and military aircraft and its capability includes helicopter rotor brakes and brake temperature monitoring.
