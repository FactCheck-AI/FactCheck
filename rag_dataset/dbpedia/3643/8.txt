Where is Enterprise, Utah ?

Enterprise lies in Washington County, in the southwestern part of the United States. Located about 40 miles north of St. George, Enterprise is situated near the Nevada border. It is roughly 300 miles south of Salt Lake City, making it part of Utah's scenic red rock country.
