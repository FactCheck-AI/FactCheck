Maria-Letizia Buonaparte, or Letizia Bonaparte, was a minor noblewoman from Corsica and the mother of Napoleon Bonaparte. Marie-Letizia Bonaparte was a Minor Noblewoman from Corsica, she is the Mother of Napoleon Bonaparte.
