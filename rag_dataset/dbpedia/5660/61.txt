Description

A set of photographs recording a visit of King Faisal II of Iraq to a textile mill in Mosul in 1957 or early 1958. The king was killed during the Revolution of 14 July 1958, led by the Free Officers movement headed by Abdul Salam Arif and Abd al-Karim Qasim. This ended the Hashemite Kingdom of Iraq (1932-58) and established the Iraqi Republic.
