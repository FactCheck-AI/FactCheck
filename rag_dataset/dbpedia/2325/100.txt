James Ussher James Ussher was an early 17th-century Archbishop of Armagh in the Church of Ireland who is most remembered for giving an exact date to the beginning of creation. His full body of work includes letters, sermons, dissertations, and exegesis but this work focuses solely on improving the Christians life and strengthening their faith. Anglican author Nicholas Bernard published an important history of the Archbishop of Armagh focusing heavily on the life, service, and death of James Ussher.
