Ruth B. Hansen, 90, of Candler died Wednesday, July 11, 2007, at her residence.

A native of Norway, she had been a resident of Buncombe County for over 15 years. Ruth had been employed as a physical therapist for many years having worked in Norway, Denmark, England, Australia and North Carolina.

She was a member of the Mount Pisgah Academy Seventh-Day Adventist Church.

Survivors include her loving husband of 67 very happy years, Knud B. Hansen; three children, Bent Bjorn Hansen of Canada, Inge-Lise Butler of Australia and Alice Anita Hansen, of Australia; one sister, Mia Bjaanaes of Norway; nine grandchildren, eight great-grandchildren along with many nieces and nephews.

A graveside memorial service will be held at 11 a.m. Wednesday at Shepherd Memorial Park with the Rev. Buz A. Menhardt officiating.

Thos. Shepherd & Son Funeral Directors and Cremation Memorial Center is in charge of arrangements. An online register book is available at www.thosshepherd.com.
