This article is about the posed gloves from Red-haired Asura. For the hair ornament from Lunar Observation, please see Mysterious Night (Hair Ornament).

Mysterious Night is a gloves accessory in the North Kingdom suit Red-haired Asura. It can be obtained from a Styling Gift Box after completing Red-haired Asura.

Appearance[]

A pair of black fingerless gloves Origin of Sin, with the right hand stretched along the body and holding Purgatory's Judgment, and the left hand bent in elbow and holding Cross Killing, pointing it upwards.
