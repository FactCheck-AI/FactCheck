Oak Ridge National Laboratory (ORNL) - ORNL is a Department of Energy national laboratory with major research activities in the areas of neutron science, energy, advanced materials and supercomputing. FSU is a core university partner with the University of Tennessee and Battelle in the research agenda of the laboratory. Oak Ridge Associated Universities (ORAU) - ORAU is a consortium of approximately 100 doctoral granting universities and Oak Ridge National Laboratory, which manages a broad portfolio of education, peer review, national security, emergency preparedness, environmental assessment and worker/public health programs.
