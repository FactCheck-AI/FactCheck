Liberty Hall of Kenansville, NC is a Southern Historic Landmark museum that stands as a proud reminder of the grace and charm of the Antebellum South. Built in the early 1800s, this ancestral home of the Kenan family, prominent founders and leaders in Duplin County and the state, has been meticulously restored and welcomes visitors from around the globe.

With its rich history and captivating architecture, Liberty Hall offers a glimpse into a bygone era, allowing guests to immerse themselves in the stories and traditions of the past. As a treasured piece of North Carolina's heritage, this museum serves as a testament to the enduring legacy of the Kenan family and the region's vibrant history.
