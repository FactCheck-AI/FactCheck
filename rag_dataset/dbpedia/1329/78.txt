FORMERLY OF NORTH SIDE

Raymond F. "Ray" Schumann, 86, of Hopewell Township, formerly of North Side, died on May 14, 2011, in Good Samaritan Hospice.

He was born on April 24, 1925, in Philadelphia, PA, the son of the late John and Elizabeth Schumann. He worked as a millwright for Miller Printing Equipment Company. He was a WWII veteran serving in the Navy. He was an elder of the former McClure Avenue Presbyterian Church, in North Side, and he attended Ohio United Presbyterian Church, in Hopewell Township, lately. He was a lifelong Steelers fan.

He is survived by his beloved wife of 61 1/2 years, Anna "Dolly" Schumann, Hopewell Township; a son and daughter-in-law, John R. "Jack" and Colleen Schumann, Huntington, NY; a daughter and son-in-law, Diane and Paul Passantino, Hopewell Township, and two granddaughters, Caitlin and Megan.

In addition to his parents, he was preceded in death by his brothers, Jack and Joseph Schumann, and his sister, Mary Hackett.

Friends will be received on Monday from 4 to 8 p.m. in the HUNTSMAN FUNERAL HOME & CREMATION SERVICES OF ALIQUIPPA, www.huntsmanfuneralhomes.com, 2345 Mill St., where a funeral service will be held on Tuesday at 11 a.m., with Rev. Eric Powell officiating.

The members of the Beaver County Special Unit will conduct military honors on Tuesday at the funeral home.

In lieu of flowers memorial contributions may be made to McClure Avenue Presbyterian Church, 45 N. Fremont Ave., Pittsburgh, PA 15202, or Ohio United Presbyterian Church, 1236 Longvue Ave., Aliquippa, PA 15001, or Good Samaritan Hospice, 146 Neely School Rd., Wexford, PA 15090.
