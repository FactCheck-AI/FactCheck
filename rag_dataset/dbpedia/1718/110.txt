Sisters Erin Jane Laroue and Melynda Amann formed Jamais Jamais (Jhah-may Jhah-may) in 2010.

After a couple years of playing out and writing music they joined forces with NW classically trained musician ,Donna Potts. Their combined influences of folk, classical, soul, pop, doom and rock have all played an integral part in their musical identity and sound. ... more
