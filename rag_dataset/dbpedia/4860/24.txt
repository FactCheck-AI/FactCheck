HELEN S. APPLEBY

Helen Shelley Appleby, 94, of Orange Park

passed away Monday, December 27, 2010, at Community Hospice - Earl

B. Hadlow Center for Caring, following a short illness. She was a

native and long time resident of Palatka, Florida where she grew up

and raised her family. Helen had retired from the Hudson Pulp and

Paper Company. She had been an active member of St. Monica Catholic

Church in Palatka as well as the Tillandsia Wildflower Club and the

Garden Club of Palatka. She was preceded in death by her parents,

Carleton and Maude Shelley, her husband, Charles J. “Shorty”

Appleby, and her daughter, Sheila Appleby McCartney.

She is survived by two sons, Barry J. Appleby

(wife, Cindy) of Deland, and Charles C. Appleby (wife, Martha) of

Jacksonville, a son-in-law, Henry McCartney, of Buffalo, NY; and a

sister, Dorothy Fay of Orange Park. Helen had seven grandchildren

whom she adored, Shelley McCartney, Rob McCartney, Christine Dumas

(husband, Phil), Jillian Bonventre (husband, Gaspare) Charles L.

Appleby (wife, Elona) Christopher Appleby (wife, Melissa) and Faith

Kicklighter (husband, Mark). Helen had eleven loving great

grandchildren, A.J. Dumas, Sydney Dumas, Alex Dumas, Phillip Dumas,

Ella Kate Bonventre, James Bonventre, Alex Bonventre, Charles C.

Appleby, II, Stephanie Kicklighter, Kevin Kicklighter and William

Kicklighter.

Mass of Christian Burial will be celebrated at

11:00 AM, Monday, January 3, 2011 at St. Catherine of Siena Catholic

Church in Orange Park with Father Dennis Nelson officiating. Burial

will follow at Oak Hill Cemetery in Palatka. No public visitation

is scheduled.

The family has requested that in lieu of

flowers, memorial donations may be made in Helen’s name to:

Community Hospice Foundation, Community Hospice of Northeast

Florida, 4266 Sunbeam Road, Jacksonville, Florida 32257.
