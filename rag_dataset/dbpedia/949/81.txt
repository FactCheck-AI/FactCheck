TOWN OF THERESA - Leo G. Kiefer, 68, of the town of Theresa, died on Sunday, Oct. 18, 2009, at Agnesian Hospice Home of Hope, Fond du Lac.

Friends may call on Wednesday, Oct. 21, from 11 a.m.

until the 2 p.m. hour of service at St. Peter’s Ev. Lutheran Church, Theresa. The funeral service will be at 2 p.m., at the church. The Rev. Jon Learman will officiate. Interment will be in Union Cemetery, Theresa.

Leo was born on Feb. 14, 1941, in Fond du Lac, the son of Andrew and Lucille (Meis) Kiefer. He was married to Nancy Sellnow on April 4, 1982, at St. Peter’s Ev. Lutheran Church, Theresa.

Leo attended school in Lomira. He farmed with his family for many years and also was employed with their salvage business. He worked for Metalcraft in Mayville for 10 years, retiring in 2007. Leo was a member of St. Peter’s Ev. Luthjeran Church in Theresa and was very active in the Wings of Charity organization for many years. Family, listening to polka music and playing Sheepshead were enjoyed by Leo.

Survivors include his beloved wife of 27 years, Nancy, Theresa; two daughters, Ann and Julieann, both of Theresa; two brothers, Donald (Judy) Kiefer, Charlotte, N.C., and Eugene Kiefer, Fond du Lac; four sisters, Judy (Donald) Blada, Coloma, Doris (Daryl) Morgan, Fond du Lac, Donna (fiance Ron Bruce) Stephany, North Fond du Lac, and Karen (Robert) Lowery, Fond du Lac; one brother-in-law, Glenn (Judy) Sellnow, Wauwatosa; nieces, nephews, other relatives and friend.

He was preceded in death by his parents; an infant son; a brother, Terry Kiefer; a sister, Paul Esser, and a brother-in-law, Edwin Stephany.

In lieu of flowers, memorial may be directed to the family.

Cottrill Funeral and Cremation Services, 15 S. Walnut St., Mayville, is serving the family.
