Mayor Of High Wycombe, Buckinghamshire

William Chalfont was the Mayor of High Wycombe in the 6th, 8th and 12th years of Henry VIII (1515, 1517, 1521) and was still apparently mayor in 1533.[2][3]

"An Addington/Chalfant Family History," by David Vern Addington, (1992). William Chalfont who was born sometime before 1500 and married Margaret Artbroke, daughter of Robert Artbroke of High Wycombe. William Chalfont had been appointed Mayor of Wycombe in the 6th, 8th and 12th years of Henry VIII (1515, 1517, 1521) and was still apparently mayor in 1533. He owned property in Bemynster, Dorsetshire, England and died 7 Feb 1543 and is buried at High Wycombe. Wycombe is situated a few miles west of Chalfonte-St. Giles.
