WYSO is the Miami Valley's only NPR News station combining programming from NPR, Public Radio International, American Public Media, PRX and the BBC as well as the the work of local and independent radio producers. It broadcasts on multiple platforms: 91.3 FM, live streaming at WYSO.org, on HD radio and on the Public Radio Player, a mobile application. WYSO is licensed to Antioch College with studios in Yellow Springs. WYSO listeners are engaged, educated, and appreciate excellence. Our listeners are the types of individuals that could be your ideal clients. Corporate support of WYSO will place your business, venue, or nonprofit organization in front of those most likely to become your best customers, subscribers and donors.
