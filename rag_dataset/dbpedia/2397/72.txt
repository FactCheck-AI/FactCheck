Theunis Pieter Zwart was a Dutch politician from the Labour Party (PvdA), born on March 20, 1919, in Winschoten. He spent much of his youth in Dokkum, where his father was a notary. Zwart graduated in law from Groningen University in 1948. He served first as a civil servant and later became the mayor of Wedde in 1952. From December 1962 until his retirement in April 1984, he was the mayor of Leek, where he lived until his death in November 2006 at the age of 87.
