After six seasons of enchantment and magical spells, it has been confirmed that Jennifer Morrison will be leaving ABC‘s hit TV show “Once Upon a Time.”

“Once Upon a Time” is currently waiting for a season seven renewal by ABC, but if the show is renewed, Jennifer Morrison will not be a part of it. In an Instagram post, Jennifer Morrison shared with fans why she decided to leave the popular network show.

Jennifer Morrison played the role of Emma Swan, the main character of the 1-hour drama.

A great amount of question has arisen with this news of what ABC plans to do moving forward with the storyline of the show because of the significance of Morrison’s role.
