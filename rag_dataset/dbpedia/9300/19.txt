Kenneth "Babyface" Edmonds is an American R&B singer, songwriter, and music producer. He has worked with Mariah Carey.

Work with Mariah[]

Babyface first worked with Mariah on the song "Never Forget You" from her Music Box album. He also wrote and produced the song "Melt Away" from Mariah's Daydream album. In 1997, Mariah served as a background singer of Edmond's song called "Every Time I Close My Eyes."[1] In 2015, Edmonds joined Mariah to sing the song "Christmas Time Is In The Air Again" during the Merriest Christmas Hallmark special.
