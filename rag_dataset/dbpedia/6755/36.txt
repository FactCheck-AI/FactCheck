Emma Thompson's performance in "Sense and Sensibility" is a true masterpiece. This period drama based on Jane Austen's novel showcases Thompson's talent for portraying complex characters with depth and emotion.

Her portrayal of Elinor Dashwood, a reserved and practical young woman, earned her critical acclaim and a well-deserved Academy Award for Best Adapted Screenplay.

Her performance earned her numerous awards and nominations, including an Emmy nomination for Outstanding Lead Actress in a Miniseries or Movie.

Thompson's role in "In the Name of the Father" is a departure from her usual period dramas. In this gripping true story, she plays Gareth Peirce, a human rights lawyer fighting for justice for a group of wrongfully convicted Irish individuals.

Thompson's performance is powerful and compelling, capturing the strength and determination of a woman fighting against injustice.

In Kenneth Branagh's adaptation of Shakespeare's play, Emma Thompson shines as Princess Katherine. Her performance in "Henry V" showcases her ability to bring Shakespearean characters to life, infusing them with wit, charm, and vulnerability.

Thompson's chemistry with Branagh, who plays the title role, is palpable, making this film a must-watch for fans of classic literature and historical dramas.

"The Remains of the Day" is a poignant drama set in pre-World War II England. Emma Thompson delivers a captivating performance as Miss Kenton, the head housekeeper at a country estate.

Her emotional portrayal of a woman torn between duty and love earned her an Academy Award nomination for Best Actress.

In "Saving Mr. Banks," Emma Thompson takes the spotlight as P.L. Travers, the author of the beloved "Mary Poppins" books.

In the third installment of the Harry Potter series, Emma Thompson enters this magical world as Professor Sybill Trelawney.

She delivers a powerful performance, bringing Trelawney to life with eccentricity and charm and adding a touch of humor to this beloved fantasy franchise. Her performance is a delightful addition to an already enchanting world.
