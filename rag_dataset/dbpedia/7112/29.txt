Style of hotel and ambience

In the hotel ALPINA, located in the heart of Lucerne, comfortably furnished rooms are available to guests for a relaxing stay. A continental breakfast is served every morning in the neighboring Hotel MONOPOL. Wi-Fi is available to guests throughout the hotel free of charge.

Location of the hotel

The hotel ALPINA is located in the centre of Lucerne at Lake Lucerne, directly at the main station and the culture and congress centre. Famous sights such as Chapel Bridge as well as many restaurants and shopping facilities are easily reachable on foot. Zurich Airport is situated at a distance of 60 kilometres from the hotel ALPINA.
