Who is Aldair Rodríguez?

Aldair Rodríguez is 30-years-old, born on 06 August 1994, and is a professional football player from Peru. Born in Lima, Aldair Rodríguez has also played in Copa Libertadores for Alianza Lima, in Copa Libertadores for América, in Copa Libertadores for Binacional and in Primera División for Alianza Atlét.. He currently plays as a Striker (Centre), Attacking Midfielder (Right) in Primera División for club Cienciano.
