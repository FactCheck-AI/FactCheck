The Real Jackpot is the Hindi dubbed version of Sahasam (transl. Adventure), a 2013 Indian Telugu-language action-adventure film directed by Chandra Sekhar Yeleti and produced by B. V. S. N. Prasad under Sri Venkateswara Cine Chitra. The film stars Gopichand and Taapsee Pannu while Shakti Kapoor, Paru Gambhir, and Ali play supporting roles. Sri Kommineni composed the music for the film while Shamdat Sainudeen handled the cinematography and Kotagiri Venkateswara Rao and Chandra Shekar G. V. edited the film. The film was mostly shot in Ladakh and Hyderabad.

The film was dubbed and released in Hindi by Goldmines Telefilms in 2014.
