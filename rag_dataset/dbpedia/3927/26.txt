Gopichand and Tapsi starrer film ‘Sahasam’ is gearing-up for June 14th release. Currently, the movie is taking final touch-ups in post production process in the Labs. Sahasam is adventurous movie with Gopichand mark action packed fights.

Eleti Chandrasekhar directed this movie produced by BVSN Prasad under his home banner Sri Venkateswara Cine Chitra India Pvt. Ltd. Shakthi Kapoor and Ali have played important roles in this movie. This movie was shot at an all new locations. Sri has composed music for this film. Audio of the film is releasing on June 2nd.

Story, Screen play and Direction: Eleti Chandrasekhar, Camera: Shyam Dutt.S, Music: Sri, editing: Kotagiri Srinivasa rao, Dialogues: KK Radhakrishna Murthy, Lyrics: Ananth Sriram Fights: Selwa.
