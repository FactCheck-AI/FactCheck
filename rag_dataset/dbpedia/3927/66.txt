Sahasam

Gautham is a security guard who dreams of becoming a wealthy man. He realizes that his grandfather Varma (Suman) is a very rich man. In Peshawar, before the partition, Varma does diamond trading business. Now, with the help of Sreenidhi (Tapsee), a religious girl, Gautham goes to Pakistan to find the treasure. The rest of the story is about how that wealth is found by Gautham.action
