William D. Russell, 39, of Sand Lake, died Monday, October 30, 2006. Bill was born February 14, 1967 in Grand Rapids, MI, the son of Robert and Susan (Kurhajec) Russell. Surviving are his wife, Wendy; two daughters, Blair and Kirsti, both at home; his father, Robert Russell of Sparta; one brother, Rick (Chris) Russell of Sparta; one sister, Sharon Russell of Kent City; several nieces and nephews. He was preceded in death by a daughter, Brandy in 1995; and his mother in 1997. The family will receive friends, Thursday, from 2 to 4 and 6 to 8 pm at the Bliss-Witters & Pike Funeral Home, Cedar Springs, www.blisswitterspike.com where services will be held Friday at 2:00 p.m. Pastor Theodore Fish officiating. Interment East Nelson Cemetery. Bliss Witters Pike Funeral Home
