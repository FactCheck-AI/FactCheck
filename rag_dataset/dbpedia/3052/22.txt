BASHKIR STATE MEDICAL UNIVERSITY

Established in 1932, Bashkir State Medical University, based in Ufa, Russia, initially had just one faculty: general medicine. In the later years, faculties like pediatrics and dentistry were added. Bashkir State Medical University ranking among Russia's top 10 medical universities is a sign of quality education. Its straightforward admission process makes it an attractive option among international MBBS aspirants.

In 2010, it joined forces with Ufa Medical University and continued to carry its own name. Currently, over 8,000 students, including 850 international ones, study here. It offers various programs, namely medicine, nursing, and prosthetic Dentistry, among others. The university’s administrator is Pavlov Valentin Nikolaevich, a member of the Russian Academy of Sciences.

Thanks to its collaboration with 50 plus international universities, it offers its students exchange programs and internships in countries such as Germany, China, etc. For 80 years, Bashkir State Medical University Russia has been producing professionals in the healthcare and pharmaceutical fields in Russia. Among Indian students, it's a favorable choice for MBBS in Russia.

ACCOMMODATION

At Bashkir State Medical University, accommodation is a top priority, with a total of five well-maintained hostels. The Bashkir State Medical University hostel rooms are well-furnished with comfortable bedding and essential amenities. To ensure privacy and safety, separate accommodations are allocated for boys and girls. Moreover, students can utilize internet facilities like broadband and Wi-Fi available in the hostels.

Safety is paramount, so the hostels are well-guarded, offering peace of mind to students and their families alike. Beyond academics, the university encourages a vibrant campus life, with a dedicated department for extracurricular activities. From basketball to mountain climbing, students have numerous ways to keep themselves active. Additionally, the university organizes summer sports camps, fostering a sense of camaraderie among peers. With such living facilities and recreational activities, students can be assured of a fulfilling university life.
