Date/Time: July 22, 2022

Location: Klein Memorial Theater, Bridgeport

Tickets: $12 for a one-day pass to the Bridgeport Film Fest

The Greatest Radio Station in the World is a new documentary film about WPKN, a Bridgeport-based community radio station with a fifty-year history of programming not available in the consolidated media. T

The first public screening will be at the Bridgeport Film Festival July 22-24, 2022. There will be a talk-back at the end of the film allowing the audience and filmmaker to share stories about documentaries and the role of radio, art, and journalism in fostering independent thinking and learning.
