Non-Stop-Pop FM (100.7 FM) is a rhythmic contemporary radio station, playing a mix of pop and dance hits in Grand Theft Auto V. The Radio station is hosted by British model Cara Delevingne.

It Is One of the Preference Stations of Tracey De Santa, Hipsters, Tao Cheng.
