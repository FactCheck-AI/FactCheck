New York: Paramont Picture Corporation, 1974. First printing. Pamphlet. An original "Handbook of Production Information" for "The Godfather: Part II", assembled by Paramount Pictures and issued in September of 1974 to promote the film. The handbook features a detailed synopsis of the film along with noteworthy aspects regarding the production of each scene along with biographies of thirty key actors from the film.

Rubber stamp on the front cover "from the collection of James Robert Parish" who was a entertainment reporter for Variety and writer of over 100 books on the genre of Hollywood nostalgia.

4to, 39pp, white printed paper wrappers, title on cover. Small stamp on rear cover "Dupl NYPL" Fine. Item #27323
