Geography

Algeria is a country at the Mediterranean coast in northern Africa. The land has a total area of 2,381,740 km² (919,594 mi²) and a total coastline of 998 km (620.1 mi). This land area is approximately 342% of the area of Texas. Algeria is thus the largest country in Africa and the tenth largest country in the world. A considerable share of the inhabitants (75%) belongs to the urban population.
