Born in Hollywood,California Charlene is best known for her International Hit Single "I've Never Been To Me". She recorded 6 albums for Motown/Prodigal Records from 1974 - 1984. Her music has been featured in films and television including: "The Adventures of Priscilla Queen of the Desert", "Me Without You", "The Last American Vigin", "Berry Gordy's The Last Dragon", "Absolutely Fabulous", "Passions" ans "Desperate Housewives".

Charlene continues to write and record music. She will be releasing a new CD in 2006.
