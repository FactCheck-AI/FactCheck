Zair Azgur was a notable Belarusian sculptor born on January 15, 1908, in Zastadollie, Mogilyov province. He became a full member of the USSR Academy of Arts in 1958 and was recognized as a People's Artist of the USSR in 1973. Azgur was also awarded the title of Hero of Socialist Labour in 1978 for his significant contributions to art. He passed away on February 18, 1995, in Minsk, leaving behind a legacy in Belarusian sculpture.
