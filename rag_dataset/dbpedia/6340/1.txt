Exhibition "Georgian Olympians" took place in the exhibition pavilion of the National Archives of Georgia in Augist, 2016.

The exhibition was dedicated to the XXXI Summer Olympic Games in Rio, in which 40 Georgian sportsmen were participate.

The exhibition presented photos of Georgian Olympic champions and prize winners, preserved in the National Archives, as exhibits of Georgian Sports Museum - Olympic medals, cups and memorial objects.

Documentaries – “Georgian participants return from the Melbourne Olympic Games to Georgia and their Award (1957), “Return of Georgian Olympic athletes from Rome to Georgia” (1960) were shown on the event. The exhibition will last til the end of August. Attendance is free.

Courators of the exhibiton: Giorgi Kakabadze, Sophio Jobava.
