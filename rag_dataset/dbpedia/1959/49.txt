Biography

Father of Charlie Chan Actor Sydney Toler. Hooper G Toler Sr. Was remarkable, leaving home at age 14 he laid his hand to many professions..He was a director or a college, a bank, manager of several businesses and had a hand in raising fine horses. He was said to be generous and smart.

1850 - living in Richmond, Virginia with his parents and siblings. Age 4.[1]

1860 - living in Kansas City, Missouri, with his parents and siblings. Age 13; attended school.[2]

1870 - living with Sarah and their son Matthew in Kansas City, Missouri. Age 23; commercial traveller.[3]

1878 - moved to Wichita, Sedgwick County, Kansas.
