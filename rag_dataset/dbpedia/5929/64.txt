DESCRIPTION

Menara 1 Sentrum is a 32 Storey Grade A Green corporate office tower certified with LEED and GBI, and compliant with MSC Status. It offer state-of-the-art business space, an exclusive address and exceptional transportation links with unrivalled on-site car parking. The building provides a very generous parking ratio and is exclusively serviced by 742 dedicated car park bays. A further 1,243 parking bays are also available in the adjacent NU Sentral retail mall.

Strategically located on Jalan Tun Sambantham in the heart of Kuala Lumpur Sentral CBD. Adjacent to the NU Sentral retail mall and connected to Stesen Sentral Kuala Lumpur via a short integrated air-conditioned walkway.

Modes of transport from Kuala Lumpur Sentral CBD:

KLIA Express - A direct, non-stop link to KLIA with a travel time of only 28 minutes

KLIA Transit - A rapid transit service to KLIA with 3 short intermediate stops at Putrajaya, Cyberjaya, Salak Tinggi & Bandar Tasik Selatan

Light Rail Transit (LRT) - Urban public transport connecting major locations within Kuala Lumpur

KTM Intercity - A central point of all rail destinations throughout Pennisular Malaysia as well as connecting you to Singapore & Bangkok

KTM Komuter - Connecting suburban towns of Rawang, Sentul, Port Klang & Seremban

Rapid KL Monorail - Another convenient rail based transportation system connecting NU Sentral to 10 major locations in KL City

MRT - A new transoprt infrastruture connecting to major locations in Kuala Lumpur which will be ready in 2016

Bus Services - The Skybus, Airport Liner, Aerobus and Star shuttle provide regular bus services to to the Low Cost Carrier Terminal (LCCT), KLIA and other strategic areas within Klang Valley
