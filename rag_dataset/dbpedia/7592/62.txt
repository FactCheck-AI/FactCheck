Troup is one of the 159 counties in Georgia. It was formed in 1825 from land stolen from the Creek tribes. In 2010 the population was 67,044, up from 58,779 in 2000. The county seat and largest city is La Grange.

The county covers 445.96 square miles (1,155 km2), of which 413.90 square miles (1,072 km2) is land. It is bordered by Heard County to the north, Coweta County to the northeast, Meriwether County to the east, Harris County to the south, Chambers County, Alabama to the southwest and Randolph County, Alabama to the northwest.
