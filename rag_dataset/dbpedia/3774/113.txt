Expertise

Robert Bines-Black is a partner in the Corporate team at Memery Crystal. He specialises in capital markets transactions, acting for both UK and international companies, with a focus on the health technology and natural resources sectors. Robert has experience of acting on a wide variety of corporate transactions, from public company flotations, takeovers and fundraises to private company sales, acquisitions and reorganisations.

Outside of work, Robert plays the piano and enjoys spending time in his hometown of Brighton.
