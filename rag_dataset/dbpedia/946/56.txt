Northumbria Journals is the fully Open Access publishing platform managed by Northumbria University Library. Northumbria Journals provides the opportunity for students and academics to publish, read, and re-use the most up-to-date research without any cost.

Each journal and article published on Northumbria has its own unique link, with articles available to download from the site. For help and support in using Northumbria Journals, or to discuss setting up your own title, please contact the Scholarly Communications Team, who manage the resource, by contacting journals@northumbria.ac.uk
