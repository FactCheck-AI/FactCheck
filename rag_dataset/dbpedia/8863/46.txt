Sama, Asturias

Sama is the second largest parish in the municipality of Langreo, Asturias, in the north of Spain, with 12,000 inhabitants. It is one of the largest town in Asturias, after Gijón, Oviedo, Avilés, Mieres and La Felguera.

Ciaño

Ciaño is one of eight parishes (administrative divisions) in Langreo, a municipality within the province and autonomous community of Asturias, in northern Spain.

Cocañín

Cocañín is one of five parishes (administrative divisions) in San Martín del Rey Aurelio, a municipality within the province and autonomous community of Asturias, in northern Spain. It is 9.86 km in size, with a population of 1,046.
