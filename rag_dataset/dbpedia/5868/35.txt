Seabiscuit (2003)

140 mins | Rated M | Drama

In the midst of the Great Depression, a businessman (Jeff Bridges) coping with the tragic death of his son, a jockey with a history of brutal injuries (Tobey Maguire) and a down-and-out horse trainer (Chris Cooper) team up to help Seabiscuit, a temperamental, undersized racehorse goes on to become one of the most successful thoroughbreds of all time.
