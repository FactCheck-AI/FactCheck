Introducing the Fender Jaco Pastorius Jazz Bass, paying homage to one of the most revered electric bassists of all time. Jaco Pastorius left an indelible mark on music, seamlessly blending genres like R&B, rock, jazz, and more with his visionary playing style, all while wielding his trusty Jazz Bass. This signature model faithfully captures the essence of his iconic instrument, delivering the unmistakable tone, feel, and aesthetic that defined his legendary sound.

Crafted with precision and attention to detail, the Jaco Pastorius Jazz Bass features a select alder body, providing a solid foundation for its rich and resonant tone. The maple neck, designed with a comfortable "C" profile, ensures smooth playability, while the special lined fretless pau ferro fingerboard offers a distinctive feel reminiscent of Jaco's original instrument. With a flat joint and 12" radius, this fingerboard is adorned with 20 inlaid white fret line markers, enhancing both its visual appeal and playability.

Driving the Jaco Pastorius Jazz Bass's signature sound are a pair of American Vintage single-coil Jazz Bass pickups, delivering the full, pure, and powerful tones that defined Jaco's groundbreaking style. Paired with a vintage-style bridge and reverse tuners, this bass offers exceptional tuning stability and intonation, allowing players to explore a wide range of musical genres with confidence and precision. As a final tribute to Jaco Pastorius, each bass features a signature-stamped neck plate, honouring the legacy of this pioneering musician.

With its impeccable craftsmanship, timeless design, and unparalleled performance, this instrument is sure to inspire players and audiences alike for generations to come.
