Church or Organization Info

The Bahçecik (Bardizag) St. Jacob Church is a historic Armenian Apostolic church located in the Bahçecik village of the Arslanköy district in the Mersin province of Turkey. The church was built in the 19th century and served as an important center of worship and community for the Armenian community in the region.

The church is known for its beautiful stone architecture, including a bell tower and intricate carvings. It was originally built as a small chapel but was later expanded to accommodate a growing community.
