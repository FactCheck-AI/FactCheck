James Arthur Wilkinson, 76, of Durham, CT passed away on February 13, 2023. Born on March 4, 1946 in Meriden, CT, Jim was a Machinist, Veteran of the Vietnam War, and enjoyed humor and coffee. He will be lovingly remembered by his children, Gabriel Wilkinson, Jaclyn Wilkinson, and Kiera Malowitz and her husband Phil; his sisters Laurel, LuAnn, Linda, and Lori; and his many nieces, nephews, and grandchildren.
