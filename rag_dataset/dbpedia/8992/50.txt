Sullivan County

Sullivan county is located in southeast New York. Sullivan county has 968.13 square miles of land area and 28.59 square miles of water area. As of 2010-2014, the total Sullivan county population is 76,892, which has grown 3.96% since 2000. The population growth rate is higher than the state average rate of 3.26% and is much lower than the national average rate of 11.61%. Sullivan county median household income is $49,388 in 2010-2014 and has grown by 33.49% since 2000. The income growth rate is lower than the state average rate of 35.25% and is higher than the national average rate of 27.36%. Sullivan county median house value is $168,800 in 2010-2014 and has grown by 80.92% since 2000. The house value growth rate is lower than the state average rate of 90.79% and is much higher than the national average rate of 46.91%. As a reference, the national Consumer Price Index (CPI) inflation rate for the same period is 26.63%. On average, the public school district that covers Sullivan county is worse than the state average in quality. The Sullivan county area codes are 607, 845.
