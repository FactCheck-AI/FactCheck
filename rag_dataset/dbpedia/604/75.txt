Complete mitochondrial genome of a sea cucumber, Euapta godeffroyi (Echinodermata, Holothuroidea, Apodida, Synaptidae)

We determined the complete mitochondrial genome sequence of a holothurian Euapta godeffroyi belonging to the order Apodida.
