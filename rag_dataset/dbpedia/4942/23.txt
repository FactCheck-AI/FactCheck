Daniel Joseph Winge was born in Darien, Georgia, in 1903. Although he was born in Georgia, Winge attended high school in East Orange, New Jersey.

Winge entered Rutgers in 1922. At Rutgers, he majored in Liberal Arts and was a member of the debate team. He graduated in 1926. After graduating from Rutgers, he attended law school at Yale for one year. He then worked in real estate, for a catering company, and served in the Armed Forces.

Winge was a member of Alpha Phi Alpha Fraternity. He died in 1966.
