CROWN PRINCE ALEXANDER DELEGATES MR DRAGOMIR ACOVIC TO LAY

WREATH FOR MR NENAD BOGDANOVIC THE MAYOR OF BELGRADE

Belgrade, 1. October 2007 – Mr. Dragomir Acovic, member of

the Privy Council had, on behalf of Their Royal Highnesses Crown Prince

Alexander II and Crown Princess Katherine, laid yesterday a wreath of flowers on

the bier of Mr. Nenad Bogdanovic, the Mayor of Belgrade.

Their Royal Highnesses are in Greece on a humanitarian

mission, and were deeply saddened of Mr. Bogdanovic’s death.
