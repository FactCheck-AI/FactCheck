Roy Acuff (1903-1992), known as the "King of Country Music", helped turn the Grand Old Opry into the nation's foremost country music institution. As one of country music's first superstars, he appeared in several movies, ran for Governor of Tennessee, performed for soldiers around the world, and ran a successful music publishing business. He was the first living member of the Country Music Hall of Fame. Scott 3812 Roy Acuff
