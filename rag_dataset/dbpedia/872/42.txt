Indian jeweller Joyalukkas is taking its brand to Malaysia, opening a mega store in Kuala Lumpur.

The flagship store will offer the widest selection of jewellery under one roof. More than one million designs are showcased at the store. It also boasts extensively-trained sales team to provide best customer service.

“We decided to create the biggest jewellery showroom for Malaysia because we wanted to create a new experience in jewellery shopping,” said chairman and MD Joy Alukkas.

Joyalukkas is a 25 year-old jewellery chain with presence in 10 countries around the world including Singapore and now Malaysia.
