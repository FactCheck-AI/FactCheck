Shqiprim Arifi (Serbian: Шћиприм Арифи / Šćiprim Arifi; born 14 June 1976) is a politician and businessman in the Preševo Valley in Serbia. Arifi is a representative of the country's Albanian community. He is currently the head of the Alternative for Changes and the mayor of the Preševo municipality since 2016.

Ljanik (Serbian Cyrillic: Љаник) is a village located in the municipality of Preševo, Serbia. According to the 2002 census, the village has a population of 29 people, all ethnic Serbs. The toponym is derived from ljanik, a variant of the word pčelinjak ("apiary"). The village was known for its beehives and hence got its name. There are two neighbourhoods (mahale), called Selo and Rosulja. It is located at the slopes of the Rujan mountain.
