What is Bob James' Net Worth?

Bob James is an American musician, arranger, and producer who has a net worth of $15 million. Bob James was born in Marshall, Missouri in December 1939. Bob's first piano teacher discovered that he had perfect pitch. At seven years old he studied with Mrs. R. T. Dufford. In the 1950s he competed at the Missouri State Fair piano competitions and won several blue ribbons. He played with Sarah Vaughan from 1965 to 1968 and then became a studio musician.
