Life at UIC Rockford Heading link

The UIC Health Sciences Campus at Rockford has over 100 years of history in the Rockford region. The campus has nine distinct academic departments and the Rural Medical Education Program is ranked as one of the top rural medical education programs in the United States.

OIS provides immigration advising and cultural adjustment resources to all international students, scholars and faculty at the UIC Rockford campus. OIS staff visit the campus periodically to provide advising, orientation and programming events to students and scholars. Learn more about campus life at Rockford.
