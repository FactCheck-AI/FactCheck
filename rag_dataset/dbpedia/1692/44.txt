Selma Vaz Dias was born on 23 November 1911 in Amsterdam, Noord-Holland, Netherlands. She was an actress and writer, known for Eine Dame verschwindet (1938), BBC Sunday-Night Theatre (1950) and Buddenbrooks (1965). She was married to Hans Werner Egli (1899-2000). She died on 30 August 1977 in London, England, UK.
