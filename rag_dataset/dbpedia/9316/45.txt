Wayne N. Friday, age 67 of Saxeville died unexpectedly Saturday, March 31, 2018 at UW-Hospital in Madison. He was born Oct 29, 1950 in Oshkosh, the son of Nyle J. and Lorraine E. (LaMore) Friday. Wayne served his country in Army during the Vietnam War and was later self-employed. An avid outdoorsman, Wayne loved hunting, fishing, 4-wheeling and going to auctions.

He is survived by three children, Brandi (Mark) Rilling, Redgranite, Jennifer (Steve) Schroeder, Oshkosh, and Joshua (Trisha) Friday, Fort Riley, KS; nine grandchildren; one great grandson; one sister, Julie (Brian) Handy, Waupaca; two brothers, David (Patti) Friday, Pine River, Terry (Bev) Friday, Florida. He is further survived by nieces, nephews, other relatives and friends. He was preceded in death by his parents.

A Celebration of Life will be held on Saturday, April 7, 2018 at 1:00 p.m. at the Pine River Town Hall, N4819 County Road E, Redgranite, WI
