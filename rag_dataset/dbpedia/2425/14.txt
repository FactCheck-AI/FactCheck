ABOUT

DC-Dispur College is a premier educational institution affiliated with Gauhati University, one of the oldest and most prestigious universities in India. Located in Dispur, Assam, it offers undergraduate and postgraduate courses in various disciplines such as Arts, Science, Commerce and Law. The college has an excellent faculty that provides quality education to its students through innovative teaching methods. It also boasts state-of-the-art infrastructure including modern laboratories for research activities. With its commitment towards excellence in academics and overall development of its students, DC-Dispur College stands out among other colleges under Gauhati University.
