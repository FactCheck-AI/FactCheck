William Goldwyn "Bill" Nunn III was an American actor known for his roles as Radio Raheem in Spike Lee's film Do the Right Thing and Robbie Robertson in the Sam Raimi Spider-Man film trilogy.

Nunn also appeared in the films Sister Act, The Legend of 1900 and Won't Back Down.

Additionally, he appeared on the television shows Chicago Hope, Touched by an Angel, Early Edition, The District and Sirens, in which he played recurring character Cash.

Nunn died September 24, 2016, in Pittsburgh, Pennsylvania. He was 62.
