Situated in Tumut at the foothills of the Snowy Mountains in New South Wales, McAuley is a Catholic coeducational school for students from Kindergarten to Year 12.

McAuley Catholic Central School is committed to Christian values and excellence in education and has a rich and warm tradition that focuses on student centred learning.

McAuley’s Vision Statement ‘Flowing Together through Faith and Learning’, encapsulates the principles upon which the school operates, strong community links, Christian values, Parent/Teacher partnership and a Quality Teaching Framework.
