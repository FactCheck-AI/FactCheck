The next League of Europe Round is the Dutch GP held at Zandvoort in North Holland, near one of the major beach resorts of the Netherlands, West of Haarlem.

At the 1952 Dutch Grand Prix, Jan Flinterman and Dries van der Lof were the first Dutch drivers to participate in a Formula One race; for both drivers, it was their only one.
