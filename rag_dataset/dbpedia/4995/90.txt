Description

First Wells Fargo bank, San Francisco, California, USA, in 1852. Wells, Fargo and Company was founded to provide express and banking services to California, at the time undergoing massive expansion due to the California Gold Rush. This first Wells Fargo bank in San Francisco was opened at 114 Montgomery Street. Wells Fargo grew to become one of the largest banks in the world. This photograph is part of the Historic American Buildings Survey.
