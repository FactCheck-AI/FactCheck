Michael Sullivan Associate Lecturer

Criminal Justice and Sociology

Mike Sullivan graduated from Curry College in 2000 with his Bachelor of Arts degree in Criminal Justice. In 2008, Mike earned his a Master’s in Criminal Justice from Boston University. In 2020, Mike also completed a graduate certificate in Cybercrime Investigations and cyber security from Boston University.

In 2000, Mike joined the Warwick Police Department (RI). In 2005, he joined the Boston Police Department (BPD). Mike has served with BPD in District B-3 (Mattapan/Dorchester), the Fugitive Unit, and the Crimes Against Children’s Unit. Mike also serves as a Task Force Officer with the FBI Boston Division’s Human Trafficking and Child Exploitation Task Force.

Mike is currently a Sergeant Detective overseeing the BPD's Internet Crimes Against Children Unit.
