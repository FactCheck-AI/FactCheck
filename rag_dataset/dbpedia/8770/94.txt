Chris Carrabba of Dashboard Confessional fame has started a new project called Twin Falls. The band consists of Carrabba, Suzie Zeldin (The Narrative) on mandolin, bassist Jonathan Clark and Ben Homola (Bad Books) on drums. The band made their live debut last month at an intimate Dashboard Confessional show at St. Aquinas High School in Fort Lauderdale, FL, and will be working on their debut full-length later this year.
