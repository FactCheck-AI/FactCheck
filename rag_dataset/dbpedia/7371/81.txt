2017 NFL Draft second round pick Dalvin Cook made a return to Tallahassee for Florida State’s Lift for Life event on Tuesday and the upcoming camp session concluding Friday.

“Why not just stop in and show my support.”

The new member of the Minnesota Vikings spoke about his time at Florida State whether that be the exceptional training he received that prepared him for the NFL or what he misses most about his time in a Seminoles uniform.

“This program (at Florida State) has become one of the elite programs in the country. We play at a high level and we play some of the best talents.”

Cook noted that he’ll be in attendance when Florida State takes on Alabama on September 2 to begin its 2017 season.

The former ’Nole also went into detail about the “smooth” transition it has been thus far from the college to NFL
