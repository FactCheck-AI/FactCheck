Mildred Washington (1696 - September 5, 1747) was a resident of Fairfax County, Virginia and the aunt of U.S. President George Washington.

Washington was born in 1696 in Virginia as the only daughter of Lawrence Washington and Mildred Warner. She lived in Virginia after her husband in 1718. In 1726, Gregory and his wife conveyed to Augustine Washington the tract of land called Mount Vernon. After her husband's death in 1731, she led an active life as a widow. She tood godmother for her nephew, future U.S. President George Washington, after his birth in February 1732.

On September 5, 1747, Washington died at the age of 51.
