Sitcom bible the British Comedy Guide pays tribute to two great comedy writers, Laurence Marks and Maurice Gran, at this special live event. Between them, their credits include 'The New Statesman', 'Goodnight Sweetheart', 'Birds of a Feather' and many more. Tonight they celebrate 35 years as comedy writers with a live Q&A hosted by Louis Barfe. And it's all in aid of charities Orbis and Crisis at Christmas.
