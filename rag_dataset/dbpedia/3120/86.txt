Almighty Techserv, a Dalmia Group Company was established in the year 2005. Company is engaged in Distribution, Procedure, Technical Support Services and Warranty Support to Companies engaged in development and production of various products, for Defense Forces and Para Military Organisations, Police and other CPO’s.

Company is currently engaged with a number of Indian Public and Private Sector Companies and Fortune 500 Foreign Companies, in different capacities namely as an Associate, as a System integrator and as a Technical Adviser. Further they provide complete hand holding to foreign companies to establish their business in India as a Consultant, Distributors, Joint Venture.

Also we are proud to be Research and Development partners with esteemed organisation of “Government of India” Defense Research Development Organisation (DRDO) for various “Make in India” projects.

As challenges have advanced, so have the solutions. We believe company needs to constantly stay ahead and keep pace with the latest technology to meet growing needs.
