BY CAR

By car you reach us comfortably from the highway exit Niederbipp in the direction of Langenthal. Within Langenthal follow the signs "Hotel Meilenstein".

BY TRAIN

By public transport, take bus no. 64 from the Langenthal railway station in the direction of "Lotzwil, Unterdorf" to the bus stop "Schwimmbad Langenthal".
