The Website for All Blues Hockey Fans

This informational fan site is dedicated to the greatest team in the NHL, the St.Louis Blues.The St.Louis Blues were founded in 1967 and have been fortunate to make numerous playoff appearances. The Blues have seen plently of hall of fame talent over the years including Wayne Gretzky, Al MacInnis, and Brett Hull.

The Blues currently have talented players that make up their roster including T.J Oshie, Patrick Bergland, Chris Stewart....
