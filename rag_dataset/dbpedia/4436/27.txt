Product Information:

This 500 gram cast gold bar is fabricated by Umicore N.V. Umicore has it's headquarters in Brussels, Belgium.

The gold bar’s front surface displays Umicore's refinery logo, as well as the bar’s weight ‘500 g’, its gold purity ‘999,9 FEINGOLD’, and the bar’s unique serial number.

Umicore N.V. is on the LBMA’s Good Delivery List for gold and hence the 500 g cast gold bar is exempt from Singapore’s Goods and Services Tax (GST).
