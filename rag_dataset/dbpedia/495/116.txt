A2IM Indie Week, the only music industry conference with a focus on the United States independent sector – bringing together labels, distributors, publishers, and music services to discuss industry trends, insights and more. Held every June in NYC.

For 13 years, A2IM has hosted the Libera Awards every June in NYC – an awards ceremony honoring the independent music sector by recognizing artists and the teams that support them. The Libera Awards is the premier celebration of independent music in the United States.
