George Adam Toth of Eastlake OH., 80, peacefully passed away Wednesday, March 13, 2024 surrounded by his family. George was born Dec. 14, 1943 in Cleveland OH to the late George P. and Rose M. (Ofchinick) Toth.George attended North High School and served in the US Army from 1961-1964.George was preceded in death by his wife of 30 years, Mary Rose (Scavone). He is survived by his loving wife of 25 years Jackie (Pesta). He was a loving father to son Adam (Jodi) Toth, daughter Wendy (Patrick) Drudy, grandfather to Erica, Matthew, Andrew, Sarah, Patrick Jr., Noah and the late Samantha. He was stepfather to David M. Pesta and the late Suzanne Pesta. George was also survived by his brother Charlie (Peggy) Toth and half brother Rudy Toth, nieces, nephews and other family and friends.George enjoyed fishing and was an avid bowler. He made a friend of all those he met, and his personality could light up a room.A private family memorial service will be held at a later date.
