Vicki Jean Toth, age 68, of Deerbrook, died Tuesday, December 20, 2016 at her home, under the care of her family and LeRoyer Hospice.

Vicki was born on July 26, 1948 to the late Clive LaMar Jensen and Anna Jean Vautrin.

Vicki was united in marriage to Roger Toth. He survives and lives in Deerbrook.

Vicki graduated from high school in Tooele, Utah.

She was employed at Eastview as a housekeeper.

Vicki enjoyed trips to the casino along with playing and eating ice cream with her grandkids. She liked working and planting in her yard, watching hummingbirds and baking for Christmas with her family.
