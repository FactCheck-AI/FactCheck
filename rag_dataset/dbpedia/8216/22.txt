Bio:

Liam Hemsworth (born in Melbourne, Australia on January 13, 1990) is an Australian actor. Hemsworth is best-known for playing Gale Hawthorne in The Hunger Games. He has also been the lead in films like The Last Song and The Expendables 2. He first gained notoriety playing Josh Taylor in the soap opera Neighbours, and as “Marcus” on the children’s television series The Elephant Princess. Hemsworth’s elder brothers, Luke and Chris Hemsworth, are also actors, and provided a path for Hemsworth to emulate. His personal life was marked by his relationship with fiancee and The Last Song co-star, Miley Cyrus. The couple got engaged in May 2012, but called things off in Sept. 2013 amid cheating rumors and reports of continuous fighting.
