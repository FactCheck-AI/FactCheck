Haitham El-Fazani is an Egyptian football player of Libyan descent, who played for the youth teams of Zamalek and El Sharkia before joining Al Ahly's youth team at the age of 16. He has played several important official matches with Al Ahly's first team, most notably in the 2006 African Super Cup match against FAR Rabat, where he came on as a substitute for the late Mohamed Abdelwahab in the 83rd minute.
