BEACH, Rex. The Mating Call. Toronto: Musson Book Co, (1927).

First Canadian Edition. Romance novel about an American man shell shocked from the war who marries a Russian immigrant. Basis for the 1928 film directed by James Cruze and starring Thomas Meighan and Evelyn Brent. Colorful dustjacket art. Near Fine in Very Good dustjacket, shallow chipping at spine ends and flap corners.
