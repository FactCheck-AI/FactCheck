Mary White O’Neal, 69, of Frederick County, Virginia passed away Monday, July 24, 2017 at Warren Memorial Hospital.

Mrs. O’Neal was born in 1948 in Elizabeth, New Jersey, the daughter of Mary White and the late Donald White, Sr. She was a dance instructor and owner and operator of Mary White O’Neal’s Dance Workshop.

Mary wed Jerry O’Neal on June 16, 1973 in Winchester, Virginia.

Surviving with her mother and her beloved husband of 44 years is a daughter, son, four grandsons, one sister, and two brothers.

Memorial contributions in memory of Mary may be made to American Cancer Society, 1833 Plaza Drive, Winchester, Virginia 22601.
