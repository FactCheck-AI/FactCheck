Antony Sher was a British artist who was born in 1949. Numerous key galleries and museums such as Ben Uri Gallery & Museum have featured Antony Sher's work in the past.The maker's first piece to be offered at auction was "A self-portrait with a handwritten wish "In 1996 I wish, I wish, for a cure for AIDS to be found"" at TW Gaze in 2024. The artist died in 2021.
