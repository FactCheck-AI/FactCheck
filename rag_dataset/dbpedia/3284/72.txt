SPIDER-MAN UNLIMITED, the latest ongoing series exclusive to the Marvel Unlimited app, set in the current continuity of Spider-Man comics. And, in its opening twelve-part arc from creators Christos Gage, Simone Buonfantino, and Fer Sifuentes-Sujo, readers will be treated to one of the most thrilling crossovers of the Spider-Verse yet: The first ever face-to-face meeting between Peter Parker of Earth-616 and the Peter Parker from the world of PlayStation’s Marvel’s Spider-Man!
