Bill Gates is the co-chair of the Bill & Melinda Gates Foundation and was formerly the CEO of Microsoft. He co-founded Microsoft with Paul Allen in 1975 and led the company to dominate the world's PC operating system market with MS-DOS and Windows. In 1994, Gates began dedicating more time to philanthropy and established the William H. Gates Foundation. He and his wife Melinda combined three family foundations into the Bill & Melinda Gates Foundation in 2000, which works to reduce extreme poverty and improve global health. The foundation is now the largest transparently operated private foundation in the world.
