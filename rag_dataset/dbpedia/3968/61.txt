Please note that, sadly, some people are taking advantage of the current pandemic crisis to send out fake emails pretending to be parish priests asking people for money in the form of gift cards, etc. They use fake email addresses which look quite similar to the real thing and use the names of the parish priests.

Please know that we will not be sending you emails asking for gift cards or other scams. We are very grateful for your donations but those should be made through this website or directly mailed to the parish.
