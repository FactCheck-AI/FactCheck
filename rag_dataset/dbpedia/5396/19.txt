Other general FAQs for Predictive Analytics Certification

Take a sneak peek into why professionals need to be certified with Predictive Analytics.

What is Predictive Analytics?

Predictive Analytics is the use of statistics and modeling techniques to forecast future outcomes.

What are the tools used in Predictive Analytics?

Some of the best tools used in Predictive Analytics that are available in the market are IBM SPSS Statistics, SAS Advance Analytics, SAP Predictive Analytics, H20, Oracle Data Science, and QResearch.

What is Descriptive Analytics vs Predictive Analytics?

Descriptive Analytics is the preliminary stage of data analysis. Predictive Analytics models predict the results by looking at the historical data whereas descriptive analytics models analyze historical data to determine how a unit may respond to a set of variables.

What is Prescriptive Analytics vs Predictive Analytics?

Prescriptive Analytics is the next step to Predictive Analytics. Prescriptive Analytics is the branch of data analytics that uses predictive modeling to suggest actions to get the optimal result.

What is Predictive Analytics software?

Predictive Analytics software mines and analyses data collected from any number of loT devices to detect patterns and predict future results.

What are the types of data analytics?

The three different types of Predictive Analytics are regression analysis, time series analysis, and machine learning algorithms.

Which is the best book on Predictive Analytics?

The Fundamentals of Machine Learning for Predictive Data Analytics is a book by JD Kelleher that covers all the main aspects of Predictive Analytics.

How are predictive analytics and machine learning related?

Predictive Analytics and Machine Learning are closely related, and predictive models include machine learning algorithms to analyze data efficiently and promptly.

What are the best practices of Predictive Analytics?

Validating database sets, defining objectives, identifying high-quality data streams, discovering the best visual representation, and monitoring the performance.
