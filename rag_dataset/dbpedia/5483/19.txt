How Does Anti-Scratch Tape Work?

Cats have a natural urge to scratch, which helps them sharpen their claws, stretch their muscles, and mark their territory. Unfortunately, this behavior can lead to shredded furniture and frustrated pet owners. Anti-scratch tape works by providing a barrier between your cat and your furniture. When your cat tries to scratch the protected surface, the sticky texture of the tape feels uncomfortable on their paws, which discourages them from continuing to scratch.

(Pro tip: buy a cat scratching post and place it near the furniture with the anti-scratch tape, giving the cats an option to claw and scratch other than the furniture)

How to Use Anti-Scratch Tape?

Using cat training tape is easy and straightforward. Simply identify the areas of your furniture that your cat likes to scratch, and apply the tape to those areas. The tape can be cut to size and is easy to remove when no longer needed.

Anti-scratch tape is a simple and effective solution to the problem of cat scratching furniture. It provides a barrier between your cat and your furniture and helps to protect your belongings.
