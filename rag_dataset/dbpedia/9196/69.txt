Q. What was the capital of Bahmani kingdom?

Answer: Gulbarga

Notes: Tughluq founded the Bahmani kingdom with its capital at Gulbarga in 1346. Thus in the fourteenth century, two powerful kingdoms emerged in the south-the Vijayanagar and the Bahmani.
