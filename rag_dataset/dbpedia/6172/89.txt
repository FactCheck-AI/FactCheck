Duke University, NC, USA

Latitude and longitude coordinates are: 36.001465, -78.939133.

The second-largest private employer in the state, Duke University is a private research school located 2.5 miles away from the city of Durham, in North Carolina, United States. It was established as Brown School in 1838. The university was also known as Trinity College from 1859 to 1924. Duke University is one of the top universities in the United States. The notable alumni of the school include Rhodes Scholars, Churchill Scholars, Nobel Prize winners, Turing Award winners, billionaires, as well as 37th president of the United States Richard Nixon and former President of Chile Ricardo Lagos. Nearby attractions are Duke University Golf Club, Duke Forest - Shepherd Nature Trail, Al Buehler Trail, Pine Thicket Trail, Wallace Wade Stadium, Nasher Museum of Art, Perkins Library, Davison Quad, Sarah P. Duke Gardens, Cameron Indoor Stadium, and Duke University Chapel.
