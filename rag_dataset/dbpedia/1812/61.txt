Jaldhaka Upazila

Jaldhaka (Bengali: জলঢাকা, Joldhaaka, means covered under water) is an Upazila of Nilphamari District in the Division of Rangpur, Bangladesh.

Dohazari Airfield

Dohazari Airfield is a former wartime United States Army Air Forces airfield near Dohazari in Bangladesh used during the Burma Campaign 1944-1945. It is now abandoned.

Dhepa River

The Dhepa is a small river in northern Bangladesh. The river originates from the Atrai river in Mohanpur and falls into the Punarbhaba.

Chandraghona

Chandraghona is forty-eight kilometers from Chittagong on the Kaptai Road. One of the biggest paper mills(Karnaphuli Paper Mills Limited) in Asian is located here.
