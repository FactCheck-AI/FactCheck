The Ashi Productions and Production I.G. produced Blue Seed that has been adapted more times to English than the bible is set to stream on HiDive on Monday, July 29, 2019, at 17:00 UTC. The streaming service has released a synopsis for the show that will get it’s streaming premiere in a few days.

“The monstrous Aragami threaten all of humanity and the only ones who can stop them are those of the ancient Kushinada bloodline. Unaware of her heritage, ordinary school girl Momoiji Fujimiya lives a peaceful life in Izumo, Japan. However, all sense of normalcy comes to an abrupt end when a horrifying monster suddenly attacks her. Now she must take on the mantle of Kushinada and help the Terrestrial Administration Center stand against the Aragami’s onslaught.”
