Christina Pickles first appeared on daytime in 1970 as Linell Conway on Guiding Light a role she played until 1972. Pickles returned to daytime in 1977 as Contess Elena dePoulignac on Another World. Pickles went on to primetime success as Nurse Helen Rosenthal on St. Elsewhere. In the 1990's, Pickles made recurring guest appearances on Friends playing Judy Geller, Ross and Monica's mother. Pickles continues to act.

Personal Life[]

Pickles was married to Victor Lobl (1962-1985) and they share two children: Oliver and Rebecca.
