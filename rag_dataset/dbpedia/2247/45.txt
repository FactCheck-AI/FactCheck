Not every band can rise up to a musical legend but every once in a while, a group is able to exceed all expectations, to define the boundaries and even extravagant them – the sky’s the limit! Among the youngest bands ever to storm the metal genre, to take the Thrash Olympus by assault and to cut their own path, Death Angel have come to be known as one of the most influential bands to emerge from the thriving Bay Area Thrash Metal scene in the early ’80s due to their inventive style and speedy, complex arrangements.
