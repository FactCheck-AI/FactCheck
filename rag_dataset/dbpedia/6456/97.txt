The film is one of the few surviving silent films based on a literary text by the famous American author F. Scott Fitzgerald - in this case the short story 'Head and Shoulders' (1920).

The film is directed by William C. Dowlan, an American stage and film actor and director of the silent era.

The silent film will be shown at Linnaeus University in Växjö in connection with The 16th International F. Scott Fitzgerald Society Conference, which takes place from 26 June to 2 July.

Time and place of the screening:

IKEA Hall, House N, Linnaeus University, Växjö, at 18:45 on Saturday 1 July.

The screening of the silent film will be accompanied by live piano, played by Elias Nilsson. There will also be an introduction to the film by Fitzgerald expert Martina Mastandrea.
