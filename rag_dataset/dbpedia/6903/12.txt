Biography

Robert William Kuhn was born on the 2nd of June 1923 in Landisburg, Pennsylvania. He was the son of Samuel Maurice Kuhn (1887-1970) and Mabel (Stambaugh) Kuhn (1889-1974).[1][2]

On the 27th of July 1944 he enlisted as a private first class in the US Army for the duration of WWII.[3] He received a Bronze Star and Purple Heart.[4]

He married Genevieve (Range) Kuhn on the 12th of November 1948 in Dauphin, Pennsylvania.[5] They raised five children in Loysville,Pennsylvania.[6]

Robert passed away on the 25th of December 2006 in Camp Hill, Pennsylvania at eighty-three.[7] He was buried in Restland Cemetery in Loysville, Pennsylvania.[4]
