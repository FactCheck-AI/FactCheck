About Panjwar

According to Census 2011 information the location code or village code of Panjwar village is 037891. Panjwar village is located in Tarn Taran tehsil of Tarn Taran district in Punjab, India. It is situated 18km away from Tarn Taran, which is both district & sub-district headquarter of Panjwar village. As per 2009 stats, Panjwar Khurd is the gram panchayat of Panjwar village.

The total geographical area of village is 1913 hectares. Panjwar has a total population of 8,046 peoples, out of which male population is 4,248 while female population is 3,798. Literacy rate of panjwar village is 55.73% out of which 59.13% males and 51.92% females are literate. There are about 1,406 houses in panjwar village. Pincode of panjwar village locality is 143301.

Tarn Taran is nearest town to panjwar village for all major economic activities.
