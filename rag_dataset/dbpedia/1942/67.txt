Biography

This profile is part of the Forbes, New South Wales One Place Study.

Cecil Turner died young.

Cecil John Turner was born on 1 May 1911 in Forbes, New South Wales. He was the son of Robert Turner and Elizabeth Ann Rousell.

Cecil passed away on 25 November 1911 in Forbes, aged about 6 months. He was buried in Forbes Cemetery, Forbes, New South Wales in an unmarked grave.

Research Notes

Place of death Bathurst St, Forbes

Death year in NSW BDMs and Find a Grave are different. Suggest NSW BDMs is the more reliable source, Find a Grave indicates that the grave is unmarked and does not indicate what the source of the death date is.
