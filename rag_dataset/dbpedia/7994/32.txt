Thal bei Graz (meaning Thal by/near Graz) (pronounced [taÀêl]) is a small village in Austria, and a suburb of Graz. The village is about 2 miles from the edge of Graz. Most famous for being the birthplace of Arnold Schwarzenegger. It has a population of 2,138.
