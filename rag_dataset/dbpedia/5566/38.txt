ISLE OF PALMS, S.C. – Actor Will Patton, of the movies Remember the Titans and Armageddon and the made-in-B.C. series Falling Skies, has been charged with driving under the influence in his native South Carolina.

Court records show that the 60-year-old Patton was stopped on the Isle of Palms on Tuesday night.

He was charged with first offence driving under the influence with a blood alcohol content of less than 0.10. The legal limit for driving is 0.08.

Court documents show he was released Wednesday on a $997 personal recognizance bond. Patton is a native of nearby Charleston.
