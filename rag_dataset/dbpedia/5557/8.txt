Azerbaijani judoka Hidayat Heydarov has won a gold medal at the European Championship in Sofia, Bulgaria.

The athlete competing in the weight category up to 73 kg defeated Giovanni Esposito (Italy), according to Report.

Another Azerbaijani athlete Rustam Orujov, who competes in the weight category up to 73 kg, defeated Adrian Sulka (Romania) and won the bronze medal in the championship.
