Northern Cyprus, officially the Turkish Republic of Northern Cyprus (TRNC), is a de facto state that comprises the northeastern portion of the island of Cyprus. It is recognized only by Türkiye, and its territory is considered by all other states to be part of the Republic of Cyprus.
