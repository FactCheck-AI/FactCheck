WTC Granite Industries

WTC Granite Industries is one of the largest U.S importers of finished monuments, benches, columbarium and mausoleums, offering a unique value that has been built on unparalleled service, guaranteed quality, innovative design and a brand that keeps moving forward.

WTC Littleton, NH offers a full-service in-house approach to drafting, sandblasting, laser etching and distribution.

WTC Columbus, OH is considered the Midwest Distribution Center.

WTC Elberton, GA offers domestic and import granite color options; as well as, has an unmatched ability to orchestrate the utilization of local talent with drafting, sandblasting, rock work and etching services.

All 3 facilities are ideally positioned for growth and are cross utilized to meet the needs of all customers.
