80s alt country punk outfit, Lone Justice, with Maria McKee, Ryan Hedgecock, Marvin Etzoni are in full flight with the release of a brand new 7”. Taken from their forthcoming album, ‘Teenage Kicks’ is a rambunctious slice of punk angst that was recorded in one take. It’s a timeless anthem with a joyous riff that explodes into a hail of feedback.
