Dominick Wickliffe, better known by his stage name Crooked I, is an American rapper from Long Beach, California. The name “Crooked I” comes from a shortened version of his original moniker, Crooked Intriago. It has nothing to do with the malt liquor. He is 1/4th of rap supergroup Slaughterhouse (with Joe Budden, Royce da 5’9” & Joell Ortiz), and is currently regarded as one the best rappers on the west coast, after rising to hip-hop prominence with his freestyle series ‘Hip-Hop Weekly’, in which he released a new freestyle over popular industry beats every week for 52 weeks. Crooked I is CEO of his own record label, Dynasty Entertainment, and Senior Vice President of Treacherous Records.
