Sana'a, Yemen

Latitude and longitude coordinates are: 15.356695, 44.200218.

Sana'a (صنعاء‎), also Sana, is the capital city of Yemen, located near the Sarawat Mountains. It is also Yemen's largest city nicknamed "Amānat Al-ʿĀṣimah". The Old City is a UNESCO World Heritage Site. The city has many historical landmarks and points of interest, such as the National Museum of Yemen, the 1,000-year-old Gate of Yemen, Al Saleh Mosque, the Ali Muhesen Stadium, and Dar al-Hajar, as well as sports facilities, hotels, markets, restaurants, monuments, old buildings, and stores. It is home to the Sana'a University. Sanaa International Airport serves the area.
