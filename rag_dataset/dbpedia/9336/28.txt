Paul Randolph Swenson, age 75, passed away peacefully at his home surrounded by family on April 30, 2018. He was born on April 6, 1943 in Salt Lake City, Utah to Merill and Mildred Horne Swenson. A devoted, loving, humorous, Husband, Father, Brother, Grandpa and Friend. Paul was an artist and a dedicated hard-working employee of JC Penney Corporate Offices in Texas and Grand Central in Salt Lake City, Utah. He graduated from West High School.

Paul is survived by his son, Brandon K. (Fawn), daughters, Jamie Swenson (Arnulfo) and Ancherie Swenson; sister, Linda "Lindy Lou" Swenson; grandchildren, Anthony, Ashley, Britney, Jake, and Sabastian; as well as cousins and dear best friends. He is preceded in death by his beautiful wife, Diana Lynn; brother, Dale; and his parents.

Graveside services will be held on Wednesday, May 9, 2018 at 1:00 p.m. at the Larkin Sunset Gardens Cemetery, 1950 East Dimple Dale Road, Sandy Utah.
