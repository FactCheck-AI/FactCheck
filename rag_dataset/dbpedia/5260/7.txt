The global pool & spa market is expected to reach USD 23.6 billion by 2027 from an estimated USD 19.1 billion in 2022, at a CAGR of 4.3% from 2022 to 2027. A pool is a structure designed to hold water to enable swimming or other leisure activities. It is mostly constructed from materials such as concrete, plastic, or fiberglass and can be of a custom size and shape or built to a standardized size. Hot tubs and spas are pools filled with water that is heated and then used for relaxation or hydrotherapy. Commercial spas are common in the pool or sauna area of a health club or fitness center. The global pool & spa market is expected to witness significant growth during the forecast period. Growth in residential and commercial construction activities and sustained growth of luxury homes that generally install swimming pools are driving the market growth.

There are several types of pools & spas depending upon the size and users requirement. A few major players have a wide regional presence and dominate the pool & spa market. The leading players in the pool & spa market include Harvia Group (Finland), Pentair (US), Fluidra (Spain), KLAFS GmbH & Co. KG (Germany), TyloHelo (Sweden).
