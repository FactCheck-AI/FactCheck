Memorial Mass for Dino James DeRose, 49, of Georgia, will be 11 a.m. Saturday at St. Williams Church, 367 E. 260th St., in Euclid.

Mr. DeRose died Sept. 29, 2005, in Austin, Texas.

Born Dec. 27, 1955, in Cleveland, he had lived in Euclid before moving to Georgia 22 years ago.

He was a 1974 graduate of Euclid High School.

Mr. DeRose had been a lighting director and designer.

Survivors are his parents, James and Jean (Gambatesa) DeRose; sisters, Cindy and Gina DeRose; and brothers, Ralph and Jim DeRose. He also leaves his companion, Michelle Carter and her daughter, Amber.

Friends may call 2 to 4 and 6 to 9 p.m. Friday at the Orlando-Donsante Funeral Home, 29550 Euclid Ave., in Wickliffe.
