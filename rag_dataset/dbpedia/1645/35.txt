Description:

This album is a compilation of works by Elena Gerhard, one of the most famous German mezzo-sopranos of the first half of the 20th century, from her 1907 recording with Nikisch as piano accompanist to her 1948 private recording. The album contains 23 tracks in total, ranging from Nikisch's piano accompaniment in 1907 to a private recording made in 1948. (C)RS
