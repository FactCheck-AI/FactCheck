Contact PowerCorps for information on the upcoming programs. Enrollment is ongoing and the next solar training program is slated for July/August 2024.

For interested candidates looking to get to work while waiting for solar training, PowercorpsPHL offers a 4-to-18 month paid workforce development program that uses environmental service to teach career and technical skills. Interested participants will gain the opportunity to develop the skills to become environmental stewards, secure meaningful work, and become civically engaged members of their community.

The program is free with a living stipend.
