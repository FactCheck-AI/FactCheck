Biography

Briefly… Can Caglar played two seasons at LMU from 2022-2023.

2023: Sophomore… Competed in three games … Logged over 27 minutes in the cage… Recorded a goals against average of 5.78 with a .643 save percentage… Recorded four saves against both Occidental and La Verne in the LMU Invite on Sept. 2, 2023… Earned ACWPC All-Academic Honors.

2022: Freshman… Competed in six games, making one start… Logged over 78 minutes in the cage… Recorded a goals against average of 7.34 with a .561 save percentage… Made a career-high 10 saves against Pomona-Pitzer (Sept. 24, 2022)… Added one assist against Fresno Pacific (Sept. 15, 2022).

Before LMU... Joins the Lions after playing four seasons of water polo and swim at Highland Park High School… Born in Istanbul, Turkey... Son of Selcuk and Pinar Caglar... Has one sister, Mina... HPHS American Field Service Club, Vice-President... Computer Science National Honor Society... Member National Honor Society... Member USA Water Polo Academic All-American, Recipient... SWZ Team 2018 Development... 2019 Cadet, 2020 Cadet for West Regional and National Championship, 2021... Youth First place at the 2021 Youth West Regional Championships... Won swim regional championship for 5A in 2020 and 2021... Competed for Pegasus Water Polo Academy... 13th place at Futures Super Finals with Pegasus 18U 2019... Texas North Region Tournament took 2nd place with Dallas ISD Water Polo... 12th Annual Pacific Winter Classic (2019, San Diego) took 1st in 16U with Pegasus Water Polo (the first ever non-California team to win the competition)... 2019 NJOs took 2nd in the 18U gold bracket with Trident Water Polo Club.
