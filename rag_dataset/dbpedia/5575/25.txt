Location

It’s nice to come back to a place where you can have a rest after an eventful day in the city Hotel «Sardegna Costa Nord Ovest» is located in Santa Maria Coghinas. This hotel is located 1 km from the city center. You can take a walk and explore the neighbourhood area of the hotel. Places nearby: Rosello Fountain, Museo Storico della Brigata and Archaeological Museum of Sanna.

At the hotel

If you travel by car, there’s a free parking zone. Specially for tourists who travel by car, there’s a parking zone. Here, you can treat yourself with water procedures as there will be a pool. There are other services available for the guests of the hotel. For example, dry cleaning and ironing.

The staff of the hotel will be happy to talk to you in English and Italian.
