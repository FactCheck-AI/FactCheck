Biography

Gene Hackman is an American actor.

Eugene Allen Hackman was born on 30 Jan 1930 in San Bernardino County, California, USA [1] and is the son of Anna Lyda Elizabeth (Gray) and Eugene Ezra Hackman.

Gene" Hackman first married in 1956 to Faye Maltese (1929–2017) with whom he had one son and two daughters. He was often out on location making films while the children were growing up. After three decades of marriage, the couple divorced in 1986.

Gene married secondly in 1991 to a classical pianist and they reside in Santa Fe, New Mexico, USA.
