Dr. Michael Bauer

Chiropractor

Dr. Michael Bauer is the owner and clinic director of West Chicago Family Chiropractic. He earned his Bachelor of Science degree from The University of Toledo and his Doctorate of Chiropractic from National University of Health Sciences in Lombard, Illinois. Dr. Bauer specializes in helping people regain and maintain a healthy and pain-free lifestyle. He is an active member in the community and regularly participates in numerous local events and fundraisers. Dr. Bauer is a member of the Illinois Chiropractic Society and frequently attends seminars to stay current on the most advanced treatment options available for patients suffering from musculoskeletal problems.
