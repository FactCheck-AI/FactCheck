Herbert Henry Asquith, 1st Earl of Oxford and Asquith, was born 12 September 1852 in Croft House, Wind Mill Hill, Morley, Yorkshire, England, United Kingdom to Joseph Dixon Asquith (1825-1860) and Emily Wrigley Willans (1828-1888) and died 15 February 1928 The Wharf Sutton Courtenay, Berkshire, England, United Kingdom of unspecified causes. He married Helen Kelsall Melland (1854-1891) 23 August 1877 in Manchester, Lancashire, England, United Kingdom. He married Emma Alice Magaret Tennant (1864-1945) 10 May 1894 in St George Hanover Square, London, England, United Kingdom.
