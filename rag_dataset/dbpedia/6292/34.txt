“MAHARAJA'S CHILDREN. BRAVE BUNCH IN INDIA” WINS MORE AWARDS

07.03.2023

The multi-media project directed by Tomasz Stankiewicz has recently received several awards and prizes at international film events.

Maharaja's Children. Brave Bunch in India won the Silver Lovie Award and the Audience Lovie Award, i.e. the “Oscars” of the European internet in the "Websites" Best Individual Editorial Experience 2022 category. The project also received the Best Documentary Award at the 5th Aryan Children's Film Festival in Jaipur, India, and the Special Award at the Chinh Festival – also in India.

The production was also longlisted for the prestigious One World Media Awards in the Digital Media Award category.
