Iran uses Iran Standard Time IRST and Iran Daylight Time IRDT. IRST is UTC +0330 and IRDT is UTC +0430. Iran’s time zones are exceptions, most time zone are offset to UTC by a number of whole hours. However, Iran's latitude and longitude is 32° 00' N and 53° 00' E. Therefore a UTC +0330 is perfect for Iran.
