Tricycles are Fun/My Stomach Is Going to Burst/A Nightmare for Dad (三輪車は楽しいゾ/お腹がパンパン痛いゾ/父ちゃんだって大変だゾ) is the 2nd episode of the Crayon Shin-chan anime television series. It aired in Japan on April 20, 1992.
