Doing My Best At Sports Day/Running With Mommy/Winning a Relay (運動会でがんばるゾ/母ちゃんと走るゾ/リレーで勝負だゾ) is the 21st episode of the Crayon Shin-chan anime television series. It aired in Japan on September 21, 1992.
