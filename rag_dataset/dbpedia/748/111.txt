Biography

Charles was born in 1765. He was the son of Charles Mill.

Charles married in 1800, Selina Morshead, eldest daughter of Sir John Morshead, Bart. of Trennant Park, in Cornwall. They did not have any children.

When he died in 1835, the baronetcy became extinct.

The estates passed to his nephew, Rev John Barker, the son of his only sister Mary and John Barker of Wareham, Esq.

The Rev John Barker assumed the surname and arms of Mills in 1835 and was created a Baronet in 1838. He became Sir John Barker Mill, 1st Baronet of Mottisfont in Hampshire.
