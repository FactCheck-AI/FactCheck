Kairana: ICICI Bank Ltd, India’s largest private sector bank, today launched a new branch at Kairana in Shamli district, Uttar Pradesh. The branch, located at 696 Satyam Colony, Shamli Road, Kairana is the Bank’s first in the city and the second in Shamli district.

Mr. S.K Mishra, Sub Divisional Magistrate, Kairana inaugurated this branch while the new ATM was inaugurated by Mr. Haji Naseem Qureshi, Managing Director, A.M.S Agro International. Mr. Mohd. Rashid, Chairman, Municipal Corporation, Kairana lit the ceremonial lamp to mark the occasion.

The branch will remain open for customers from 9:00 am to 6:00 pm on Monday to Friday and 9.00 am to 2.00 pm on Saturday. It will offer the entire gamut of ICICI Bank products including a comprehensive range of deposits and loans in addition to Privilege Banking and locker facilities to cater to different customer profiles and needs.

ICICI Bank has 3,753 branches and extension counters, and over 11,315 ATMs spread across the country as on March 31, 2014. The Bank services its large customer base through a multi-channel delivery network of branches, ATMs, call center and internet banking.
