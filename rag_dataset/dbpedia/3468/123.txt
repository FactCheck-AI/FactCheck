Template:Year nav topic2 Template:Yearsinfilm The year 1914 in film involved some significant events.

Events[]

The 3,300-seat Strand Theater opens in New York City.

February 2 - Charlie Chaplin's first film, Making a Living is released.

May 4 - Release of Charlie Chaplin's Caught In The Rain, the first film entirely written and directed by him.

May 8 - Paramount Pictures is formed.

June 8 - Opening of Ebbets Field Theater, the first drive-in cinema.

Filteo Alberini introduces a new 70 mm format, Panoramico, with the film Il sacco di Roma
