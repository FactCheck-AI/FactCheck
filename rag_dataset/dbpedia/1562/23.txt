EMIL THEODORE HLADISH

Emil T. Hladish, 94, Township of Harmony-Phillips, died on Wednesday, January 6, 2016, at the Aspirus Pleasant View Nursing Home, Phillips.

He was born on September 2, 1921, to proud Czech parents Anton and Frances (Kuchera) Hladish on the family farm in the Township of Harmony. He attended the Lake Ten Grade School. At the age of 14 he became a logger and a milk hauler then went into business with his brother Louis doing bulldozing, logging, and farming.. He was married to Beulah Gayle Hainy on May 13, 1944, in Phillips. He then continued his working career as a logger, farmer, and bulldozer operator as well as became a bus driver for the Phillips School District for 27 years prior to his retirement. Following his retirement he graded roads for the Township of Harmony. In his free time he was an avid baseball fan and especially enjoyed spending time with his family and friends. His wife Beulah Gayle preceded him in death on March 12, 1996.
