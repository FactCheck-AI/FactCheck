Gregory Davis Jr. (also with acting credits under Gregory D. Davis II, Greg Davis, and Klarity) is a African American comedic and commercials actor of Jamaican descent. He is an actor, producer, internet personality and Vine creator, best known for King of California (2007), Beyond the Lights (2014), and L.A. Noire (2011). Davis made a cameo appearance on Youtube series Epic Rap Battles of History as the Zulu Warriors in the episode "Shaka Zulu vs. Julius Caesar". As well, he has guest starred in many shows including Lincoln Heights, The Shield, and CSI: New York.
