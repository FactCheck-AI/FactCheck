Currently, Luca Maestri occupies the position of Chief Financial Officer & Senior Vice President for Apple, Inc. In the past Mr. Maestri occupied the position of Chief Financial Officer & Executive Vice President of Xerox Corp., Chief Financial Officer at Nokia Siemens Networks Oy, Chief Financial Officer of General Motors Ltd., Chief Financial Officer of General Motors do Brasil Ltda., Chief Financial Officer at General Motors (Thailand) Ltd., Chief Financial Officer of General Motors Europe AG, Director-Operations Analysis at General Motors Asia Pacific (Japan) Ltd. and Director-Operations Analysis at General Motors Ireland Ltd. Luca Maestri received a graduate degree from Boston University and an undergraduate degree from The LUISS University of Rome.
