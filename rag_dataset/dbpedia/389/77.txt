Suriya unveiled the first look posters of director Bala’s Naachiyaar on Twitter yesterday. Starring Jyothika and GV Prakash in important roles, the film will have music composed by Isaignani Ilaiyaraja. Suriya tweeted: “Jo you’re always blessed and deserve the best!! Can’t be more happier to tweet First Look of Bala Anna’s #Naachiaar#Jyotika#DirectorBala.”

The shooting kickstarts today and the team is planning to release the movie in September. Meanwhile, there are rumors that the movie is based on a psycho killer named Jayaprakash from the ’80s who murdered nine of his relatives. GV Prakash plays a criminal in the movie and Jo reportedly plays a cop.
