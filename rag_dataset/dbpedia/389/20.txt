Jyothika's upcoming film Kaatrin Mozhi, which is the Tamil remake of Vidya Balan starrer Tumhari Sulu will hit the screens on October 18, 2018 for the pooja holidays. The movie is directed by Radha Mohan and also starrs Vidharth and Lakshmi Manchu in pivotal roles. The techincal crew includes A.H. Kaashif for music, Mahesh Muthuswami for cinematography and Praveen K.L. for the edits. Here are some new stills from the film.
