Jyothika's upcoming movie Raatchasi is hitting the screens worldwide on July 5, 2019. Prime Media has acquired the USA distribution rights of the film. Raatchasi will have its US Premiere on 4th July. The details of the theatre list and showtimes will be released soon by the distributors.

The movie has Jyothika playing the role of a teacher, Geetha Rani, who is on a mission to reform the poorly managed government school she is working for. Raatchasi also stars Poornima Baghyaraj, Sathyan and Hareesh Peradi in key roles. Produced by SR Prabhu under the banner of Dream Warrior Pictures, the film also marks the directorial debut of Gowtham Raj.
