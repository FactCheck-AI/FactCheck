Adorn your Christmas gifts with a beautiful handmade gift tag using the Glad Tidings Stamp & Die Set from Gemini.

This set includes 1 metal die and 6 coordinating stamps that allow you to make a wonderful gift tag brought to life with a festive nutcracker. Simply cut the symmetrical die once, then stamp your character onto the front and back for a perfect, double-sided tag.

Complete your design with the additional sentiment stamps that include 'Wishing You All The Joys of The Season', 'Glad Tidings' and 'Deck The Halls' for a lovely finishing touch to your creation.
