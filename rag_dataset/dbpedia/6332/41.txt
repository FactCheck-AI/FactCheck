For the Harlem, Georgia, radio station that held the call sign WCHZ at 95.1 FM from 1992 to 2011, see WGAC-FM.

WCHZ-FM (93.1 MHz, "Hot 95.5/93.1") is a radio station broadcasting a classic hip hop format. Licensed to Warrenton, Georgia, the station goes by the name "Hot 95.5/93.1" and is owned by Beasley Media Group.
