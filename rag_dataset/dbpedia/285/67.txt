Langarud, Gilan, Iran

Latitude and longitude coordinates are: 37.187820, 50.157520.

Langarud is a large town in the province of Gilan, northern Iran, situated about 4 miles from the Caspian Sea. There are about 20,000 families live in the town, and it is connected with the neighboring towns by a large highway. There are two large parts of the town, divided by Langarud river. The town is famous with boat building companies.
