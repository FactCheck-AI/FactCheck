Burkhardt Söll

Born in 1944. Studied fine arts and composition in Berlin and Paris. Works since 50 years as graphic artist, painter and composer. He arranged pieces of music i.a. for the project Le jardin garde le sourire. At this moment he is preparing a series of etchings with his son Paul du Bois-Reymond and composes a cycle of children songs with English texts of Manuela.

Manuela

du Bois-Reymond (1940-2024)

Born in 1940. Prof. em. of science of education (University of Leiden). Main research interests: life chances of young people in Europe, recently with special attention to migrants. Text writer for musical projects.

Lisanne Wepler

Born in 1980. Art historian and optician. Learned to make etchings and print them. Specially interested in all intermediate tones between black ink and white ground. Loves watching, drawing and etching animals and trees.

Gregor J. M. Weber

Born in Düsseldorf in 1956. Addicted to art history since 50 years. Busy in that field at universities and museums. Used to copy old master paintings to understand the medium of the visual arts. He will again paint, but his own art – he promised.
