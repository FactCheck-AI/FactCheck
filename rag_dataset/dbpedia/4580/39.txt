Boston lawyer Edouard C. LeFevre died on Jan. 13.

He was a member at Mintz, Levin, Cohn, Ferris, Glovsky & Popeo, focusing his practice on M&A transactions, private equity, venture capital and angel financings, according to the firm’s website.

LeFevre was a graduate of Boston College Law School, where he was executive editor of the U.C.C. Reporter-Digest.

He was a dedicated fan of the Boston area’s sports teams and enjoyed fishing trips that took him from the Chesapeake Bay to British Columbia, his family said.
