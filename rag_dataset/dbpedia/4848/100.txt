Beki Jackson began as Pom Coach and Spirit Coordinator in 2013. She came to Oklahoma State University in 2002 to pursue a degree in elementary education. During that time, she choreographed for various dance camps, dance studios, and high school dance teams. Beki was a member of the OSU Pom Squad from 2005-07, and in 2008, began assistant coaching for OSU Pom. She also taught in the Stillwater Public School system for eight years. Beki and her husband, Brandon, have been living in Stillwater since 2008 and have two children. Her responsibilities for OSU Spirit are coaching the Pom squad, coordinating appearances for Pistol Pete, and coordinating community and athletic events for all Spirit Group teams.
