Los Angeles, CA – (September 21, 2015) – Carly Rose Sonenclar releases collaboration song with Timeflies. The song, “Runaway”, which Carly co-wrote and is a featured vocalist on, is part of the group’s latest release Just For Fun. The song is available now on iTunes.

Sixteen year old American singer, songwriter and actress Carly Rose Sonenclar was the runner-up on the second season of the U.S. version of the X FACTOR. Beginning her career in 2006, Carly has performed on Broadway in The Night of the Hunter, Les Miserables and Wonderland. She was named the 2011 best young performer by Broadway.com. Her film and TV credits include Sisterhood of the Traveling Pants 2, The Nanny Diaries, The Electric Company (PBS) and Law & Order: SVU.

In 2014, Seventeen magazine named Carly as one of the most powerful women under twenty-one years old. She is currently working on writing and recording her debut release.
