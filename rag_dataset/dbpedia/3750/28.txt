Wallace “Wally” Robert Lowry, 62, of Lake City, Fla., died unexpectantly on Friday, November 10, 2023 at UnityPoint Health – Trinity, Rock Island. Per his wishes, cremation rites were accorded. There will be no formal services. Wheelan-Pressly Funeral Home and Crematory, Rock Island, is assisting the family.

Wally was born in Geneseo, Illinois on May 25, 1961, a son of Donald Sr. and Sylvia Stevenson Lowry.

Wally was a self-employed in Florida as a pool installer and service technician.

Those left to cherish his memory include his sons, Keith (Jessica) Lowry and their children, Kelvin, Amber, Kami, Kylee and Talon, Chris (Brittany) Lowry and their children, Kendall, Laylee, Bryce, Sora and Ollie and Clint (Holly) Lowry and their son, Carter; and several nieces, nephews and cousins.

He was preceded in death by his parents and his brothers, Donald Lowry Jr. and Richard Lowry.

Online condolences, memories and expressions of sympathy may be left for the family at wheelanpressly.com
