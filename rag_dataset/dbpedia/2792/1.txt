Fast Facts

The youngest of four children, Savas was born in Harlem, New York, and raised in Northern New Jersey

Received his big break at 15 when he was chosen from 15,000 actors for a two-episode role on HBO's The Sopranos

Cofounded the YouTube comedy channel Reckless Tortuga and has acted in numerous Web series, including The Online Gamer, Psycho Girlfriend and Office Douche Bag

Trained with actual Navy SEALs to play the character of PO3 Cossetti on the TNT drama The Last Ship

Added producer to his resume with the feature film Bad Roomies, a dark comedy partially funded by Kickstarter ($75,000 was raised), and starring himself and Patrick Renna
