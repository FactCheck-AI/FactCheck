Oswald Mitchell’s British comedy is the first of the series, in which Old Mother Riley (Arthur Lucan) and her daughter (Kitty McShane) try to stop the plans of some disinherited relatives.

The story concerns Mother Riley (Arthur Lucan) and her daughter Kitty (Kitty McShane) who thwart a strategy of deprived relatives to break a will, which states that two unknowns have to be taken in for half-a-year.

Arthur Lucan and Kitty McShane both give okay performances in their respective parts as the mother-and-daughter involved who manage to stop the plans of the relatives, only for Mother Riley to find herself in the courtroom.
