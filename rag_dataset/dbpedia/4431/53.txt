Centerville, Texas is located in the Central Time Zone, which means that the time is Central Standard Time (CST) during regular time and Central Daylight Time (CDT) when daylight savings is in effect.

Central Standard Time

When it is Central Standard Time, Centerville, Texas is UTC-06:00 which means that it is -6 hours offset from Coordinated Universal Time (UTC).
