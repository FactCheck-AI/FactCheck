Revco Lighting + Electrical Supply

REVCO Lighting + Electrical Supply, Inc. retails lighting and electrical supplies. It offers decorative lighting for the interior/exterior, wireless shades and home automation, tools, light bulbs, and solar/wind products. The company was formerly known as Revco Electrical Supply, Inc. and changed its name to REVCO Lighting + Electrical Supply, Inc. in December 2010. REVCO Lighting + Electrical Supply, Inc. was founded in 1978 and is based in Southampton, New York.
