Guwahati, Assam, India

Latitude and longitude coordinates are: 26.115103, 91.703239.

Guwahati (also known as Gauhati) (গুৱাহাটী) is a large city in Assam, Northeast India. It is one of the oldest cities in the country, and it is also known as the city of temples. Guwahati is a major river port and the commercial center of the state of Assam. The city is home to Gauhati University (GU), the oldest university in northeast India, and located in Jalukbari. There are a lot of educational institutions in Guwahati such as the Indian Institute of Technology Guwahati (IIT), Cotton University, Assam Science and Technology University, Indian Institute of Technology Guwahati, Gauhati Commerce College, and many more.

Local attractions include a number of bazaars and shopping centers like Paltan Bazaar, Beltola Bazaar, Fancy Bazaar, Big Bazaar, Sohum Shoppe, Shine Towers, and a stadium, ACA Stadium. You can also visit Basistha Temple, Jojuwa Waterfall, Maa Kamakhya Temple, Kamakhya Mandir Gate, Bhubaneswari Temple, One-Horned Rhino Statue, Navagraha Temple, Purva Tirupati Shri Balaji Temple, Umananda Temple, Regional Science Center, and Shrimanta Shankardeva Kalakshetra. Lokpriya Gopinath Bordoloi International Airport (or Guwahati Airport) is one of the busiest airports in the country and 28 km (18 miles) away from the city center.
