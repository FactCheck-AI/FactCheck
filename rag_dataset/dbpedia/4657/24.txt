Movie poster artwork is a genre in and of itself. While final movie poster versions contain a plethora of text and information, there’s a growing demand for ‘textless’ movie posters that let fans enjoy just the artwork. They also make for great wallpapers and background images for your phone!

In fact, there are huge archives online (1000+ here) of textless movie posters on forums and message boards. There are also two huge Imgur albums (here and here) that feature high-res versions of the movie posters below.

Take a trip down memory lane and admire these movie poster artworks!
