Located in St. John’s, Antigua and Barbuda, Canada Place is a state-of-the-art US$40 million investment into the Antiguan economy by business magnate and philanthropist, Calvin Ayre.

The five-story, multi-tenant complex is home to many of Antigua’s leading high-tech firms, while one floor is dedicated to the Ayre Group’s various pioneering technology companies.

Canada Place is designed to take care of the people who work there and the environment. The environmentally friendly building has a solar farm that can power the entire building and wellness amenities such as an emergency child care center, a fitness center, and a restaurant.
