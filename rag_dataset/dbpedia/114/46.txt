Aster Park is a new master-planned community conveniently located in McKinney with easy access to nearby Prosper. It is nestled just 10 miles from the charming and historic downtown area that boasts more than 120 shops, restaurants, and galleries. This luxury gated 414-acre community with multiple high-end neighborhood amenities for residents to enjoy, including private gated entrances, a state-of-the-art clubhouse, a resort-style pool, and an extensive trail system. Aster Park is zoned to the highly sought-after and award-winning Prosper ISD!
