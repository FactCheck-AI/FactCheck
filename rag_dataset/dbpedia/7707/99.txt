ABOUT THE FILMS:

DUCK SOUP, 1933, Leo McCarey, Universal, USA

In this screwball comedy, Groucho Marx plays a newly installed dictator of the bankrupt Freedonia. Bordering Sylvania senses a weakness in the country’s leadership and sends two totally inept spies, played by Harpo and Chico Marx, to set the stage for all out mayhem.

FORMAT: DCP

THE COCOANUTS, 1929, Robert Florey, Joseph Stantley, Universal, USA

While trying to keep his Hotel de Cocoanuts afloat, owner Hammer (Groucho Marx), flatters his only paying guest, Mrs. Potter (Margaret Dumont) while other guests also compete for her affection. Brothers Harpo and Chico, played by Harpo and Chico Marx, wreak havoc around the hotel while Hammer’s back is turned.

FORMAT: DCP
