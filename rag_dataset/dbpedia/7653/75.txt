American drag queen RuPaul, 57, was born in San Diego. He was a recording artist and talk show host before launching his reality show, RuPaul’s Drag Race, for which he has won three Emmy awards. He married Australian rancher Georges LeBar last year and lives in Beverly Hills.

I always wanted to be famous and I always knew I would be. My mother saw a psychic when she was pregnant, who told her it would happen. There were times when I doubted it. For 11 years, I didn’t make a dime. When I was 28, I was sleeping on my sister’s couch. I was walking in Los Angeles. And you must have a car in LA.
