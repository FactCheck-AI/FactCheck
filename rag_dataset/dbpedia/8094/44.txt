The Olainfarm Excellence Scholarship was founded in 2011. It includes four scholarships and placement opportunities at AS Olainfarm for students of RSU Faculty of Pharmacy. The purpose of the student scholarship is to financially support the most successful and talented students and to offer them the possibility to learn about career opportunities after graduation from the university. Scholarship holders are selected via a competition.

The AS Olainfarm Excellence Scholarship is 200 euros a month for ten months of studies. Those citizens and residents of the Republic of Latvia who are 4th year RSU Faculty of Pharmacy students may be eligible for the scholarship.
