Since 2015, BOSS has been official fashion partner to Real Madrid C.F.. Founded in 1902, the historic team is the most successful club in European Cup history and renowned worldwide. The club has won a record 33 “Primera División” titles, as well as 13 European UEFA Champions League titles, four European Super Cups and seven FIFA Club World Cups. Furthermore Real Madrid C.F. is recognised as the ‘The Best Club of the 20th Century’ by FIFA.

Real Madrid C.F. wears sharp-cut BOSS tailoring when off the pitch, underscoring their drive, skill and accomplishments in the world of football. The team is one of three founding members of La Liga never to have been relegated from the top division, and it is one of the most widely supported in the world.
