Nicolas Louis André Prévost was a Swiss 19th Century artist who was born in 1817. Nicolas Louis André Prévost's work has been offered at auction multiple times, with realized prices ranging from 325 USD to 2,864 USD, depending on the size and medium of the artwork. Since 2016 the record price for this artist at auction is 2,864 USD for View of a City by a River, sold at Lempertz, Cologne in 2022. The artist died in 1864.
