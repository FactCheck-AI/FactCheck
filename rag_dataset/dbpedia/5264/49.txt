Pentair plc, a water treatment company, announced that it completed the acquisition of Pelican Water Systems for $120 million in cash.

Headquartered in DeLand, Florida and founded in 2007., Pelican Water Systems has 79 employees and provides residential whole home water treatment systems.

“We are excited about the addition of Pelican Water Systems for the new growth opportunities it brings to Pentair and for the innovative products and services we can bring to consumers, enhancing where, how and when they can buy their water treatment solutions,” said John L. Stauch, Pentair President and CEO.

Pentair has approximately 11,250 global employees and provides water solutions to homes, businesses and industries worldwide.
