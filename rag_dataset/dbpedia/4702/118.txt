Go kart racing at K1 Speed Carlsbad has sure come a long way from the homemade wood versions your dad built in his garage! Now, go karts are sleek, sophisticated, and fast! At K1 Speed, you’ll feel like you’re behind the wheel of a race car! Kart racing at K1 Speed is a thrilling, can’t miss experience for the race car driver in all of us. K1 Speed, with a location in Downtown San Diego and another in Carlsbad, has age-appropriate racing for everyone, so your kids can race too!

San Diego County is the spiritual home of K1 Speed. Their first-ever location was opened in Carlsbad in 2003, and they have since been drawing racing fans from all over San Diego. They are pleased to announce with the opening of the downtown San Diego indoor karting center their fans no longer have to make the trek up the 15 or 5 freeways! The K1 Speed San Diego center brings indoor go-kart racing to the heart of America’s Finest City! K1 Speed invites you to come down today – it’s the same thrilling experience, much closer to home.
