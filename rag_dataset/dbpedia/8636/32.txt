June 10, 1942-Nov. 10, 2012

Rosetta Frances Lennon died Saturday at Wayne Memorial Hospital.

Funeral will be Saturday at 1 p.m. at Jerusalem Free Will Baptist Church in Seven Springs. Interment will be in the church cemetery.

She was born June 10, 1942, to the late Bernard and Alma Lofton in Wilson County.

She is survived by her children.

Visitation will be Friday from 4 to 7 p.m. at the funeral home.

Arrangements are by E.M. Matthews Funeral Home and Cremation Inc.
