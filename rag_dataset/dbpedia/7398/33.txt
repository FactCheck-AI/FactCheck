A brief report about Renowned Broadcaster, Writer and Journalist Rehan Alavis Unique Book with Unique presentation and inauguration

The Sydney launch of Rehan Alavis book Ek Ghazal Ek Kahani was held on the afternoon of Sunday 22 October 2017 at The Connection in Rhodes with the presence of politicians, media personalities and community leaders. Author/Journalist/Poet Rehan Alavi celebrated the publication of EK GHAZAL EK KAHANI (Collection of authors prose and poetry). Mobinah Ahmad, provides photographs to Sada-e-Watan.

Rehan Alavi

Rehan Alavi is writing fiction for more than two decades. He came across with thousands of real-life stories during his career as radio journalist and executive producer since he migrated from Pakistan to Australia fourteen years back. Professionally, he is an IT operations analyst in an American multinational. Rehan graduated from University of Technology, Sydney but equally loves to live behind the microphone besides writing on social and political topics. Rehan has contributed articles and blogs to a variety of magazines and written many short stories. Several of his popular fictions are published in Urdu language. He spends his time mixing with and writing about some of the shadiest characters of real life. He loves social drama and the artistic suspense of breaking moments that reflects in his stories and grips the reader. Rehan is married and settled in Sydney with his wife and two daughters.

The Prominent Australian Pakistani Poet and head of SBS Urdu service Mr. Rehan Alavis book launch was attended by well-known respectable dignitaries and high profile personalities. Various literary and academic personalities spoken on the occasion. Event was dedicated to the diversified values of Australia and showcased Pakistani/Urdu literary contribution of Pakistanis living in Australia.
