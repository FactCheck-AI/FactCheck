Photograph of Alfonso, Prince of the Asturias, 1931

Photographic portrait of Alfonso, Prince of Asturias, (1907-1938) - heir apparent to the throne of Spain from 1907 to 1931. The newly-proclaimed Second Spanish Republic had sent the Royal Family into exile, and the Prince was studying at Dartmouth Naval College " with a view to entering in the British Navy, unless events in Spain take a turn."
