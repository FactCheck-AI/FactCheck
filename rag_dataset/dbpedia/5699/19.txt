The death has occurred of William Joseph (Bill) O'Donovan of Kilbarrack, Dublin.

O’Donovan William Joseph (Bill), (Kilbarrack, Dublin 5) – January 3rd, 2020 – in the wonderful care and compassion of the staff at St. Francis Hospice, Raheny, after a long illness borne with extraordinary positivity and good humour. Loving son of the late Donal and Gerry and beloved brother of Pauline and Barry. Very sadly missed by his sister-in-law Gaye, Conor and Claire, Oísin and Karen-Ann, Labhaoise and Alvaro, Kate, Conor, Cillian, Charlie and Oscar, extended family, relatives, neighbours and friends.

Bill will be reposing at the Kirwan Funeral Home, Fairview Strand on Monday (Jan. 6th) from 5pm to 7pm, with family in attendance. Removal to St. John the Evangelist Church, Kilbarrack on Tuesday (Jan. 7th) arriving at 9.50am for 10am Requiem Mass followed by cremation at Glasnevin Crematorium. Family flowers only please, donations in lieu, if desired to St. Francis Hospice, Raheny.

“May he Rest in Peace”
