A powerful and flexible application designed to convert audio and video files between different media formats. It has very flexible settings and allows you to transcode media files without loss of quality.

MediaCoder supports almost all existing codecs, and is constantly updated. With it, you can convert files between all popular audio and video formats. In addition, the program allows you to "rip" BD, DVD, VCD and CD discs, and capture video directly from the camera.

MediaCoder has a variety of filters with which you can improve the quality of audio or video files. And flexible parameters will give you full control over your media file.
