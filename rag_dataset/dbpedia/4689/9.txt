As Executive Vice President and Controller, Bruce Herman oversees all administrative, financial, investment and treasury management for New England Development and its family of companies and Family Office. Bruce manages all reporting requirements for various joint venture partners and third-party management contracts. He joined NED in 1983, later serving as Chief Financial Officer at Liberty Properties in Boston, before returning to the company in 1990.

Bruce is a graduate of Bentley College and is a member of the American Institute of Certified Public Accountants, the Massachusetts Society of Certified Public Accountants, and the ICSC. He has also served on the Town of Needham Council of Economic Advisors and was Treasurer of the Needham Sports Council.
