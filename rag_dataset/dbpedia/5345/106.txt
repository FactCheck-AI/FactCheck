Chelsea Clinton is the only child of former President Bill Clinton and former U.S. Secretary of State and presidential candidate Hillary Clinton.

Clinton was born in Little Rock, Arkansas, during her father's first term as governor. She received her B.A from Stanford, an MPH from Columbia's Mailman School of Public Health, and both an M.Phil. and doctorate in international relations from Oxford University.

Clinton previously worked for McKinsey & Company and Avenue Capital Group. She was a special correspondent for NBC News from 2011 to 2014. Since 2011, she has had a seat on the board of the Clinton Foundation and Clinton Global Initiative, and teaches at Columbia University's Mailman School of Public Health. In addition, she serves on the boards of the School of American Ballet, Common Sense Media, Weill Cornell Medical College and IAC/InterActiveCorp.

Clinton campaigned for her mother during her 2008 and 2016 presidential campaigns.
