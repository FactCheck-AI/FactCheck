Guitarist and singer-songwriter Andy Roberts talks about his journey in music and his 50 year collaboration with Iain Matthews and Plainsong. In this extensive podcast we also cover his time with The Scaffold and Liverpool Scene in the 1960s and as well as playing with The Bonzo Dog Band, Pink Floyd, Roy Harper and Cat Stevens.
