OFTC assists and guides students from all walks of life to increase their career readiness and earning power. OFTC offers more than 100 associate degree, diploma and certificate programs, in addition to, specialized training through continuing education, contract training, and workshops. The Oconee Fall Line Technical College Adult Education program offers services such as adult basic education (ABE), GED preparation and testing, English Literacy Programs (ELP), and Workplace Literacy Programs. Services are offered at many convenient locations throughout the 11-county service area.
