The Principality or, from 1253, Kingdom of Galicia–Volhynia,[lower-alpha 1] also known as the Kingdom of Ruthenia,[better source needed][lower-alpha 2] was a medieval state in Eastern Europe which existed from 1199 to 1349. Its territory was predominantly located in modern-day Ukraine, with parts in Belarus, Poland, Moldova, and Lithuania. Along with Novgorod and Vladimir-Suzdal, it was one of the three most important powers to emerge from the collapse of Kievan Rus'.
