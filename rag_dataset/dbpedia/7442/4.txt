The best time to visit the area around Darunta Dam, considering the climate of Jalalabad, is during the spring (March to May) and autumn (September to November). These seasons feature mild temperatures that are more suitable for sightseeing and outdoor activities.

Tourists in the area can enjoy sightseeing, exploring local culture and historical sites, and sampling the region's fresh fruits. However, recreational activities directly at the dam are likely to be limited due to its operational nature.
