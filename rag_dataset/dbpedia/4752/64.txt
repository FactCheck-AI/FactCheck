INDIANAPOLIS--(BUSINESS WIRE)-- Republic Airways Holdings Inc. (NAS: RJET) announced today it will hold a conference call to discuss financial results for the fourth quarter and full year 2012. The conference call will be held Thursday, February 28, at 10:30 a.m. EST.

The call will be webcast by Thomson/Reuters and can be accessed at Republic Airways Holdings' website at rjet.com. Those wishing to participate should call 800-901-5259 (international callers dial +1-617-786-4514); the password is 80290913. To listen to a telephone replay of the webcast, call 888-286-8010 and use password 54438962. International telephone replay will be available by calling +1-617-801-6888 and using the same password. The replay will be available from Feb. 28, at 12:30 p.m. until 11:59 p.m. Mar. 7, 2012.

The webcast is also being distributed through the Thomson StreetEvents Network. Individual investors can listen to the call at earnings.com, Thomson's individual investor portal, powered by StreetEvents. Institutional investors can access the call via Thomson StreetEvents (www.streetevents.com), a password-protected event management site.

Republic Airways Holdings, based in Indianapolis, Indiana, is an airline holding company that owns Chautauqua Airlines, Frontier Airlines, Republic Airlines and Shuttle America, collectively "the airlines." The airlines operate a combined fleet of more than 270 aircraft and offer scheduled passenger service on nearly 1,500 flights daily to over 145 cities in the U.S. as well as to the Bahamas, Canada, Costa Rica, Dominican Republic, Jamaica, Mexico and Turks and Caicos Islands under branded operations at Frontier, and through fixed-fee flights operated under airline partner brands, including AmericanConnection, Continental Express, Delta Connection, United Express, and US Airways Express. The airlines currently employ approximately 10,000 aviation professionals. For more information on Republic Airways, please visit our website at www.rjet.com.
