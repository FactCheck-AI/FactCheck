earMUSIC proudly announces that it will release the new Status Quo album “In Search For The Fourth Chord” in the United Kingdom. “In Search For The Fourth Chord” was the band´s 33rd album and for this album, the band reunited with Pip Williams, the man who produced the likes of Rockin' All Over The World, Whatever You Want and In The Army Now.

Status Quo is the one of the most durable and successful rock bands in the history of music. Quo sold more than 118 million records world-wide. They have recorded 63 British hit singles, 22 of which have hit the Top Ten. Quo have spent in excess seven and a half years (415 weeks) in the British Single Charts – The eleventh highest ever. They have had more hit albums in the British Album Charts than any other band apart from The Rolling Stones.
