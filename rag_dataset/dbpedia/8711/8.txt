Early life

She was born and brought up in Kolkata. She has done her schooling from Carmel Convent School. She completed her graduation in BA from Jogamaya Devi College in southern Kolkata. This Bengali beauty has grown up from being a very shy girl to a confident model and an aspiring actress. She has a younger sister.

Personal life

Reshmi married to Siddharth Vasudev, her co-star from Shobha Somnath Ki on 1 February 2013 at Kolkata. Reshmi and Siddharth Vasudev are also seen as on-screen couple in Buddha.
