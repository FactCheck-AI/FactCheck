BPI France SA agreed to acquire 12.23% stake in Peugeot S.A. (ENXTPA:UG) from Agence des participations de l'Etat for €1.9 billion on March 27, 2017. France would retain its two voting board seats and reclaim its statutory double voting rights two years after the change of ownership of Peugeot. The transaction will take effect after Peugeot's annual shareholder meeting on May 10, 2017. Daniel Hurstel, Gabriel Flandin, Grégoire Dumazy, Faustine Viala, Mathilde Ayel and Emilie Patoux of Willkie Farr & Gallagher LLP acted as legal advisors to BPI France. Olivier Diaz, Charles de Reals and Corentin Charlès of Skadden, Arps, Slate, Meagher & Flom LLP acted as legal advisors to Agence des participations de l’Etat. BPI France SA completed the acquisition of 12.23% stake in Peugeot S.A. (ENXTPA:UG) from Agence des participations de l'Etat on June 20, 2017.
