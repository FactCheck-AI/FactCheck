Virginia Mae "Ginny" Williams 75 of Mannington passed away on Thursday, June 25, 2020 at her residents with her family surrounding her. Virginia was born February 11, 1945 in Keyser, WV the daughter of the late Melvin Barr and Virginia Harrington Barr.

Along with her parents, Virginia was preceded in death by her husband Donald Williams.

Virginia is survived by one daughter Gwenda Fetty husband Mark of Mannington, one son Terry Lee Williams and wife Jessica of Clarksburg, two granddaughters Clarissa Shaw of North Carolina, Quintessa Scott husband Justin of Enterprise, three grandsons Seth Fetty of Mannington, Donald Williams wife Kaitlyn of East View Terry Williams Jr. of Wallace, one sister Sandra Willis husband Gene of North Carolina, five great-grandsons Cedrick, Seth Jr., Eli, Wesly, Leeland, five great-granddaughters Mackenzie, Sasha, Marissa, Jaylee and Maleea.

Virginia was a baker at Eat-N-Park restaurant in Bridgeport, WV.

Per Virginias request she will be cremated, memorial services will be held at Dorsey Funeral Home 701 South Pike Street, Shinnston, WV on Wednesday, July 1, 2020, 10 AM- 11AM. Interment will follow at Marshville Cemetery in Marshville, WV.

Dorsey Funeral Home is honored to be serving the Williams family. Online condolences and donations can be made at www.dorseyfuneralhomewv.com
