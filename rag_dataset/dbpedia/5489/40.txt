Whitworth University is a private, liberal arts university in Spokane, Washington, United States. Founded in 1890, Whitworth is affiliated with the Presbyterian Church (USA) and offers bachelor's and master's degrees in a variety of academic disciplines. The university is nationally recognized for its commitment to academic excellence, diversity, and service.
