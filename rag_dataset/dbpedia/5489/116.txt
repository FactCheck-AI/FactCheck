Whitworth University is a private Christian liberal arts college located in Spokane, Washington, United States. Founded in 1890, Whitworth is a private, residential, liberal arts institution affiliated with the Presbyterian church. Whitworth's community of teacher-scholars is committed to rigorous and open intellectual inquiry and to the integration of Christian faith and learning.‌ The university offers bachelor's degrees and master's degrees in a variety of academic disciplines. The university, which has an enrollment of nearly 3,000 students, offers over 100 undergraduate and graduate programs
