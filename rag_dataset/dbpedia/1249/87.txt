An incredible opportunity to hear Professor Balázs Szokolay at The Stoller Hall for the first time. A prizewinner at many international piano and chamber music competitions, he is now also a hugely respected teacher. Currently Professor of Piano at the Franz Liszt Academy of Music in Weimar, his students have won more than 50 international prizes in different music competitions.
