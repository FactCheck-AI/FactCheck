Remember back in March, when we told you Jonathan Lethem was giving away the film option to his latest novel, You Don’t Love Me Yet? Well, he’s picked a filmmaker upon whom to bestow that gift: writer-director Greg Marcks (right), who helmed the star-studded 11:14 a few years back. “Greg’s ideas for the film are really terrific,” Lethem writes on his website, “and I’m excited to see him try to make it.”
