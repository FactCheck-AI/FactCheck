Michael C. Wenzel, beloved Husband, Father, Grandpa, Great Grandpa, Brother, and Son, passed away November 25, 2023.

Mike was born January 6, 1931 in Milwaukee, Wisconsin. He married the love of his life Patricia in 1951 and they recently celebrated their 71st Wedding Anniversary. Mike was a Fireman for the West Allis Fire Department before moving to California. He began his career in Finance with IBM and moved to Colorado in 1966. He retired from IBM in 1993 and worked several part-time jobs to keep busy.

Mike enjoyed watching football and spending time with his family. He is preceded in death by his parents, brother Charles, daughter Debbie, and 2 grandsons Michael and Matthew. He is survived by his wife Patricia, sons Mike and Mark (Bang), daughter Diane (Darrell), granddaughters Tina and Jessica, great-granddaughter Bryleigh, and sister Marilyn (Don).

No services will be held at this time.
