Guardian Media Group is amongst the world’s leading media organisations with a global, progressive audience.

Our main business is Guardian News & Media, which is the publisher of theguardian.com, one of the largest English-speaking quality news websites in the world. We also have a presence in the US and Australia as Guardian US and Guardian Australia. In the UK, we publish the Guardian newspaper six days a week and the world’s oldest Sunday newspaper, The Observer.

We are owned by The Scott Trust. Our ownership structure is unique and exists to secure the financial and editorial independence of the Guardian in perpetuity. It safeguards our journalistic freedom and liberal values, ensuring they remain free from commercial or political interference.
