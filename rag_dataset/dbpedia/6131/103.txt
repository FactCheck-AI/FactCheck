Featuring a grand 2-storey food & retail podium that is conveniently located just above the one-north Mass Rapid Transport (MRT) Station, as well as below the offices, Galaxis has a lot to offer.

Filled with a multitude of gastro bars, cafes, bakeries, restaurants as well as a food court, the selection of food and beverage options here is vast, from classic western food to the familiar taste of traditional malay food. To top it all off, Galaxis also has a great array of retail amenities and services, such as the renowned Myeongdong Hair Studio for your hair needs.
