Swansea midfielder Gylfi Sigurðsson scored both goals Monday as Iceland pulled off a shock 2-0 defeat of the Netherlands in a European Championship qualifier.

After the win, documentarian Sölvi Tryggvason caught this footage of the team celebrating in the locker room.

It was Iceland’s first ever victory over the Netherlands in 11 matches.

The win kept Iceland perfect in three qualifiers and left us level atop Group A with the Czech Republic.
