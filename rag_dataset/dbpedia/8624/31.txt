The death occurred at the

Ballina District Hospital after

a short illness of Noel Arthur

Anderson, aged 17, second

youngest son of Mr. and Mrs.

Ray Anderson of Martin

Street, Ballina. He is survived

by his parents and three

brothers, Clarrie and Glenn

(Ballina) and Gordon

(R.A.A.F., Mascot). One

brother, Keith, predeceased him

nine years ago.
