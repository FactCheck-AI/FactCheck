Ferdinand Philippe, Duke of Orleans is a work by German artist Franz Xavier Winterhalter (1805 - 1873). It's one portrait oil on canvas painting created in 1843. The painting is owned by the museum of Château de Versailles, and is accessible to the general public. Franz Xavier Winterhalter was influenced by Romanticism and Neoclassicism, and made most paintings about portrait and figure and tableau.
