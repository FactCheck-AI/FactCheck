The Phantom of Haunted Isle is a villian in the Scooby-Doo World.

First Appearance: Hassle In The Castle Identity: Bluestone The Great

Physical Appearance[]

The phantom is a white sheet ghost with two black eyes.

Appearances[]

Scooby-Doo, Where Are You! []

The phantom first appeared in the episode Hassle In The Castle. The gang met him on haunted isle at a pirate's castle. When they first saw him, he ordered them to "leave haunted isle and never return!". Later, in the episode, when Scooby catchs the phantom he is revealed to be ex-magiacan BlueStone the Great, who was looking for the pirate's treasure.

Videogames[]

Scooby-Doo And The Cyber Chase[]

The Phantom appears in the Scooby-Doo And The Cyber Chase game, despite the fact that he doesn't appear in the Scooby-Doo And The Cyber Chase movie.

Scooby-Doo! Night of 100 Frights[]

The Phantom also appears in every areas of Night of 100 Frights. He is the only villian who can't be killed.
