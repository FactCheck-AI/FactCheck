About this school

Westfield Middle School is a suburban public school in Bloomingdale, Illinois that is part of Bloomingdale School District 13. It serves 494 students in 6 - 8 with a student/teacher ratio of 13.0:1. Its teachers have had one project funded on DonorsChoose.
