"Harder, Better, Faster, Stronger" is a song by the French electronic music duo Daft Punk, and was used, along with the rest of Daft Punk's second album, Discovery, as part of the soundtrack to Interstella 5555: The 5tory of the 5ecret 5tar 5ystem.

A live version of "Harder, Better, Faster, Stronger" was released from the album Alive 2007 as a single on October 15, 2007. The Alive 2007 version of the song was nominated to 2008 Grammy Award for Best Dance Recording. The Alive 2007 version of the song features parts of Daft Punk's songs "Television Rules the Nation" and "Around the World".
