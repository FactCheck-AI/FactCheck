Ian Hicks who is also known as “DJ Hixxy” is a British Happy Hardcore musician and DJ. He is from Portsmouth England, and was born on the 3rd August 1975.

From a young age Hixxy’s route to become a professional DJ came through an early obession with US Hip-Hop & Rap music.

At the age of 15 he had a passion for Rave Music. He joined with other DJs to perform on the South coast of England.

In 1992 he created his own music label for happy hardcore music and has called it “Raver Baby”

He hit the big time with the song “Toytown” which he collaborated with DJ Sharkey on the “Bonkers” album, which was released in 1996. He is also known for other songs “More & More,” “Together Forever” and “Sea Of Love”
