Full Name: Joaquin Rafael Bottom

Profession: Actor

Biography: A member of the famous Phoenix family, including brother River Phoenix and sister Summer Phoenix, he has received international acclaim for films such as To Die For (2005), Quills (2000) and Gladiator (2000).

He has received Academy Award nominations for his role in Walk the Line (2005) where he portrayed Johnny Cash, and for his role as a World War II veteran in the Paul Thomas Anderson film The Master (2012). Other notable roles include Signs (2002), Inherent Vice (2014) and The Village (2004).

Aside from acting Phoenix is also a producer and activist, particularly for animal rights.

Born: October 28, 1974

Birthplace: San Juan, Puerto Rico, USA

Age: 49 years old
