Sony Pictures is set to release the trailer tomorrow for the highly mysterious sci-fi thriller “65,” starring Oscar nominee Adam Driver.

Ariana Greenblatt and Chloe Coleman co-star in the $91 million-budgeted film, which only has a very brief synopsis available” “An astronaut crash lands on a mysterious planet and discovers that he is not alone”.

The project hails from producer Sam Raimi whilst “A Quiet Place” scribes Scott Beck & Bryan Woods are writing and directing. Shot around Louisiana, Danny Elfman is doing the film’s score.

“65” is slated to hit cinemas on March 10th next year.
