We are one of the prominent suppliers of quality Mono Sodium Glutamate (AJINOMOTO), which is commonly used as a flavour enhancer. Our vendor's process this chemical by the fermentation of molasses or starch. Exclusively used in all food preparations like meats, gravies, sambhar and sauces, AJINOMOTO is widely demanded amongst our clients. AJINOMOTO is also called Sodium Glutamate and MSG and is a sodium salt of the naturally occurring non-essential amino acid glutamic acid.
