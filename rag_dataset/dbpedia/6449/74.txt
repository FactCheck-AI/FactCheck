Christopher Shannon Penn (born October 10, 1965 in Los Angeles, California â€“ died January 24, 2006 in Santa Monica, California) was an American film actor, voice actor and television actor.

Career[]

Penn has appeared in movies such as Reservoir Dogs, All the Right Moves, and Beethoven's 2nd (along with Debi Mazar).

He also appeared in TV shows such as Faerie Tale Theatre, The Young Riders, CSI: Miami, AFP: American Fighter Pilot, Law & Order: Criminal Intent, The Brotherhood of Poland, New Hampshire, Chicago Hope and Will & Grace.

He provided the voice of C.R.A.S.H. police officer Eddie Pulaski in Grand Theft Auto: San Andreas.

His brothers are two time Academy Award winning actor Sean Penn and the musician Michael Penn. On January 24, 2006, Penn died of a heart disease exacerbated by prescription drugs at the age of 40 in Santa Monica, California.
