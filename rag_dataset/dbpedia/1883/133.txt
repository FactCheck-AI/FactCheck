Mobb Deep put a spin on their 20 year old record ‘Survival Of The Fittest’ which will serve the purpose of the NBA Countdown opening music during the Western Conference Finals. Along with the new song, they announce that a Survival Of The Fittest EP will drop on Monday which will be 5 songs long. You can listen to the new remix below and check out the tracklist for the EP below.

1. Hide Away Produced By Havoc

2. Survival Of The Fittest (ESPN Remix) Produced By Havoc & Additional Keys By Yung Fokus

3. Whats Going On Produced By Havoc

4. Life Is What You Make It Produced By S.C.

5. All About It Produced By Yung Fokus
