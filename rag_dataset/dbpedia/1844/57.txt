Magadan Climate Summary

Located at an elevation of 54.02 meters (177.23 feet) above sea level, Magadan has a Subartic, severe winter, no dry season, cool summer climate (Classification: Dfc). The city’s yearly temperature is -2.07ºC (28.27ºF) and it is -7.34% lower than Russia’s averages. Magadan typically receives about 77.36 millimeters (3.05 inches) of precipitation and has 113.63 rainy days (31.13% of the time) annually.
