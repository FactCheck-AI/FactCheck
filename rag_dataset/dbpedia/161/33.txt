Mr. and Mrs. Ramachari is a 2014 Kannada-language Drama Romance movie written by Santhosh Ananddram and Santosh Ananddram. The movie is directed by Santhosh Ananddram and produced by Jayana Bhogendra and Jayanna under the banner of Jayanna Combines. A man whose life ms straight out of a classic Kannada movie faces highs and lows in life and love. Mr. and mrs. ramachari features Yash in the title role along with an ensemble cast including Radhika Pandit, Achyuth Kumar, Srinath, Ashok Sharma, Malavika Avinash and Aruna Balaraj. The screenplay is written by Santhosh Ananddram and Vijay Naagendra. The music was composed by V. Harikrishna. Cinematography was done by Vaidy S and editing by K. M. Prakash. The film has a running time of 160 minutes. It was released on 25th December 2014. The Film was successful at box office. Mr. and Mrs. Ramachari was made on a budget of ₹6 crore and it was a super hit at box office gross of ₹50 crore. Distribution rights for the film were acquired by Jayanna Combines.
