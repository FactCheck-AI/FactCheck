What’s the climate like in Zurich?

The current climate in Zurich features temperatures ranging from a high of 26°C to a low of 5°C throughout the year. The average yearly temperature is around 16°C. At night, temperatures in the colder months average around 0°C and in the warmer months around 15°C.

The highest temperature recorded in Zurich in recent years was 36°C in August 2023. The lowest daytime temperature recorded was -9°C in February 2018.

On average, Zurich gets 1026 mm of precipitation each year, including rain, snow, and hail. Of this, about 68 cm is snowfall. For comparison, New York gets 833 mm of precipitation each year.
