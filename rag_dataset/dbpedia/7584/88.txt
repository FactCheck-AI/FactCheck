What’s the climate like in Zurich?

The current climate in Zurich features temperatures ranging from a high of 30°C to a low of -4°C throughout the year. The average yearly temperature is around 13°C. At night, temperatures in the colder months average around -15°C and in the warmer months around 13°C.

The highest temperature recorded in Zurich in recent years was 41°C in August 2018. The lowest daytime temperature recorded was -28°C in December 2022.

On average, Zurich gets 520 mm of precipitation each year, including rain, snow, and hail. Of this, about 208 cm is snowfall. For comparison, New York gets 833 mm of precipitation each year.

The best time to visit Zurich for sunny weather is May through September. During this period, you'll have good temperatures and not too much rain, making it a great time to visit Zurich. In the other months of the year, there's too much rain or the temperature isn't ideal for a visit if you want sunny and pleasant weather.
