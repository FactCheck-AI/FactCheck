Volume 3 of Devilman: The First (デビルマン ザ ファースト) a manga written and illustrated by Go Nagai. It was published in Shōnen Magazine between June 1972 and June 1973.

This manga tells the story of Akira Fudo a kind-hearted young man who is possessed by the demon Amon, also known to be the warlord. From this moment on, Akira's life will change and he will have to face other demons who are organizing a war on earth.
