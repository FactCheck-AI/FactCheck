Manama, Mar. 3 -- (BNA): National Guard Staff Director Major-General Shaikh Abdulaziz bin Saud Al Khalifa met the Commandant General of British Royal Marines Major-General Martin Smith and his accompanying delegation.

The Director of Staff welcomed Major-General Martin Smith, praising the constant growth and development of cooperation in various fields.

He affirmed the National Guard Presidium's aspiration for further military cooperation between the National Guard and British Royal Marines, and explored with him means capable of developing this cooperation through the exchange of expertise and conducting joint exercises.

His Highness Staff Major Shaikh Salman bin Mohammed bin Isa Al Khalifa the Commander of the Special Operations Unit attended the meeting.

The Director of Staff and Major-General Smith exchanged shields.
