Biography

Stephen Eugene Low/Lowe Jr. was born in St. Louis, Missouri in 1871. He was the son of Stephen Low and Mary Peaslee.

He went to France in WWI with the 35th Division and after WWI served in Armenia. In 1923, Colonel Stephen Lowe was director of American Near East Relief at Corfu.

Colonel Stephen Lowe was commander of the 138th Infantry, Missouri National Guard. Served with the National Guard on the Mexican border.

Buried Lakeview Cemetery, Sarnia, Ontario, Canada.
