The Shining Victory, a combat Lightsaber with incredible maneuverability

If this lightsaber is appreciated by many fans, it is not only for its sparkling design. In addition to standing out from the iconic sabers of the saga, this piece is perfectly suited for duels. The handle is light and ergonomic, making it suitable for intense and repeated movements.

The model has been designed mostly with aluminum alloy to stand the test of time, while offering an elegant visual. As for the polycarbonate blade, it will not break despite heavy blows during fights.

Considered as effective in confrontation as in collection, this lightsaber is equipped with an RGB LED. This one offers the possibility to brandish a blade of 12 different colors according to its mood.
