The 2-star Sharon Ultimate Hotels Wupa, situated 3.6 km from Abuja National Mosque, offers meeting rooms for corporate guests. Located 10 minutes by car from Magic Land Abuja Amusement Park, this Wupa hotel features 110 rooms with views of the city.

The accommodation is about 40 km from Nnamdi Azikiwe International airport and a 15-minute ride from such cultural venues as Thought Pyramid Art Centre. Amigo supermarket stands approximately a 10-minute drive from the hotel.

The Sharon Ultimate Hotels Wupa offers breakfast in the restaurant.
