The 30th Space Wing announced its annual awards for Team V and the wing at the Pacific Coast Club on Feb. 1. The awards are given to Airmen and civilians that distinguish themselves from their peers by working hard everyday all year long, while demonstrating professionalism, integrity and excellence.
