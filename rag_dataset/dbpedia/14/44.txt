Q. Sultan Muzaffar Shah defeated which of the following kings to capture Dhar?

Answer: Husang Shah

Notes: Zafar Khan assumed the title of Sultan Muzaffar Shah and ruled Gujarat upto 1411 CE. His reign was marked by a prolonged conflict between Gujarat and Malwa in which Husang Shah was defeated and he succeeded in capturing Dhar.
