Shamsuddin Muzaffar Shah

He was a sultan of the Bengal Sultanate.

He was killed by the rebels led by his wazir Sayyid Husain in 1494.

Nasiruddin Nasrat Shah

He was the son of Sultan Alauddin Hussain Shah.

He is also known as "Sultan of Bengal".

He was associated with the administration and was allowed by his father the prerogative of issuing coins in his own name.
