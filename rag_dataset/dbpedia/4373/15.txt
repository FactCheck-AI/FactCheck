More about Woodburn:

Woodburn, Oregon is a small city located in the US state of Oregon. It is situated in the northwestern Willamette Valley region of the state, approximately 30 miles south of Portland. The city has a population of around 24,000 people and is known for its many local wineries. The climate in Woodburn is temperate, with mild temperatures year-round. Average temperatures in the summer months are in the mid-70s, while winter temperatures usually stay around the mid-40s. Precipitation is fairly consistent throughout the year, with average annual rainfall of around 37 inches. There are occasional snowfalls in the winter months, with average snowfall of around 6 inches per year.
