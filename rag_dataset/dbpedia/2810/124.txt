Frank D. Miller was an American artist who was born in 1939. Frank D. Miller's work has been offered at auction multiple times, with realized prices ranging from 45 USD to 1,100 USD, depending on the size and medium of the artwork. Since 2015 the record price for this artist at auction is 1,100 USD for Battle for Supremacy, sold at Simpson Galleries in 2022. The artist died in 2000.
