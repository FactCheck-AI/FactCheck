Early life[]

Born on June 24, 1987, in Rosario, Santa Fe,[1] Messi is the third of four children of Jorge Messi, a steel factory manager, and Celia Cuccittini, a magnet manufacturing workshop worker. His father is of Italian and Spanish descent, while his mother is primarily Italian.

Trivia[]

He is widely regarded as one of the greatest players of all time.

He has won a record eight Ballon d'Or awards, a record six European Golden Shoes, and was named the world's best player for a record eight times by FIFA.[2]

He has scored over 800 senior career goals for club and country, and the most goals for a single club (672).
