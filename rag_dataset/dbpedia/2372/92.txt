Surrounded by Carillon Historical Park’s 65–acre campus, with its commanding view of the Great Miami River, Deeds Carillon (Ohio’s largest carillon) provides an iconic setting for a wedding ceremony. It was built in 1942 by Reinhard & Hofmeister of New York, the architectural firm responsible for Rockefeller Center.
