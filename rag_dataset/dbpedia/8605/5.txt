Marisa de Azevedo Monte (July 1, 1967) is a Brazilian singer, composer, instrumentalist and producer of MPB (Música Popular Brasileira; or Brazilian Popular Music, translating to English) and since the 1980's is one of most successful artists. She is also a member of the group Tribalistas formed by Carlinhos Brown and former Titãs member Arnaldo Antunes.
