Heber Springs, AR, USA

Latitude and longitude coordinates are: 35.491467, -92.031258.

Heber Springs is a small city in the state of Arkansas, USA. It was founded by Max Frauenthal and is the seat of Cleburne County. Located on the shores of Greers Ferry Lake in the central part of the county, the city has many attractions, including Dam Site Marina, Bridal Veil Falls, Sandy Beach, Jeff Anderson Trail, Ruland Junction Train Museum, Heber Springs Sports Complex, Spring Park, and Dam Site Recreation Area, as well as a few campgrounds. Heber Springs Municipal Airport and Arkansas Highway 5 serve the area.
