Sebastian Junger is an American author, journalist and documentarian, most famous for the best-selling book "The Perfect Storm: A True Story of Men Against the Sea" (1997), his award-winning chronicle of the war in Afghanistan in the documentary film Restrepo (2010), and his book "War" (2010).
