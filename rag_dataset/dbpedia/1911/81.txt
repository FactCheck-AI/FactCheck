Philip Urso is cofounder of Rhode Island Power Yoga, a yoga studio in North Kingstown, Rhode Island, and Live Love Teach, a national yoga teacher training school.

Philip studied for ten years with Baron Baptiste, where he rose to cofacilitator. He has taught more than 50 teacher trainings since 2005.

He is known around the world for his two 5-star podcasts, A Crash Course in Miracles and Yoga Classes Live Love Teach.
