Electronic artist / producer Joel Zimmerman, better known by his stage name DeadMau5 has opened up a brand new, top-quality recording studio in his Campbelville, Ontario home.

Appropriately named the “Mau5trap”, the artist repurposed the master bedroom wing of his new palace, turning two rooms into a studio fit for sound producing, editing and mastering.
