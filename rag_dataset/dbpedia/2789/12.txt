Second release in Finders Keeprs triptych of soundtracks from the films of Alejandro Jodorowsky. If you have not seen it El Topo is a highly surreal western film and this delicately presented edition of the soundtrack is a tab of unforgettable acid-drenched folk, come join the quest for enlightenment.
