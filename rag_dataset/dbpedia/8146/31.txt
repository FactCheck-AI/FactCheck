Actress Mishti Mukherjee, who worked in films and music videos, died of kidney failure on Friday night (October 2). The actress was admitted to a hospital in Bengaluru, where she breathed her last. The actress was in a lot of pain before her death. Mishti made her debut in Bollywood with the film Life Ki Toh lag Gayi in 2012.

While Mishti Mukherjee appeared in a few films and music videos, she was mostly in news for controversies. In 2014, she was arrested along with her father and brother for the possession of pornographic content and alleged prostitution racket. The actress, however, claimed that she and her family were framed in false cases.
