The Music Box Theatre is a historic movie theater located in Chicago, Illinois. Built in 1929, it has been operating continuously as an art-house and repertory cinema since the early 1980s. The Chicago International Film Festival is proud to present our Opening Night screenings at the Music Box Theatre as well as co-presenting several screenings as part of the Music Box of Horrors series.

Public Transit

The Southport Brown Line station is within 0.5 miles of the Music Box Theatre and is a physically accessible station with an elevator. The #152 bus stop is 0.2 miles from the theater.

Accessibility

Restrooms with accessible stalls can be located outside Theater 1, with additional accessible, single-use restrooms located in the Music Box Lounge.
