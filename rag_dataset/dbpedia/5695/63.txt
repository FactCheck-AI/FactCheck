WaterTower Music has released the first soundtrack album for the Netflix original series Manifest. The album features selections of the show’s original music composed by Danny Lux (Grey’s Anatomy, Ally McBeal, Halloween: Resurrection, Boston Legal, Station 19). The soundtrack is now available to stream/download on all major digital music services, including Amazon. Manifest is created by Jeff Rake and stars Melissa Roxburgh, Josh Dallas, J.R. Ramirez, Luna Blaise, Athena Karkanis, Jack Messina, Matt Long, Parveen Kaur, Holly Taylor, Daryl Edwards and Ty Doran. The final episodes of the mystery drama’s fourth and final season are premiering today exclusively on Netflix.
