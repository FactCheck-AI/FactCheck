Sowing Seeds/Going to the Grocery Store/A Face Without Eyebrows (たねまきをするゾ/八百屋さんへおつかいだゾ/マユゲなしの顔だゾ) is the 45th episode of the Crayon Shin-chan anime television series. It aired in Japan on April 12, 1993.
