Director Selvaraghavan, who got engaged to 23-year-old Geethanjali recently, is all set to tie the knot on July 3, 2011 in Chennai. Geethanjali is currently assisting Selva in Irandam Ulagam with Dhanush and Richa in the lead roles. Selva had married actress Sonia Agarwal at a high profile ceremony on 15 December, 2006. After two years, they separated and later got divorced. Interestingly, Karthi will also get married to Erode based Renjani on the same day in Coimbatore.
