Summit Design and Engineering has expanded into the newly renovated 100 E at Anderson Plaza!

Summit Design and Engineering has taken advantage of the renovations we've made at 100 E at Anderson Plaza and has expanded and moved its office space from Six Forks Place III to 100 E at Anderson Plaza. Summit Design and Engineering provides its customers with many different services from surveying and land development, to architecture and much more. Just recently they were acknowledged by the Triangle Business Journal for being the 37th fastest growing private company of the more than 100,000 such companies in the Triangle area. If you would like to learn more about Summit Design and Engineering or the services that they provide, take a peek at their website.
