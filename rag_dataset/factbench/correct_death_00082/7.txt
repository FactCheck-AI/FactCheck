When St. Louis IX roi de France was born on 25 April 1214, in Poissy, Yvelines, France, his father, Louis VIII 'le Lion' roi de France, was 26 and his mother, Blanca de Castilla reina de Francia, was 26. He married Marguerite de Provence reine de France on 27 May 1234. They were the parents of at least 6 sons and 5 daughters. He registered for military service in 1258. He died on 25 August 1270, in Tunis, Tunisia, at the age of 56, and was buried in Saint-Denis, Seine-Saint-Denis, France.
