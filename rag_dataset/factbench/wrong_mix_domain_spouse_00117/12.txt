The Canadian singer, who is engaged to Nickelback frontman Chad Kroeger, has recorded the band's 2001 hit How You Remind Me, reports Anime News Network.

The Sk8r Boi star's rendition of the song will feature on the soundtrack for an upcoming manga film called One Piece Film Z.

She will also contribute another song to be used on the soundtrack.

Avril, 28, got engaged to 37-year-old Chad this summer after dating him for several months. Her marriage to first husband Sum 41 frontman Deryck Whibley ended in 2009 after three years.
