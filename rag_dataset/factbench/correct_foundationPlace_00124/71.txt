Valve Corporation (formerly Valve Software, commonly referred to as Valve) is a successful and influential games development company based in Bellevue, Washington. It was founded by former Microsoft employees Gabe Newell and Mike Harrington in 1996. Their first project was the highly acclaimed Half-Life, based on a Quake engine source code license they bought.
