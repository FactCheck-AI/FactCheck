Limited-edition of Russell Hoban’s post-apocalyptic ‘autobiography’ of Riddley Walker. Includes full-colour illustrations by Quentin Blake, together with three ‘writings’ on Riddley Walker:

- by Russell Hoban: Acknowledgements, Afterword, Notes, Glossary

- by Quentin Blake: ‘Draw is an Intersting Word’

- by Rowan Williams: ‘Myth and Knowledging in Riddley Walker’
