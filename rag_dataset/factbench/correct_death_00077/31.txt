Here are some interesting facts about Kishore Kumar:

Kishore Kumar's real name was Abhas Kumar Ganguly and he was the younger brother of Ashok Kumar.

When Abhas Kumar got a break he changed his name to Kishore. Initially he sang as a chorus singer in Bombay Talkies.

Inspired by KL Saigal, Kishore Kumar considered him as his Guru.

Kishore Kumar made his debut as an actor in 'Shikari' in 1946.

Notorious in nature, Kishore Kumar married four times.

His popular song 'Roop Tera Mastana' got him 'Filmfare Award' in 1969. He won 8 such awards for Best Playback Singer.

The Madhya Pradesh government has awarded Kishore Kumar with the 'Lata Mangeshkar Award' and also initiated 'Kishore Kumar Award' to honour the singer.
