Bohr's Atomic Model

Bohr modified Rutherford's hypotheses of the atomic theory and provided a better model which he published on The Philosophical Magazine. You can find the magazine here https://www.tandfonline.com/doi/abs/10.1080/14786441308634955

Bohr's Nobel Prize

The Nobel Prize in Physics 1922 was awarded to Niels Henrik David Bohr "for his services in the investigation of the structure of atoms and of the radiation emanating from them.

Philosophy of Science!

1927 debate Bohr vs Einstein a great debate for Quantum Theory! They not only represented one of the highest points of scientific research in the first half of the twentieth century but also brought to focus quantum non-locality, which is absolutely essential in our modern understanding of the physical world.
