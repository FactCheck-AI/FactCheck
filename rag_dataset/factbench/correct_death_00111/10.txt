When Prime Minister Lester Bowles Pearson PC, OM, CC, OBE was born on 23 April 1897, in Newton Brook, Toronto, Ontario, Canada, his father, Edwin Arthur Pearson, was 28 and his mother, Annie Sarah Bowles, was 30. He married Maryon Elspeth Moody on 22 August 1925, in Winnipeg, Manitoba, Canada. They were the parents of at least 1 son and 1 daughter. He immigrated to Vermont, United States in 1924 and lived in Hamilton, Wentworth, Ontario, Canada in 1911 and Canada in 1972. In 1963, his occupation is listed as 14th prime minister of canada in Canada. He died on 27 December 1972, in Ottawa, Ontario, Canada, at the age of 75, and was buried in Wakefield, Gatineau, Quebec, Canada.
