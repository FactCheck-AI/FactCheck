In their 64th episode, DCOMmentaries' Al & Val are joined by special guest, Phylicia McLeod, to enjoy summer vacation in Disney Channel movie,High School Musical 2 (2007). Starring Zach Efron, Vanessa Hudgens, Ashley Tisdale, Corbin Bleu, Lucas Grabeel, and Monique Coleman, HSM2 returns to Albuquerque just in time to see the crew trying to make some cash at an exciting summer job.
