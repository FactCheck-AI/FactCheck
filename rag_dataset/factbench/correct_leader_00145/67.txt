Prime Minister of Belarus presents Mogilev oblast with State Honorary Banner

On January 12, Prime Minister of Belarus Sergei Sidorsky presented the Mogilev oblast authorities with the State Honorary Banner of the Republic of Belarus in recognition of great economic achievements and in view of the 70th jubilee of the oblast. Sergei Sidorsky read out the message of the President of Belarus and congratulated the participants of the gathering on the occasion. The Prime Minister also presented the best workers of the oblast with Honorary Diplomas of the Council of Ministers.
