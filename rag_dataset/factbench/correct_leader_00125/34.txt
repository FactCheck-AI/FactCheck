Universiti Tun Hussein Onn Malaysia (UTHM), is one of the public universities in Malaysia primarily focusing on Engineering and Technology. UTHM is strategically located in green sub-urban environment in Batu Pahat, Johor which is about 3 hours drive to Kuala Lumpur and 1.5 hours to Singapore via North-South Expressway (NSE).

Currently, the university has more than 18,000 students including international students from 22 countries all over the world. The university has been awarded overall 5-star by QS Stars University Ratings with 5-Star in Teaching, Employability, Innovation, Online Learning, Engineering Civil Structural and Inclusiveness.

This page is designed to provide the information on how to apply for an Undergraduate and Postgraduate programmes at UTHM.
