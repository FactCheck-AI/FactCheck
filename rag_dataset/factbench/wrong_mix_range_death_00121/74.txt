What is Conway Twitty's Net Worth?

Conway Twitty was an American country music singer who had a net worth of $14 million at the time of his death in 1993. Conway Twitty was born in Friars Point, Mississippi in September 1933 and passed away in June 1993. He also played guitar and his styles included country, rock and roll, and rockabilly. Twitty released 57 studio albums with his debut Conway Twitty Sings being released in 1959.
