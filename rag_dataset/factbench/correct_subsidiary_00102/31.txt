Pelyco Partners with Hyperion Solutions

webPUBLISH™ and SmartSpreadSheet™ Complement Hyperion Product Offering with Supply Chain Solutions

Silicon Valley, CA, February 16, 2007 - Pelyco Systems, a global provider of web-based supply chain visibility solutions, is an Affiliate Partner of Hyperion Solutions; the worldwide leader in Business Performance Management software. This affiliation recognizes the skill and expertise of our staff to implement business solutions using the Hyperion Solutions software suite.

Joint Value Proposition

Pelyco Systems' supply chain and logistics visibility solution is designed to enhanced operational business performance and is an ideal complement to the Hyperion user community. Combined with Hyperion, Pelyco's webPUBLISH provides a powerful total supply chain visibility and performance management solution that leverages the experience and expertise developed with other leading Hyperion customers such as IBM. webPUBLISH coupled with the power and agility of Hyperion offers the opportunity to improve the entire supply chain and enables collaboration, information sharing, business performance improvement and shared decision making.

"Our partnership with Hyperion demonstrates that we share the vision of bringing world-class solutions to the 'last mile' of supply chain visibility," said Colin Finn, Vice President of Pelyco. "We're pleased to join with Hyperion to provide unified solutions that help companies better manage their logistics and supply chain processes.â

Hyperion and Pelyco's partnership is an innovative and powerful union of technologies and vision delivering significant value for a range of customers including Caterpillar and IBM.

About Hyperion

Hyperion Solutions Corporation, a wholly owned subsidiary of Oracle Corporation, is the global leader in Business Performance Management software. More than 10,000 customers in 90 countries rely on Hyperion software to provide visibility into how their businesses are performing and to help them plan and model to improve that performance. Using Hyperion software, customers collect data, organize and analyze it, then communicate it across the enterprise. Hyperion offers the industry's only Business Performance Management solution that integrates financial management applications with a business intelligence platform into a single system. Named one of the FORTUNE 100 Best Companies to Work For (2004), Hyperion serves global customers in 45 countries. A network of more than 600 partners provides the company's innovative and specialized solutions and services. For more information, please visit www.hyperion.com.

About Pelyco Systems

Pelyco Systems delivers supply chain visibility solutions. Established in 1993, Pelyco focuses on exceeding all customer expectations when delivering advanced supply chain visibility products and services. Pelyco develops and supports leading edge, web-based business solutions for manufacturers and their supply chains. The Pelyco Systems solutions and services transform the supply chain to provide companies with significant business value and competitive advantage. Pelyco is a privately held company headquartered in Foster City, California. For more information on the company and its products and services, visit Pelyco on the web at http://www.pelyco.com.
