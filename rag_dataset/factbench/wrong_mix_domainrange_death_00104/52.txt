When William Jennings Bryan was born on 17 February 1840, in Alabama, United States, his father, Richard J Bryan, was 28 and his mother, Sarah Ann Oliver, was 21. He married Elizabeth Anne Smith on 29 June 1858, in Greene, Georgia, United States. They were the parents of at least 5 sons and 3 daughters. He lived in District 162, Greene, Georgia, United States for about 10 years and District 163, Greene, Georgia, United States in 1920. He died on 7 September 1927, in Greene, Georgia, United States, at the age of 87, and was buried in Veazey, Greene, Georgia, United States.
