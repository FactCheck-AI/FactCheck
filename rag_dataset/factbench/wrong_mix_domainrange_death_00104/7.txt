When William Jennings Bryan Wilkinson was born on 19 February 1897, in Clinton, Missouri, United States, his father, Francis Jefferson Wilkinson, was 25 and his mother, Marian Eva Ettie Cheesman, was 18. He married Gladys Margaret Morgan on 4 May 1914, in Los Angeles, Los Angeles, California, United States. They were the parents of at least 2 sons. He lived in Lynwood, Los Angeles, California, United States in 1930 and Compton Judicial Township, Los Angeles, California, United States in 1940. He registered for military service in 1918. He died on 29 December 1966, in Los Angeles, California, United States, at the age of 69, and was buried in Compton, Los Angeles, California, United States.
