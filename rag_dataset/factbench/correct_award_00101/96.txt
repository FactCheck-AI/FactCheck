Profession: Poet

Biography: Seamus Heaney won the 1995 Nobel Prize in Literature - "for works of lyrical beauty and ethical depth, which exalt everyday miracles and the living past".

Some of his best regarded collections of poetry include his first "Death of a Naturalist" (1966) and "The Spirit Level" (1996), which won the Whitbread Book of the Year Award.

Born: April 13, 1939

Birthplace: Castledawson, County Londonderry, Northern Ireland

Generation: Silent Generation

Chinese Zodiac: Rabbit

Star Sign: Aries
