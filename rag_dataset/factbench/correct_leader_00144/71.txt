Algiers / Information Office / July 19

HE Prime Minister of the People's Democratic Republic of Algeria Ahmed Ouyahia met with HE Ambassador of the State of Qatar to Algeria, Ibrahim bin Abdulaziz Al Sahlawi, on the occasion of ending his tenure.

During the meeting they discussed the bilateral relations and ways to boost and develop them. They also discussed topics of common interest.

HE the prime minister expressed his wishes of success to HE the ambassador in his future diplomatic posts.

In turn, Qatar's ambassador thanked Algeria's president, government and people for what they offered to facilitate the performance of his duties.
