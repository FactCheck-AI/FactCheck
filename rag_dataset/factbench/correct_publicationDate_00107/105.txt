A very rare and highly collectable piece of space memorabilia.

Encounter with Tiber book signed by the Author and second man on the moon Buzz Aldrin.

Brand new and unread hard back book - only opened to be signed by Buzz at a private signing in 2004. The signing was witnessed by Tim Miller CEO of Flatsigned books and Lisa Cannon who is Buzz's attorney.

Limited edition number 673 of 1500 copies

Very Fine Condition with a Certificate of Authenticity from the Flatsigned Press.

22 Carat gold embossed leather binding with raised hubs on the spine, moire fabric end panels, and a satin ribbon page marker. The gold gilded page ends are in Very Fine Condition.

Please note that the pages are NOT yellow with age, they have a gold gilded edge which makes them look like this on the photo.
