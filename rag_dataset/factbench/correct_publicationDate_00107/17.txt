This incredible Encounter with Tiber book was written and is hand signed by astronaut Buzz Aldrin. The concept of the book is based on long-extinct beings that once visited Earth, the Tiberians. Records of the Tiberians exist on the Moon and Mars, and in a dire twist of fate, long-time astronaut Chris Terence dies trying to recover this treasure. Despite this, his family are determined to continue his mission, which leads them on an amazing journey to Mars and beyond!
