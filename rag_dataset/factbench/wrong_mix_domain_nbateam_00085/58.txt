About

The leadership team of the Minnesota Timberwolves has experience in finance, accounting, and entertainment. They have held positions at Midwest Entertainment Group, Clear Channel Entertainment, Jack Link's Protein Snacks, and Nuvera. They also have experience as a shareholder at Winthrop & Weinstine. P.A. The team is educated at the University of Minnesota-Duluth, University of St. Thomas, and College of Saint Benedict.
