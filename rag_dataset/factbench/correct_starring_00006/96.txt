This is an actual church that the wedding massacre took place for the 2003 movie "Kill Bill: Vol. 1" as well as the 2004 "Kill Bill: Vol. 2" starring Uma Thurman, Lucy Liu, Vivica A. Fox, Daryl Hannah, David Carradine, and Michael Madsen. This very active and alive church is in a remote desert location about 20 miles outside of Lancaster, CA.
