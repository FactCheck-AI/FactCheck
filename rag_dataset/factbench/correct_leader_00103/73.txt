Doha / Information Office / February 25

HE the Deputy Prime Minister and Foreign Minister Sheikh Mohammed bin Abdulrahman Al-Thani received a written message from HE Iraqi Foreign Minister Ibrahim Al Jaafari, related to supporting and developing bilateral relations between the two countries.

The message was delivered by HE charge d'affaires at the Embassy of Iraq in Qatar Abdulsattar Hadi al Janabi when HE the Deputy Prime Minister and Foreign Minister met him today.
