Online Meeting with

Mr. George A. Papandreou,

Former Prime Minister and Minister of Foreign Affairs of Greece

May 15, 2020

On May 15, 2020, Mr. George A. Papandreou, Former Prime Minister and Minister of Foreign Affairs of Greece, addressed GRF and GRF Community members at an online meetinng. In his speech, Mr. Papandreou shared with the participants his impressions of Greece’s relations with the EU and Turkey post-global pandemic. Despite a number of recent issues between Turkey and Greece, Mr. Papandreou stated that Greece has been an important partner in Turkey’s EU accession process and stressed that this partnership has to continue after the global pandemic has abated. Following his speech, Mr. Papandreou answered the participants’ questions.
