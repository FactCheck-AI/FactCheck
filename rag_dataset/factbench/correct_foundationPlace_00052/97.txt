Amina Helmi is full professor at the Kapteyn Astronomical Institute of the University of Groningen, the Netherlands. She obtained her PhD from the University of Leiden, after which she held postdoctoral fellowships in Germany and the Netherlands. She has received numerous prizes, including the 2004 Christiaan Huygens and the 2010 Pastoor Schmeits prizes for astronomy.
