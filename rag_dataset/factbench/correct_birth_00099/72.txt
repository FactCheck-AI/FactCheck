19th Century Metal Relief Portrait Plaque of William Gladstone, British Prime Minister.

William Ewart Gladstone was born in Liverpool in 1809. He first entered Parliament in 1833 as the Member for Newark and in 1847 as the Member for the University of Oxford. He later became a successful Chancellor of the Exchequer. He was Prime Minister of Great Britain from 1868 to 1874, 1880 to 1885, 1886, and again from 1892 to March 1894. He died in 1898.

In excellent condition with a fine patina.

Approximate diameter is 14 cm.
