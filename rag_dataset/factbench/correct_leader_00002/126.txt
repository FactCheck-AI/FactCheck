The Institute for Government was delighted to welcome former Prime Minister, the Rt Hon Gordon Brown, to present a keynote speech on Brexit and the future of the UK.

He was in conversation with Bronwen Maddox, Director of the Institute for Government.

Gordon Brown served as Prime Minister of the United Kingdom from 2007–10. He previously served as Chancellor of the Exchequer from 1997–2007, making him the longest-serving Chancellor in modern history.
