Charlie Walton

Charlie Walton is a college student majoring in sports journalism who covers the Minnesota Timberwolves for Canis Hoopus. Charlie debuted at Canis in 2023, but found a love for creating content and covering the NBA in 2020 when he started up a Timberwolves fan page, and since then, the rest is history. Charlie aims to provide the best, most reliable content and coverage of his favorite team, the Timberwolves, hoping to do it full-time someday! You can follow Charlie on Twitter and Instagram @CharlieWaltonMN!
