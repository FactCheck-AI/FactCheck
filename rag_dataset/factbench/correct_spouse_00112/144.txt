After months of rumors, Ryan Phillippe and girlfriend Abbie Cornish hve finally split up. Cornish was seen moving her things out of the L.A. home she shared with Phillippe, according to a report in People magazine.

Cornish and Phillippe met on the set of the movie Stop-Loss, striking up a romance a year later following his divorce from Legally Blonde star Reese Witherspoon. Phillippe and Witherspoon were married for seven years before divorcing in 2006. Cornish’s possible involvement in the Phillippe-Witherspoon split brought her unwelcome under media scrutiny. –KADEEN GRIFFITHS
