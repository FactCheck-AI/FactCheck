On Tuesday, November 4, 2008, Czech Premier Mirek Topolánek will receive the visiting Finnish Prime Minister Matti Vanhanen.

Their bilateral negotiations will start after a welcoming ceremony to be held in the Garden of the Office of the Government of the Czech Republic. Under discussion will be primarily the priorities of the Czech EU presidency and further promotion of mutual trade and economic ties. Both countries also share concern for the safe use and operation of nuclear power stations.
