When Frederick (Fred) William Drees was born on 22 March 1895, in Alton, Sioux, Iowa, United States, his father, Frederick Drees, was 29 and his mother, Elizabeth Arens, was 28. He had at least 1 son with Martha Petronella (Nellie) Krapfl. He lived in North Fork Township, Delaware, Iowa, United States in 1930 and Bremen Township, Delaware, Iowa, United States in 1940. He registered for military service in 1919. He died on 7 January 1964, at the age of 68, and was buried in Dyersville, Dubuque, Iowa, United States.
