KATHMANDU,

The film ‘Shehzada’ directed by Rohit Dhawan starring Kartik Aryan, Kriti Sanon in the lead role is set to release on February 10, 2023. In the action drama, actor Manisha Koirala will also be seen in a lead role.

The film is the Hindi remake of the 2020 Telegu film ‘Ala Vaikunthapurramuloo’ starring Allu Arjun and Pooja Hegde. The further members playing lead role in the Hindi remake include Paresh Rawal, Ronit Roy and Sachin Khedekar, while other characters will be enacted by Kushal Awatarsing, Sunny Hinduja, Ankur Rathee, Ali asgar, Shalini Kapoor and Ashwin Mushran.

The film is produced by Bhushan Kumar, Aman Gill, Allu Aravind and Krishan Kumar. Previously, Koirala was seen in Rajkumar Hirani’s film ‘Sanju’ as Nargis Dutt and in the earlier week, Koirala had joined Sanjay Leela Bhansali’s Netflix series ‘Heeramandi’.
