SEVILLE (FIBA Europe Championship) - Andris Biedrins enters the 2007 FIBA Europe Championship as one of the true emerging stars in the NBA after his incredible breakout season with the Golden State Warriors. The 2.08m center now with the Latvian national team in Seville, where they will take on Croatia, world champions Spain and Portugal in the toughest tournament of the summer. Twenty-four hours before Latvia’s opener against Croatia, he spoke briefly to FIBA’s Jeff Taylor.
