When Harold John Gluyas was born on 15 April 1922, in Telowie, South Australia, Australia, his father, Ralph Miller Gluyas, was 29 and his mother, Louise Maud Beyer, was 32. He married Sybil Priscilla Luetchford on 7 December 1955, in Colombo, Western, Sri Lanka. He immigrated to New York City, New York, United States in 1950. He died on 18 March 2012, in Smithfield, South Australia, Australia, at the age of 89, and was buried in Willaston, South Australia, Australia.
