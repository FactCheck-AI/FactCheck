Alcatel Lucent Enterprise SA (ALE International) is a provider of communications infrastructure solutions and services. The company offers products such as communication systems, switches, contact centers and attendants, ecosystems integration, wireless local area network (LAN), phones, softphones and devices, communications management and security, network management and security. It also provides digital-age networking, business continuity and digital-age communications solutions. ALE International services include professional, support, training, customization, customer and location services. The company serves healthcare, hospitality, education, energy and utilities, digital government, transportation and small and medium business sectors. It operates in Europe, the Middle East, Africa, Asia Pacific and the Americas. ALE International is headquartered in Paris, Ile-de-France, France.
