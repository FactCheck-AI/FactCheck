This home ‘Icon Edition’ jersey was worn by Jabari Smith Jr. on November 14, 2022, when the Houston Rockets played the Los Angeles Clippers. Smith Jr. finished with 7 points, 6 rebounds, 1 assist, and 3 steals. The Los Angeles Clippers beat the Houston Rockets, 122–106. This was Jabari Smith Jr.'s rookie season.

Jabari Smith Jr. was drafted with the 3rd overall pick in the 1st round of the 2022 NBA Draft. Smith Jr. was a 2023 All-Rookie Second Team selection after his first season.

At the time of cataloging, he has career averages of 12.8 points per game, 7.2 assists per game, and 1.3 rebounds per game.
