Feast your eyes on Om Shanti Om

Model Yuvika Chaudhary has a cameo in the movie (she plays an actress). Yuvika has done many commercials like Ponds, Rupa with Sanjay Dutt, Innova with Aamir Khan, Kotex and many more. But it was her Thumbs Ups ad with Kunal Kapoor that got Farah's eye. She was also a contestant in Zee Cinestar Ki Khoj and has starred in the serial Astitva Ek Prem Kahani.
