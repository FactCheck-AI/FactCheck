Gary Cooper of Flora Vista, NM passed away in Farmington, NM at the age of 38 on March 18, 2023. He was born March 26, 1984 in Los Alamitos, CA to Melencia Lockwood.

Gary was friendly, loving, amazing husband and daddy. He always had a smile on his face. He loved to spend time with family and play video games. Gary loved telling jokes.

He is survived by his wife Tammy Cooper, two beautiful daughters Brooklynn Cooper age 3 and Maya Cooper age 9. Gary will be missed dearly by all who knew and loved him.
