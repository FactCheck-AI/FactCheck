STUDIO CITY (CBSLA.com) — The widow of Legendary Gene Kelly, Patricia Ward Kelly, visited the KCAL9 studios Friday to talk about the intimate performance she will be presenting this weekend in honor of her husband.

During a unique evening, Patricia Ward Kelly—his widow, biographer and the person who knew him best—presents an intimate portrait of this dynamic and innovative artist who created some of the most memorable and iconic scenes in film history.

This special live program makes for a remarkable experience praised as "a real treat" by Variety and hailed as "deeply moving" and "mesmerizing."

It is being held at the Bram Goldsmith Theater, Wallis Annenberg Center for the Performing Arts located at 9390 N. Santa Monica Blvd in Beverly Hills.
