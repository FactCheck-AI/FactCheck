Sol y Sombra, Sligo Spanish Society, with the kind assistance of Instituto Cervantes in Dublin, will host a lecture by Belgian Professor Jacques de Bruyne on Literature Nobel Prize winner Camilo Jose Cela on Thursday, November 14th, at the Yeats Memorial Building at 8p.m.

Jacques de Bruyne is a Professor of the University of Antwerp, also a corresponding member of the Real Academia Espanola and the North American Academy of Spanish language. He is a specialist in linguistics and literature and did his doctoral thesis on Camilo Jose Cela.
