Jun 27, 2001 - The Atlanta Hawks selected Pau Gasol in Round 1 with Pick 3 in the 2001 NBA Draft. Jul 19, 2001 - The draft rights to Pau Gasol was acquired by the Memphis Grizzlies, along with Brevin Knight, Lorenzen Wright and a TPE, from the Atlanta Hawks in exchange for Shareef Abdur-Rahim and the draft rights to Jamaal Tinsley. Sep 6, 2001 - Pau Gasol signed a multi-year contract with the Memphis Grizzlies. Oct 5, 2003 - The Memphis Grizzlies exercised their Team Option to extend the contract of Pau Gasol. Sep 30, 2004 - Pau Gasol signed a rookie scale extension with the Memphis Grizzlies. Feb 1, 2008 - Pau Gasol was acquired by the Los Angeles Lakers, along with a 2010 2nd round pick (MEM own), from the Memphis Grizzlies in exchange for Kwame Brown, Javaris Crittenton, Aaron McKie, the draft rights to Marc Gasol, a conditional 2008 1st round pick (LAL own), a conditional 2010 1st round pick (LAL own) and cash. Dec 23, 2009 - Pau Gasol signed a veteran extension with the Los Angeles Lakers. Jul 1, 2014 - Pau Gasol, previously with the Los Angeles Lakers, became a free agent. Jul 13, 2014 - The Los Angeles Lakers renounced their free-agent exception rights to Pau Gasol. Jul 18, 2014 - Pau Gasol signed a multi-year contract with the Chicago Bulls. Jul 1, 2016 - Pau Gasol, previously with the Chicago Bulls, became a free agent. Jul 7, 2016 - The Chicago Bulls renounced their free-agent exception rights to Pau Gasol. Jul 14, 2016 - Pau Gasol signed a multi-year contract with the San Antonio Spurs. Jul 1, 2017 - Pau Gasol, previously with the San Antonio Spurs, became a free agent. Jul 24, 2017 - Pau Gasol signed a multi-year contract with the San Antonio Spurs. Mar 1, 2019 - The San Antonio Spurs placed the contract of Pau Gasol on waivers. Mar 3, 2019 - Pau Gasol, previously with the San Antonio Spurs, became a free agent. Mar 3, 2019 - Pau Gasol signed a contract with the Milwaukee Bucks. Jul 1, 2019 - Pau Gasol, previously with the Milwaukee Bucks, became a free agent. Jul 6, 2019 - The Milwaukee Bucks renounced their free-agent exception rights to Pau Gasol. Jul 25, 2019 - Pau Gasol signed a contract with the Portland Trail Blazers. Nov 20, 2019 - The Portland Trail Blazers placed the contract of Pau Gasol on waivers. Nov 22, 2019 - Pau Gasol, previously with the Portland Trail Blazers, became a free agent.
