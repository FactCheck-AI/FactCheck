BIOGRAPHY

Katy Perry began singing in church during her childhood before signing with Red Hill Records and releasing her debut gospel studio album Katy Hudson (2001) under her birth name. After Red Hill went bankrupt, she moved to Los Angeles at age 17, adopted the stage name “Katy Perry” and began working on an album titled Fingerprints with various producers, but she left this project. He then signed a recording contract with Capitol Records in April 2007 and rose to fame with his second studio album and major label debut, One of the Boys (2008), a pop rock record containing his debut single ” I Kissed a Girl” and follow-up single “Hot n Cold”, which respectively reached number one and three on the US Billboard Hot 100. Perry has since become one of the world’s best-selling music artists, has nine number one singles on the Billboard Hot 100, three number one albums on the Billboard 200.
