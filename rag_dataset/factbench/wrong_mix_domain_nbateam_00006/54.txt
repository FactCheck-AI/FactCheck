NBA Draft

on june 27th 2001, Pau gasol was drafted third overall pick by the atlanta hawks but was traded to the memphis grizzlies. in his rookie year, he averaged 17.6 points and 8.9 rebounds per game. he became rookie of the year.

Traded to the Lakers

on february 1st, 2001 Gasol was traded to the Los angeles Lakers along with a 2010 second draft pick for Kwame Brown, Javaris Crittenton, Aaron Mckie, the rights of Marc Gasol Pau's younger brother, and 2008, and 2010 first round draft picks. In the playoffs gasol was going crazy. he scored 36 points, 16 rebounds, 8 assists and 3 blocks.

loses the first finals and win the next the one

on june 17th 2008, Gasol and the lakers lost to the boston celtics by 50 points in game 5. it was a disappointing loss. in 2009 nba finals, the gasol and the lakers beat the orlando magic 4-1 series, and gasol had a game of his life.
