Terje Strand has a strong background in software development and communication services. Terje co-founded Expeto in 2015 and served as the CTO, where they developed a cloud-based LTE core network stack platform. Prior to this, Terje worked at Oracle from 2006 to 2015, holding various roles including Director of Software Development and Software Architect. Terje worked on products such as the Oracle WebRTC Signalling Controller. Terje also served as the CTO at Net4Call from 2002 to 2006 before it was acquired by Oracle, and was a Senior Developer at Net4Call from 2000 to 2001.

Terje Strand attended the University of Bristol from 1995 to 1999, where they obtained their MEng degree in Computer Systems Engineering.
