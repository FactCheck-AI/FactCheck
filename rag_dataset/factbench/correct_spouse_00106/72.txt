After six years of marriage, One Tree Hill's Bethany Joy Galeotti is divorcing her musician husband Michael.

The actress announced the split on her blog, adding that she will revert to her maiden name, Bethany Joy Lenz. "I'm sorry to say that Michael and I have decided to divorce," she wrote. "We remain friendly and dedicated to raising our beautiful girl in love and we appreciate your prayers and support during this difficult time."
