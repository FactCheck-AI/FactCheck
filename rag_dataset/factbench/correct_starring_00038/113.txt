2012 will be released on 4K Ultra HD (with Blu-ray and Digital) on January 19 via Sony Pictures. The 2009 disaster movie is directed by Roland Emmerich (Independence Day, Godzilla).

John Cusack, Chiwetel Ejiofor, Amanda Peet, Oliver Platt, Thandie Newton, Danny Glover, and Woody Harrelson star. Emmerich wrote the script with frequent collaborator Harald Kloser (10,000 BC).
