Jonas Nachmanson is Swedish and was QlikTech’s CTO from 1996–2012. He was also a member of QlikTech’s executive team for more than 15 years, throughout and beyond the IPO in 2010. In his role as head of R&D strategies and implementation, Jonas led the development of the world renowned product QlikView. – an accomplishment he still considers to be one of his greatest achievements. Before QlikTech he worked for Tetra Pak in various roles, both in Sweden and several years in Russia. Jonas holds a Masters Degree from Lund University. In his free time he rides horses, does photography and enjoys cooking and fine wines.
