Model/actress Ali Landry is a mother again after giving birth to a son.

The TV star and her film director husband Alejandro Monteverde welcomed little Marcelo Alejandro in Los Angeles on Saturday (08Oct11).

The couple, which wed in 2006, is already parents to four-year-old Estela.

Former beauty queen Ali Landry was briefly married to Saved By The Bell star Mario Lopez, but the union was annulled two weeks later after the actor admitted cheating on her.
