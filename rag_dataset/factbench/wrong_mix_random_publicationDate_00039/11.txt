Biography

Birth and Parents

John Thurston was born in New York.

John, the ninth of twelve children of Joel Thurston and Miriam Blakeley was born on 22 June 1775 at Easton, New York.

Marriage and Children

John married Amanda Tripp (1812-1909), the daughter of Everett Tripp on 6 January 1800 at Easton, New York.[1] They had eleven children:

Betsy Thurston (b. 1801; d. 1870) m. Luke English (1791-1870)

Milly Thurston (b. 1803; d. ) m.

Arissa Thurston (b. 1805; d. 1901) m. at Barker, NY, Uriah Decker (1801-1879)

David Thurston (b. 1808; d. 1880) m. 5 Jan 1830 at Barker, NY, Catherine Fuller (1812-1893)

Alfred Thurston (b. 1810; d. 1889) m. 1840 at Broome, NY, Olive Freeman (1812-)

Amanda Thurston (b. 1812; d. 1909) m. 27 Sep 1833, Ambrose Southerland Gager (1812-1874)

John Thurston (b. 1814; d. ) m. 29 Jun 1842, Experience Stoddard (1816-)

Abigail Thurston (b. 1816; d. 1850) m. 6 Oct 1833 at Barker, NY, Albert Keeler (1813-1889)

Thomas Thurston (b. 1818; d. 1882) m. 1847 at Broome, NY, Ann Decker (1834-)

Louisa Thurston (b. 1821; d. ) m. James Masten (1819-)

Mahala Thurston (b. 1829; d. 1843)

In 1850, John was 75 years of age and living with his wife Abigail (69 years) at Barker, Broome, New York.[2]

Death

John Thurston died on 10 March 1853 in his 77th year at Barker, New York. [Needs source].
