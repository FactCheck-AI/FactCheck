Mad Men star Jessica Pare is set to become a first-time mom.

The Canadian actress, 33, and her musician beau John Kastner are preparing to welcome their first child, her representative confirms to Us Weekly.

Pare, who plays Jon Hamm's wife on the acclaimed TV drama, was previously married to actor Joseph M. Smith for three years until their split in 2010.
