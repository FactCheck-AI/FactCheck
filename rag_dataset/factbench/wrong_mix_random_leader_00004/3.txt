Bobby Parezi is a recurring character on FOX drama soap Melrose Place. He is portrayed by John Enos III.

Character[]

Bobby is the brother of Jack Parezi, who was Amanda Woodward's first husband. He is the only known member of the crime-ridden Parezi family who strived to make an honest living. Originally in the Navy, he fought in the Gulf War, considered the good son in comparison to Jack.

Following Jack's death during a struggle with Amanda, Bobby was sent by his father to take vengeance. Instead, Bobby developed a brief romantic relationship with Amanda and found himself unable to harm her.
