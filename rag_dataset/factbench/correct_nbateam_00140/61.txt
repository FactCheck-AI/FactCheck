According to Sean Deveney of Sporting News, Gerald Green has agreed to a guaranteed one-year deal with the Boston Celtics.

Green had a tumultuous season last year as he averaged just 8.9 points on a dismal 39.2 percent from the field.

The 6’8″ swingman was a longshot to return after the Miami Heat added both James Johnson and Derrick Williams in free agency.
