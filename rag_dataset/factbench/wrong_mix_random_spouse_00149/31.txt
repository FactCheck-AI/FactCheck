CENTRE ISLAND, N.Y. - Singer-songwriter Billy Joel has married a Miami University graduate.

Joel and 23-year-old Kate Lee exchanged vows Sunday at his Long Island mansion.

Lee, a Huntington, West Virginia, native recently graduated from Miami, which is in Oxford, Ohio. She works as a restaurant correspondent for the PBS show "George Hirsch: Living It Up!"

She and Joel began dating last year.

The 55-year-old Joel has married twice previously, including to supermodel Christie Brinkley.
