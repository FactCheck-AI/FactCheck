Kingston-born superstar Bryan Adams will be coming back home to Kingston at the Leon’s Centre as part of his “So Happy It Hurts” Tour on October 8.

Adams, who was born in Kingston in 1959, has been writing and performing hits since the 80s – including ‘Heaven’, ‘Straight from the Heart”, ‘Summer of 69’, ‘Run to You’, ‘Cuts Like a Knife’, and ‘Everythimng I Do, I Do It For You’- and continues filling concert venues around the world forty years later.

Leon’s Centre ticket pre-sale runs on May 26th (10am-10pm) with public sales launching May 27th at 10am and Box office sales beginning May 31st at 10am.
