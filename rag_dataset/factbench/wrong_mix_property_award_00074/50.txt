Dimensions: 95 x 135 mm. On the reverse side near the top edge a small trace of underpainting, otherwise very good condition. Unmarked by photo establishment.

Wladyslaw Stanislaw Reymont - born 7.5.1867 in Kobiele Wielkie, died 5.12.1925 in Warsaw - Polish writer, novelist and prose writer, one of the main representatives of Young Poland's prose realism. Winner of the Nobel Prize in literature for his four-volume "peasant epic" The Peasants. One of the most outstanding and important writers in the history of Polish literature.
