Cary Grant Carroll, "Jerry" went to heaven, June 9, 2024 after many months of fighting cancer.

He is the son of the late Leo Carroll and Mae Emory Carroll. Cary was also preceded in death by his brother: Charles Carroll.

He is survived by wife Julia Carroll of 61 years & two daughters: Darlene "Dolly" Seeley (husband David) & Wanda Emory. Two grandchildren Kristina Emory & Grant Seeley, two step grandchildren Tiffany Jones & Tyree Seeley, two great grandchildren Trinity Jones & Natan Seeley, two sisters Helen Carroll & Margaret Green & 1 Brother Leon Carroll

He retired from N.C. State University. He was a member of Sherron Acres First Free Will Baptist Church. Cary was a # 1 Duke fan.

Graveside services will be 10:00 am, Friday, June 14, 2024 at Woodlawn Memorial Park in Durham NC.

The family requested no flowers please.
