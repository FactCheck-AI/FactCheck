The Akira Kurosawa Memorial Short Film Competition is a major international short film awards ceremony, run by the Akira Kurosawa Foundation. Since the inaugural 2004-2005 competition, the Grand Prix and numerous other prizes have been awarded on an annual basis.

2006

The Akira Kurosawa Memorial Short Film Competition 2006 Final Round and Award Ceremony was held in Yurakucho, Tokyo on Friday 17th November 2006. [ja icon [http://www.kurosawa-foundation.com/sfc_2007/eng/news/news_02.html SFC2006 : Final Results ] ]

2004-2005

The Akira Kurosawa Memorial Short Film Competition 2004-2005 Final Round and Award Ceremony was held in the Asahi Hall (Yurakucho Marion Build. 12F) on 4th September 2005. [ [http://www.kurosawa-foundation.com/sfc_2007/eng/news/news_01.html SFC 2006 ニュース - 001 ] ]
