Kill Bill: Volume 1 is a 2003 American martial arts film written and directed by Quentin Tarantino. It stars Uma Thurman as the Bride, who swears revenge on a team of assassins and their leader, Bill , after they try to kill her and her unborn child. Her journey takes her to Tokyo, where she battles the yakuza. It features Lucy Liu, David Carradine, Vivica A. Fox, Michael Madsen, Daryl Hannah and Julie Dreyfus in supporting roles.

It was the 211th film to join the Marquee of Fame.
