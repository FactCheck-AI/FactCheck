Synopsys, Inc. (Nasdaq: SNPS) is the Silicon to Software™ partner for innovative companies developing the electronic products and software applications we rely on every day. As an S&P 500 company, Synopsys has a long history of being a global leader in electronic design automation (EDA) and semiconductor IP and offers the industry's broadest portfolio of application security testing tools and services. Saber is the Synopsys platform for modeling and simulating physical systems for applications in analog/power electronics, electric power generation/conversion/distribution and mechatronics. The Synopsys LucidShape® CAA V5 Based product is the industry's only complete workflow solution within the CATIA V5 environment to create and visualize automotive lighting designs.

Synopsys’ LucidShape CAA V5 Based tool offers comprehensive CATIA-based optical design and simulation capabilities and integrates easily into the workflow of anyone using CATIA to model automotive lighting components. Designers can prep illumination optics for manufacture and quickly verify design compliance with performance requirements and industry regulations, which helps save costs and get products to market faster.

Synopsys’ SaberES Designer™ provides an integrated process for electrical system design from concept to manufacturing.
