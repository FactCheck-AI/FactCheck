When Sir Walter Scott was born on 19 April 1620, in Roxburghshire, Scotland, United Kingdom, his father, Sir William Scott, was 29 and his mother, Agnes Murray, was 30. He married Anne Isabel Macdougall in 1650, in Scotland, United Kingdom. They were the parents of at least 5 sons and 2 daughters. He died on 18 July 1688, in Smailholm, Roxburghshire, Scotland, United Kingdom, at the age of 68, and was buried in Kelso, Scottish Borders, Scotland, United Kingdom.
