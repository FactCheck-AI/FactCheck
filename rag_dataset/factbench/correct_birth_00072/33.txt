Cara Black is an American author of mystery novels. Her novels feature Aimee Leduc, a private investigator based in Paris, France. She has won the Anthony Award for Best First Novel and Best Novel. Cara has worked as a preschool teacher and preschool director.

Cara Black became a published author in 1998 with Murder in the Marais. Below is a list of Cara Black’s books in order of when they were originally published (and in chronological order):
