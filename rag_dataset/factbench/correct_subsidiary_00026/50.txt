DICE Los Angeles

Profile

DICE Los Angeles (also known as EA Los Angeles) is a video game developer founded originally in 1995 as DreamWorks Interactive LLC, a subsidiary of DreamWorks SKG.

The company was acquired by Electronic Arts in 2000 from the film studio (and Microsoft, which had part ownership of the company) and in 2003 merged with the Westwood Studios (the original creators of the Command & Conquer Series) and EA Pacific (originally Westwood Pacific) to create the new Electronic Arts Los Angeles development studios.
