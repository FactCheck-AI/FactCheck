About Westwood Studios

Westwood Studios was a computer and video game developer, founded in 1985 as Westwood Associates by Brett Sperry and Louis Castle and based in Las Vegas, Nevada. Westwood is best known for developing game series in the real-time strategy, adventure, and role-playing genres. In 2003, the company was acquired and closed by Electronic Arts, which continues to develop games based on Westwood's Command & Conquer series.
