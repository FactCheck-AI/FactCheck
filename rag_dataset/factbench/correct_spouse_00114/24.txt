Wedding bells for Tori and actor/playwright Charlie.TORI Spelling has landed a husband.

Spelling, best known for her role as virginal Donna Martin on “Beverly Hills, 90210,” will marry actor/playwright Charlie Shanian next year, her publicist said.

Spelling, in fact, is co-starring with Shanian in his play, “Maybe, Baby It’s You” in L.A.

The marriage is expected to happen next summer. Spelling, 30, is slated to star in an upcoming WB sitcom, “The Help.”
