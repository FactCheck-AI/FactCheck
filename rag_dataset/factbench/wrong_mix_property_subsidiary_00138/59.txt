Oracle acquires MetaSolv Software

Oracle, the American business software group, has agreed a $219 million (£117 million) deal to buy MetaSolv Software, the Nasdaq-listed software developer, which creates products for the communications and media industries. The cash deal will see MetaSolv’s employees join Oracle’s own communications unit. The acquisition is the latest in a series of deals by Oracle to boost sales.
