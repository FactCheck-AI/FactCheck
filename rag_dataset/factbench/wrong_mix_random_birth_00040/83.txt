Kelly Brianne Clarkson is an American singer, songwriter, children's book author, and occasional actress, who rose to fame after becoming the winner of the first season of the Fox reality singing competition American Idol in 2002. She also hosted her self titled Kelly Clarkson Show, and voiced Moxy in the 2019 animated film UglyDolls and Delta Dawn in the 2020 animated film Trolls World Tour.

For Disney, Clarkson's song "Breakaway" was contributed in the 2004 film The Princess Diaries 2: Royal Engagement. She also voiced herself in the Phineas and Ferb Christmas special "A Phineas and Ferb Family Christmas" and guest starred in the ABC musical drama series Nashville.

In addition, she was mentioned in the Hannah Montana episode "It's My Party and I'll Lie If I Want to". Her song "Stronger" was covered by Dove Cameron and China Anne McClain in Under the Sea: A Descendants Story.
