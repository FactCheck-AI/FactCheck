When President John Adams was born on 30 October 1735, in Braintree, Suffolk, Massachusetts Bay Colony, British Colonial America, his father, John Adams, was 44 and his mother, Susanna Boylston, was 27. He married Abigail Smith on 25 October 1764, in Weymouth, Suffolk, Massachusetts Bay Colony, British Colonial America. They were the parents of at least 3 sons and 3 daughters. He lived in Braintree, Quincy, Suffolk, Massachusetts, United States in 1790 and Braintree, Quincy, Norfolk, Massachusetts, United States in 1810. He registered for military service in 1797. His occupation is listed as president of the united states of america in Washington, District of Columbia, United States. He died on 4 July 1826, in Quincy, Norfolk, Massachusetts, United States, at the age of 90, and was buried in Hancock Cemetery, Quincy, Norfolk, Massachusetts, United States.
