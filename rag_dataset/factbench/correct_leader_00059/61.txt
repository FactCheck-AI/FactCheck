While in Tallinn, Minister Rau will hold talks with Estonia’s President Alar Karis, Prime Minister Kaja Kallas, and Foreign Minister Eva-Maria Liimets. The main items on the agenda will include bilateral cooperation development and security in the region.

Later during his visit, the head of Polish diplomacy will attend the ceremony to award Siim Kallas with the Commander’s Cross of the Order of Merit of the Republic of Poland. Siim Kallas, former Estonian Prime Minister, Minister of Finance and Foreign Affairs Minister, also held the post of the European Commission’s Vice-President.
