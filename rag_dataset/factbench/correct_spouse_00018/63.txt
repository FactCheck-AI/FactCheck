Not so long ago, reports about deep trouble in the couple’s marriage floated around. And now we hear that they have officially called it quits

Bollywood actor Manisha Koirala got married to businessman Samrat Dahal in 2010. But shortly after tying the knot, we heard murmurs that something wasn't fine in their paradise.

The rift in Koirala’s marriage with Samrat came out in the open when Manisha reportedly him her worst enemy on a social networking site. But later her mom stepped in and clarified that the couple was having a minor tiff.

Turned out the matter was a serious one ‘coz Manisha moved back to Mumbai after announcing at a party that she had decided to get a divorce. What’s more, Manisha was also recently spotted at the special screening of Vidhu Vinod Chopra's Parinda where she revealed that she had signed three Hindi films.

A birdie reveals that the actor, now single and happy, is staying at her very own penthouse in Mumbai. And soon she will be seen in Ram Gopal Varma’s Bhoot 2. All we can say is – all the best for this second innings of your life, dear Manisha!
