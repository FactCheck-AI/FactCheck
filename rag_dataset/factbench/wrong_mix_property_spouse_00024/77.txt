HORROR-RAMA 2022 is thrilled to announce our first guest: actor Thom Mathews! Thom is best known for his signature roles as Freddy in RETURN OF THE LIVING DEAD, (he also starred in the sequel, RETURN OF THE LIVING DEAD PART 2) and Tommy Jarvis in FRIDAY THE 13th PART VI: JASON LIVES (he would also reprise the character in 2017’s FRIDAY THE 13th: THE GAME). Come and meet one of the most beloved ’80s horror stars, only at HORROR-RAMA!
