When Ernest Wadsworth Longfellow was born on 28 November 1845, in Cambridge, Middlesex, Massachusetts, United States, his father, Henry Wadsworth Longfellow, was 38 and his mother, Frances Elizabeth Appleton, was 26. He married Harriet Maria Spelman on 21 May 1868, in Cambridge, Middlesex, Massachusetts, United States. He lived in Manhattan, New York City, New York, United States in 1910 and United States in 1921. He died on 24 November 1921, in Boston, Suffolk, Massachusetts, United States, at the age of 75, and was buried in Mount Auburn Cemetery, Cambridge, Middlesex, Massachusetts, United States.
