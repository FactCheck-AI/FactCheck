When Henry Wadsworth Longfellow Dana was born on 26 January 1881, in Boston, Suffolk, Massachusetts, United States, his father, Richard Henry Dana III, was 30 and his mother, Edith Longfellow, was 27. He immigrated to New York City, New York, United States in 1928. He died on 26 April 1950, in Cambridge, Middlesex, Massachusetts, United States, at the age of 69, and was buried in Mount Auburn Cemetery, Cambridge, Middlesex, Massachusetts, United States.
