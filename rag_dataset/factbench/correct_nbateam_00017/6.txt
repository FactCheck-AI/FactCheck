The New Orleans Hornets were a team in the National Basketball Association. They competed in the Western Conference. They changed their name to the New Orleans Pelicans.

Coaching History[]

2002-2003: Paul Silas

2003-2004: Tim Floyd

2004-2009: Byron Scott

2009-2010: Jeff Bower

2010-2013: Monty Williams
