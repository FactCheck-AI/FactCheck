Publisher’s Blurb

Two great novels and a collection of superb short stones by Britain’s leading science-fiction writer collected into one volume. NON-STOP: Aldiss’s first SF novel which instantly established him as one of the most original authors in the field. . . . ‘fascinating reading up to the end, and when you discover what it is all about. you start again at the beginning’ The Observer. MALE RESPONSE Aldiss explores the forces behind the human sex drive. The setting: the new African republic of Goya; the theme: one man’s response to this delectable and confusing country, where sensuality runs riot. SPACE, TIME AND NATHANIEL Fourteen brilliant short stories that show Aldiss’s astonishing imagination at its very best.
