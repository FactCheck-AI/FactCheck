The Portland Trail Blazers are a team in the National Basketball Association. They compete in the Western Conference. They are best known for their pinwheel logo.

Coaching History[]

1972: Stu Inman

1972-1974: Jack McCloskey

1986-1988: Mike Schuler

1988-1994: Rick Adelman

1994-1997: PJ Carlesimo

1997-2001: Mike Dunleavy, Sr.

2001-2005: Maurice Cheeks

2005: Kevin Pritchard

2005-2012: Nate McMillan

2012: Kaleb Canales

2012-Present: Terry Stotts
