Ernest Hemingway

(1899-1961)

American author and journalist (1899–1961) – Ernest Hemingway was born in Oak Park (Village in Cook County, Illinois, United States; suburb of Chicago, Illinois) on July 21st, 1899 and died in Ketchum (City in Blaine County, Idaho, United States) on July 2nd, 1961 at the age of 61. Today Ernest Hemingway would be 125 years old.
