Anne Marie Perez de Tagle's success as an actress began when she was a mere 16 years old. Perez de Tagle began her career with roles in "Just Jordan" (Nickelodeon, 2006-08) and "Camp Rock" (Disney, 2007-08). Later, Perez de Tagle acted in the Paul Iacono dramatic adaptation "Fame" (2009) and "Camp Rock 2 The Final Jam" (Disney, 2010). More recently, Perez de Tagle acted on "Baby Daddy" (ABC Family, 2011-).
