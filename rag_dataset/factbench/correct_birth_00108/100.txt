When President William Howard Taft was born on 15 September 1857, in Cincinnati, Hamilton, Ohio, United States, his father, Alphonso Taft, was 46 and his mother, Louisa Maria Torrey, was 30. He married Helen "Nellie" Herron on 19 June 1886, in Cincinnati, Hamilton, Ohio, United States. They were the parents of at least 2 sons and 1 daughter. He lived in New Haven, New Haven, Connecticut, United States in 1920. In 1921, his occupation is listed as u.s. supreme court justice in United States. He died on 8 March 1930, in Washington, District of Columbia, United States, at the age of 72, and was buried in Arlington, Virginia, United States.
