Brian Wilson joined Holt Lunsford Commercial in 2024 and serves as Senior Vice President on the Tenant Representation team. He has completed more than $250 Million of real estate transactions throughout the course of his career, representing companies in the private and public sectors.

Prior to joining HLC, Brian was a Managing Director with Whitebox Real Estate in Dallas, where he represented clients with their corporate real estate requirements including lease renewals, relocations, expansions, dispositions, and acquisitions.
