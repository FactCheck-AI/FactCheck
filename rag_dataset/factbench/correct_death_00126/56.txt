When Errol Leslie Thomson Flynn was born on 20 June 1909, in Battery Point, Tasmania, Australia, his father, Dr. Theodore Thomson Flynn, was 25 and his mother, Lily Mary Young, was 21. He married Liliane Marie Madeleine Carre on 29 June 1935. They were the parents of at least 1 son. He lived in Los Angeles, Los Angeles, California, United States in 1950 and Hollywood, Natal, South Africa in 2014. In 1932, his occupation is listed as australian-born actor. He died on 14 October 1959, in Vancouver, British Columbia, Canada, at the age of 50, and was buried in Forest Lawn Memorial Park, Glendale, Los Angeles, California, United States.
