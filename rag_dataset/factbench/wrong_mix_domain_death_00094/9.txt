Grave of King Frederick II of Prussia (1712 - 1786) also known as Frederick the Great (Friedrich der Große) next to the Sanssouci Palace in the Sanssouci Park in Potsdam, Germany. Visitors often place potatoes on his grave in honour of his role in promoting the use of the potato in Prussia.
