San Antonio Spurs great Tony Parker announced today that he and his wife, Axelle, have decided to end their marriage after nine years.

Parker posted a message to Twitter saying the separation will be guided by mutual respect, for the "preservation" of the well-being of their two sons, and there will be no further comments.

Parker and Axelle were married in San Antonio, TX in 2014.

He was previously married to actress Eva Longoria for three years before they finalized their divorce in 2011.
