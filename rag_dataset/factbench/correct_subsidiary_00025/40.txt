Maxis Bhd (Maxis), a subsidiary of Binariang GSM Sdn Bhd, provides telecommunication services and Internet technology services. It provides business broadband, business fiber Internet, business voice enhanced, eSMS, Maxi One Business+ Office 365 suite, mPOS payment service, business telephone services, business voice enhanced and integrated service digital network. It also offers Internet access, business broadband, domestic private leased circuit, cyber security, internet of things, cloud computing, enterprise SMS solutions, machine to machine solutions and calls and voice management. Maxis makes provision for an international gateway network that offers cross border interconnect services and domestic fixed-line networks that offer voice and data services. The company owns, maintains, builds, and operates radio facilities and associated switches. It has operations across Malaysia, and Indonesia. Maxis is headquartered in Kuala Lumpur, Malaysia.
