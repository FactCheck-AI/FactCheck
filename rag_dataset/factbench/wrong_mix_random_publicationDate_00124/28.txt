Comedian and talk show host Chelsea Handler has announced her 17-city stand-up Sit-Down Comedy Tour in support of her new book, “Life Will Be The Death of Me,” which is due out April 9.

The tour will kick off in Boston, Mass., on April 11 and it comes to The Wiltern in Los Angeles on May 30. The show will feature Handler discussing stories from the new book and more. The E! “Chelsea Lately” star has written five books, all of which landed on the New York Times Best Seller List, including “My Horizontal Life: A Collection of One-Night Stands,” “Are You There, Vodka? It’s Me, Chelsea” and “Uganda Be Kidding Me.” Handler has also had a couple of shows on Netflix including her documentary series, “Chelsea Does,” and a late night talk show that ran for two seasons simply titled, “Chelsea.”

Tickets for the Life Will Be the Death of Me: Chelsea Handler’s Sit-Down Comedy Tour go on sale at 10 a.m. Friday, Feb. 8, at LiveNation.com. Every ticket includes a copy of Handler’s new book.
