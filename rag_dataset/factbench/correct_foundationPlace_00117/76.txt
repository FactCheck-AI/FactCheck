HUNTSVILLE, Alabama -- Intergraph's SmartPlant Foundation has been selected to manage engineering information for offshore platforms and oil fields for the China National Offshore Oil Corporation, the largest offshore oil and gas producer in China.

SmartPlant Foundation will be used to develop the oil and gas producer's Engineering Digital Information System project, which will manage the engineering information of 33 offshore platforms for three of CNOOC’s four oil fields, located in Tianjin, Shenzhen and Zhangjiang.

SmartPlant Foundation’s data-centric capabilities and single-source-of-information concept will support all of the company’s requirements, including the management and implementation of engineering changes for enhanced safety, improved quality of data and increased productivity and efficiency.
