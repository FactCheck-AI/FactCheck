When Juliette Albury was born in September 1882, in Key West, Monroe, Florida, United States, her father, Charles E Albury, was 25 and her mother, Caroline Fredic Forsberg, was 23. She married Rufus Wike in 1902, in Key West, Monroe, Florida, United States. She lived in Big Rock Township, Pulaski, Arkansas, United States in 1900. She died in November 1960, in Winter Haven, Polk, Florida, United States, at the age of 78.
