Michael C. Hall is best known for his roles as 'David Fisher' in the HBO drama series Six Feet Under and 'Dexter Morgan' from the Showtime series Dexter. He started out as a stage actor and worked on Broadway long before he moved to TV. He made his Broadway debut in 1999 with Cabaret where he was first discovered by Alan Ball, writer of Six Feet Under.

In 2006, he was cast as the title character in Dexter and has since been nominated for a Golden Globe for Best Actor in a Drama, a TCA Award and several Emmy Awards. Hall was born in Raleigh, North Carolina and was married in 2002 to actress Amy Spanger; they have since divorced.
