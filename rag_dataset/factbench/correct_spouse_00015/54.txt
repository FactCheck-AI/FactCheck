TAMIL movie heart-throb Prasanth married Grihalakshmi, the daughter of industrialist Dhanasekaran, in a glittering ceremony at the Mayor Sri Ramanathan Chettiar Centre in Chennai on Thursday morning.

Malaysian Works Minister Datuk Seri S. Samy Vellu was among the prominent personalities, including industrialists and Tamil film celebrities, who attended the ceremony, Tamil Nesan reported yesterday.
