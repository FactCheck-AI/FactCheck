Tun Abdul Razak (born 11 March 1922) is a potential leader of Malaya, in the event of a UMAJF victory during the Malayan Emergency.

In-Game role

After the war, elections will be held in Malaya and either Ahmad Boestamam or Tun Abdul Razak becomes leader. It is possible for Tun Abdul Razak to win the election fradulently with the help of the CIA, via in-game CIA decisions available for the United States to influence the elections in favour of him.

Trivia

Tun Abdul Razak was the Second Prime Minister of Malaysia from 1970 until his death in 1976, succeeded Tunku Abdul Rahman after resigning in the aftermath of the 13 May Incident.
