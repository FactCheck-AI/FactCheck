William Fuller Borland

October 16, 1918 ~ October 12, 2009

PEBBLE BEACH â€“ William Borland, 90, has been a resident since 1972. He was born in Chicago and lived for many years in Lake Forest, Illinois.

Bill Borland served the community for over 30 years. During this time he was a Trustee of the Community Hospital of the Monterey Peninsula from 1974-1980 and 1983-1992. He was Chairman from 1976-1979. He also served as Chairman of the Monterey Peninsula Foundation. He was a well-known, popular, and respected member of the community.

He is survived by his children, Leslie vanBuren, Anne Borland, William F. Borland, Jr., and Dorothy Kitt and the children and stepchildren of Nancy Morse Borland, Michael G. Hooker, Rodman L. Hooker, Anne Walker and stepdaughter Martha Gibbs.

A memorial service will be held on Friday, October 16, 2009 at 2:00pm at the Church in the Forest at the Robert Louis Stevenson School in Pebble Beach.
