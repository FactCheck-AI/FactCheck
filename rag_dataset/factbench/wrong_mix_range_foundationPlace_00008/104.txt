SAP SE (SAP) is a provider of enterprise application software and related services. It provides a wide range of solutions, including enterprise resource planning and financial management, intelligent technologies, analytics, people engagement, human resource, digital supply chain, network, spend management, customer relationship management, and customer experience. The company offers a range of business software and enterprise applications that are designed for applications, analytics, cloud, and database and technology operations. It serves clients in the areas of consumer products, oil, gas and energy, financial services, aerospace and defense, transportation, and logistics, chemicals, government, healthcare, higher education and research, media, real estate, retail, and telecommunications. The company has business presence in Europe, the Middle East, Africa, the Americas, and Asia-Pacific. SAP is headquartered in Walldorf, Baden-Wurttemberg, Germany.
