Arthur Ashe Stadium is a tennis stadium in Queens, New York, which is part of the USTA Billie Jean King National Tennis Center. It is the main stadium of the US Open tennis tournament, and the largest tennis stadium in the world, with a capacity of over 23,000. Tours are also available, take advantage of a once in a lifetime peek inside exclusive players' areas and experience the world of pro tennis through the eyes of the athletes and sports journalists.
