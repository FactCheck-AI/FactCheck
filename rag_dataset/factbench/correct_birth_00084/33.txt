What is Jelena Jankovic's Net Worth?

Jelena Jankovic is a Serbian professional tennis player who has a net worth of $15 million. Born on 28 February 1985, in Belgrade, SFRY, Jankovic is a former World No. 1 tennis player in singles, a prestigious title that she acquired in the months following her finals appearance at the 2008 US Open. First introduced with the sport as a nine-and-a-half year old and initially coached by her older brother Marko, Jankovic mastered her skills at the Tennis Academy of Nick Bollettieri. In her early career, while she was still a junior, Jankovic achieved her first win at the 2001 Australian Open reaching the junior #1 position.
