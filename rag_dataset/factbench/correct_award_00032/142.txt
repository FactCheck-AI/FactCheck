The 2005 Nobel Peace Prize was jointly awarded today to the United Nations International Atomic Energy Agency and its director, Mohamed ElBaradei. The award citation refers to ElBaradei as a "fearless advocate" of promoting safe nuclear energy and curbing nuclear arms.
