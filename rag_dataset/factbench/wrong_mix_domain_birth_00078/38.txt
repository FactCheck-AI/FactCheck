When Edith Jean Roddick was born on 7 April 1885, in Montreal, Quebec, Canada, her father, Andrew Roddick, was 23 and her mother, Elizabeth Lizzie Ann Hannaford, was 25. She married Hugh McGill in 1913, in Verdun, Montreal, Quebec, Canada. They were the parents of at least 1 son and 1 daughter. She lived in Jacques-Cartier, Quebec, Canada in 1911 and Saint Boniface, Winnipeg, Manitoba, Canada in 1926. She died on 17 March 1974, in Winnipeg, Manitoba, Canada, at the age of 88, and was buried in Winnipeg, Winnipeg, Manitoba, Canada.
