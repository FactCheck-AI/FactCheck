When Huntly Roddick Redpath was born in 1911, in Pincher Creek, Pincher Creek No. 9, Alberta, Canada, his father, John Reginald Redpath, was 40 and his mother, Florence Amelia Botterell, was 39. He had at least 1 son with Margaret Edna Heal. He lived in Fort Macleod, Willow Creek No. 26, Alberta, Canada in 1916. He died on 21 December 1972, in Montreal, Montreal Urban Community, Quebec, Canada, at the age of 61, and was buried in Outremont, Montreal, Montreal Urban Community, Quebec, Canada.
