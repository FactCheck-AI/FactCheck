Israeli Prime Minister Yitzhak Shamir delivers a passionate speech to Russian and Ethiopian immigrant at a ceremony marking the arrival of the 400,000th immigrant on March 24, 1992 in his office in Jerusalem. Shamir said that Jews must prepare a country to absorb the immigration and the such country cannot be a palestinian state, but must be the land of Israel. AP Photo/Jacqueline Arzt)
