Jun 26, 2003 - The Memphis Grizzlies selected Kendrick Perkins in Round 1 with Pick 27 in the 2003 NBA Draft. Jun 26, 2003 - The draft rights to Kendrick Perkins was acquired by the Boston Celtics, along with the draft rights to Marcus Banks, from the Memphis Grizzlies in exchange for the draft rights to Troy Bell and the draft rights to Dahntay Jones. Jul 9, 2003 - Kendrick Perkins signed a multi-year contract with the Boston Celtics. Oct 29, 2005 - The Boston Celtics exercised their Team Option to extend the contract of Kendrick Perkins. Sep 11, 2006 - Kendrick Perkins signed a rookie scale extension with the Boston Celtics. Feb 24, 2011 - Kendrick Perkins was acquired by the Oklahoma City Thunder, along with Nate Robinson, from the Boston Celtics in exchange for Jeff Green, Nenad Krstic, a Least Favorable 2012 1st round pick, a conditional 2013 1st round pick (LAC own) and a 2013 2nd round pick (CHA own). Feb 28, 2011 - Kendrick Perkins renegotiated his contract with the Oklahoma City Thunder. Feb 28, 2011 - Kendrick Perkins signed a veteran extension with the Oklahoma City Thunder. Feb 19, 2015 - Kendrick Perkins was acquired by the Utah Jazz, along with Grant Jerrett, the draft rights to Tibor Pleiss, a 2017 2nd round pick (DET own) and a conditional 2018 1st round pick (OKC own), from the Oklahoma City Thunder in exchange for Enes Freedom and Steve Novak. Feb 21, 2015 - The Utah Jazz placed the contract of Kendrick Perkins on waivers. Feb 23, 2015 - Kendrick Perkins, previously with the Utah Jazz, became a free agent. Feb 24, 2015 - Kendrick Perkins signed a contract with the Cleveland Cavaliers. Jul 1, 2015 - Kendrick Perkins, previously with the Cleveland Cavaliers, became a free agent. Jul 27, 2015 - Kendrick Perkins signed a contract with the New Orleans Pelicans. Jul 1, 2016 - Kendrick Perkins, previously with the New Orleans Pelicans, became a free agent. Jul 20, 2016 - The New Orleans Pelicans renounced their free-agent exception rights to Kendrick Perkins. Sep 25, 2017 - Kendrick Perkins signed a contract with the Cleveland Cavaliers. Oct 14, 2017 - The Cleveland Cavaliers placed the contract of Kendrick Perkins on waivers. Oct 16, 2017 - Kendrick Perkins, previously with the Cleveland Cavaliers, became a free agent. Apr 11, 2018 - Kendrick Perkins signed a multi-year contract with the Cleveland Cavaliers. Jun 27, 2018 - The Cleveland Cavaliers exercised their Team Option to extend the contract of Kendrick Perkins. Jul 17, 2018 - The Cleveland Cavaliers placed the contract of Kendrick Perkins on waivers.
