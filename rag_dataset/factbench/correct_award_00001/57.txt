Yes, the Nobel Peace Prize has been awarded every year since 1901. Nobel Prizes were not awarded during the period of World War I (1914-1918) and World War II (1939-1945). The Nobel Peace Prize was awarded to two scientists Jean Henry Dunant and Frédéric Passy. The Nobel Prize is awarded in memory of Alfred Nobel, Swedish inventor and entrepreneur. The Nobel Prize is awarded to the person who has done the greatest benefit to humankind.
