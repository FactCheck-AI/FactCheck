The Hague ranks 101st in number of biographies on Pantheon, behind Leipzig, Chiba Prefecture, and Antwerp. Memorable people born in The Hague include Christiaan Huygens, Gustav Krupp von Bohlen und Halbach, and William II, Prince of Orange. Memorable people who died in The Hague include Baruch Spinoza, Christiaan Huygens, and Slobodan Milošević. The Hague has been the birth place of many politicians, and soccer players and the death place of many politicians, and painters. The Hague is located in Netherlands.
