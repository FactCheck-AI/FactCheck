Biography

Kelly Clarkson is an American singer, songwriter, author, and television personality.

Kelly Brianne Clarkson was born on 24 April 1982 at Fort Worth, Texas, USA.

In 2002 she rose to fame after winning the first season of the tv show "American Idol", and has since been established as "The Original American Idol". Her debut single, "A Moment Like This", topped the US Billboard Hot 100 and broke the record for the biggest jump to number one in the chart's history; it became the best-selling single of the year in the country.

In 2003 there was one-off performance show, "World Idol" where she was the runner-up.

Kelly Clarkson married about 2013. They had 2 children and divorced in 2022.

Her former father-in-law has also been her manager, who at one time was married to Reba McEntire, who is also a notable singer,
