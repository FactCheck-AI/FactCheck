Tallinn, Stenbock House7 April 2003Prime Minister Siim Kallas met today with Stjepan Mesic, President of the Republic of Croatia. At the meeting, Kallas confirmed that Estonia supports the aspirations of Croatia towards membership in NATO and the European Union, and is ready to share its experience with Croatia. The international situation and more closely the possibilities for developing tourism were also discussed at the meeting. Both Kallas and Mesic expressed hope that bilateral tourism will become more active in the near future.As of 1 April 2003, Estonia is also represented in Croatia through its ambassador Toivo Tasa.Government Press Office
