At stake in this election:

The office of President of Croatia

Description of government structure:

Chief of State: President Stjepan MESIC

Head of Government: Prime Minister Ivo SANADER

Assembly: Croatia has a unicameral assembly (Sabor) with 152 seats.

Description of electoral system:

The President is elected by popular vote to serve a 5-year term.

In the assembly (Sabor) 152 members are elected by popular vote to serve 4-year terms.
