Jamie Lynn Spears Splits With Casey Aldridge

Britney's little sis, Jamie Lynn Spears, has reportedly split from boyfriend and baby daddy Casey Aldridge, according to US Magazine.

The cause of the breakup was not initially reported. However, a source for Star is now claiming that the former 'Zoey 101' star has dumped Aldridge for 28-year-old Louisiana business man James Watson, who lives relatively close to the Spears family estate in Kentwood.
