Sunnyvale, CA, USA

Latitude and longitude coordinates are: 37.368832, -122.036346.

Sunnyvale is a city in Santa Clara County, California, USA. It is one of the largest cities in the Bay Area, and a technology and innovation hub. The city is bordered by San Jose, Mountain View, Los Altos, Cupertino, and Santa Clara. Since it is located in Silicon Valley, the city is home to the headquarters of several technology companies. San Jose International Airport (SJC) is the closest airport to the city and located 9.5 miles from downtown Sunnyvale. Attractions in the city include Las Palmas Park, Ortega Park, Seven Seas Park, Baylands Park, The Golf Club at Moffett Field, Twin Creeks, Hindu Temple & Community Center, Bay Trail Sunnyvale, Shirdi Sai Darbar | Bay Area Hindu Temple, Sunnyvale Community Center, and Heritage Park.
