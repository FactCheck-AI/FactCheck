Full Name: Christian Matthias Theodor Mommsen

Profession: Historian

Biography: Regarded as one of the greatest classicists and historians of the 19th century, Mommsen was awarded the 1902 Nobel Prize in Literature with a citation that said he was "the greatest living master of the art of historical writing, with special reference to his monumental work A History of Rome".

His work on Rome is still extremely important for contemporary historical research, and his works on Roman law and on the law of obligations had a significant impact on the German civil code (BGB).

Born: November 30, 1817

Birthplace: Garding, Duchy of Schleswig

Star Sign: Sagittarius
