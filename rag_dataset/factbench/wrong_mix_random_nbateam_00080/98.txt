Susannah Harker (born 26 april 1965) is a British actress who played Irene St. Claire in 1991 in TV movie The Crucifer of Blood with Charlton Heston as Sherlock Holmes. She also played Adelaide Savage in 1994 in TV episode The Dying Detective of the TV series Sherlock Holmes with Jeremy Brett (Sherlock Holmes).

TV
