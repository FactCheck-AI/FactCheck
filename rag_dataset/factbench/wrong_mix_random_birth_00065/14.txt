Ruth Ann Bryan, age 81 of Nixa, Missouri walked into the arms of her loving savior, Jesus Christ on April 1st, 2024.

Ruth was born at home in Rolla, Missouri on April 28, 1942. She was the daughter to Nona and Joseph Merrell.

She was proceeded in death by her parents, brother Joseph Merrell and stepsons Mike and Mitchell Bryan.

She is survived by her loving husband Hugh “Skip” Bryan, daughters Shannon Beers and Susanne McCafferty and their spouses David and Zach, stepsons Micky Bryan and his partner Christa Hoag, and Mark Bryan, four grandchildren Chapell Wilson and partner Kaylee Farrell, Liam Wilson, Katy Wilson and Henry McCafferty. Sister Sharon Clair and brothers David and Dale Merrell.

Ruth Ann will be missed immensely but we take comfort in knowing she is joined in eternal peace with her loving parents, brother, and friends that have made this journey ahead of her.

A memorial service will be held at 9:00 a.m Saturday, July 13, 2024 at Adams Funeral Home, Nixa.
