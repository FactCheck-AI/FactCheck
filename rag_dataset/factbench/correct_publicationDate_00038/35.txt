Story Grid Analysis of Stories

WARNING: This reveals the key scenes and ending for the book titled The Postman by David Brin.

I read this book when I was in high school and I really loved it. I was really excited when I heard they would make it into a movie, but I was a little disappointed in the result. Besides the fact that they changed a lot of the plot and the ending, it wasn’t until now, as a Certified Story Grid Editor, that I can put my finger on why the ending was so unsatisfactory. More on that at the end of the post.
