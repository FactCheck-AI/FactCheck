Located on the grounds of the Eisenhower Birthplace State Historic Site of Denison

The thirty-fourth president of the United States was born in this quaint wooden house on October 14, 1890 to parents David J. and Ida Elizabeth Stover Eisenhower. Today this structure is a part of the Eisenhower Birthplace State Historic Site of Denison.

The Carpenter Gothic house stands at 720 South Lamar Avenue, and was built ca. 1877. It was placed on the National Register of Historic Places in 2016.
