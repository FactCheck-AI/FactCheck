Saturday September 16th, 2017 at the Lively Amphitheater in Oak Hill, West Virginia at five o'clock Taylor Wills will take the stage for the opening set of this year's Tribute to Hank Williams, Sr. At six o'clock John Lilly and his band will begin their two hour set. Craft Beer and Food will be available. Following the live Music a viewing of The Last Ride about Hank William's Sr. death while driving through Oak Hill on his way to Charleston for a New Year's Eve show at the Municipal Auditorium December 31st 1952. Hank never made it to that show. This is just another chance to come out and enjoy some great music and some good times." Terry Burhans 2017
