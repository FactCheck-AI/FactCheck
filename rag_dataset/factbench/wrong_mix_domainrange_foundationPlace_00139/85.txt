Kaspersky Lab B.V. has signed a lease with HSA B.V. for approximately 470 sq. m. of office space and 12 parking spaces in the “Rijnveste” office building at Rijnzathe 8 in De Meern, Utrecht.

Salvatore Stasio, Manager of Finance at Kaspersky: “We have chosen Rijnveste because this was the building with the best price-quality ratio. A well-maintained property in a great location, with a cafeteria, reception services and excellent parking. This location in De Meern allows us to stay in the Utrecht region at a highly favourable price.”
