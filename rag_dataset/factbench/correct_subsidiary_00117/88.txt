The Polycom SpectraLink 8030 CBD300 Dual Charger Bundle (2200-37167-001) is a wireless VoIP phone. The SpectraLink 8030 features push to talk, a 2 way method of communicating similar to radios. Push to talk bypasses the PBX and eliminates the need for an additional radio when your team is communicating.

The Polycom Spectra Link 8030 wireless VoIP phones sold by Dotcom Computers also include the dual charger bundle (CBD300). The dual charger bundle enables users to have more than one docking and charging station for the phone, allowing it to be carried and transported frequently.
