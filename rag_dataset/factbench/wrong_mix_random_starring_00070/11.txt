Synopsis[]

In Characterville 2022, Almost 75 years after the events of the first film along with the murder of Marvin Acme in 1947, Maroon Cartoon Studio (now Maroon Entertainment Studio) is now run by Eddie Valiant and his son Daniel. When Judge Doom is mysteriously revived along with a new boss except in fact it is the cousin of the son of Judge Doom united with Various Villains, and the army of Toon Patrol, who created the Superweapon, So Roger Rabbit and all the other toons must team up with the Valiants to stop them from taking over Characterville along with the second coming of the Dip.

Plot[]

It had been 75 years since the first time Eddie Valiant met Roger Rabbit and entered Toontown, the home of all animated cartoon characters (aka Toons). Things had drastically changed since then. Toontown had become more urbanized in 1986 and is now known as Characterville, while Maroon Cartoon Studio, now called Maroon Entertainment Studio, is now under the control of Eddie and his son Daniel.

But just as everything seems to be going smoothly, a new threat arises. The sons of Judge Doom, the infamous villain who had once attempted to destroy Toontown, had united with the weasel, alien, rabbid & monster army of the Toon Patrol. Villain Doom, Lucius DeGeraldson, and Wacky Doom Jr. are determined to finish what their late father had started – the destruction of all toons.

Using their knowledge of their father's diabolical plans, the Doom brothers create the Superweapon, a more advanced version of Judge Doom’s Dip machine. With this weapon in their possession, they plan to wipe out all toons and turn Characterville into a desolate wasteland.

As news of their plans spread, panic and fear engulf Characterville. Roger Rabbit, the beloved toon who had helped Eddie defeat Judge Doom all those years ago, was determined to not let history repeat itself. Roger seeks the help of all of the toons to stop the Doom brothers and their army of weasels, aliens, rabbids & monsters, But it would not be an easy task. The Doom brothers had planned everything about their attack carefully, and they will not tolerate criticism.

Roger knows he cannot do it alone. He needs the help of the Valiants, the only humans he trusts. Eddie, Daniel are reluctant to join the toons because they had put their past behind them and were content with their lives in Characterville. But when they see the devastation caused by the Doom brothers and their Superweapon, they know they have to act.

With the Valiants on their side, the toons manage to infiltrate the Doom brothers' lair and steal the Superweapon on the night of the planned attack, But not without a fight. The Doom brothers and their enhanced Toon Patrol 2 put up a tough battle, but in the end, the toons emerge victorious and destroy the Superweapon.

As the toons celebrate their victory, a feeling of nostalgia sweeps over Roger. He remembers the adventure he had with Eddie all those years ago and thanks the Valiants for coming to their rescue once again. With the Superweapon destroyed and the toons safe, Characterville goes back to being the happy place it once was.

And as for the Valiants, they have once again proven that no matter how much time had passed, they would always be there to protect the toons. Characterville may have changed, but the bond between humans and toons remained unbreakable.
