Descriptions

Founded in 1991, AVG Technologies provides security software products to home and business computer users. The company distributes its family of security software products globally through resellers and the web. Its products support all major operating systems and platforms. The company has more than 110 million active users around the world. It is also the world's fourth largest vendor of anti-virus software measured by installed user base. AVG Technologies has partners such as Cisco, VeriSign and Microsoft. The company also provides state-of-the art technologies, such as behavioural detection and AVG LinkScanner to ensure class protection. AVG Technologies employs some of the world s leading experts in software development, risk analysis, and threat detection and prevention.
