Overview

J. Robert Lennon is the author of three story collections, "Pieces For The Left Hand," "See You in Paradise," and "Let Me Think," and ten novels, including "Hard Girls," "Broken River," and "Subdivision." His short fiction has appeared in "The New Yorker," "Harper's," "Playboy," "Granta," "The Paris Review," and elsewhere, and his criticism in "The New York Times Book Review," "The Guardian," "The Globe and Mail," "The Nation," and "The London Review of Books." He is the co-editor of "Critical Hits," an anthology of nonfiction writing about video games.
