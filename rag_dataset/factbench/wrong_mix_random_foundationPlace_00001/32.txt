The Case Western Reserve University community is invited to attend Thursday’s Ford Distinguished Lecture featuring Karl Deisseroth, the D.H. Chen Professor of Bioengineering and Psychiatry at Stanford University.

Deisseroth will present his lecture, “Optical Deconstruction of Fully Assembled Biological Systems,” at 4:30 p.m. on Thursday, Oct. 17, in the Wolstein Research Building auditorium.

This free public event is part of the Allen and Constance Ford Distinguished Lecture Series in partnership with the Department of Biomedical Engineering.
